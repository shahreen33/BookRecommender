Junie B. is having a run of bad luck, losing every game at the schoolcarnival. When she finally wins the Cake Walk on her last ticket, she choosesa fruitcake as her prize. But who in the heck knew that sparkly foil-wrappedfruitcake was really a lethal weapon in disguise? "The innocent first-personnarrative will make lower-school readers laugh out loud at hermispronunciations as they recognize their own confusion andfailure."-- "Booklist"
