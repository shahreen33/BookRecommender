Spinning out of the pages of BATMAN! The teenagers of Gotham City have adopted the "R" and made it their own. A new Robin? No, hundreds of new Robins! The Boy Wonder has inspired a movement in Gotham, where different people of all creeds, colors, genders and religions have donned the "R" insignia to combat crime. But will this clean up the city...or make more trouble than it's worth? Don't miss the start of this new series from rising star writer Lee Bermejo (JOKER, SUICIDERS). Collects WE ARE ROBIN #1-6.
