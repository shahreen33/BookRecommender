There is a first for everything…Zan always knew the role for which he was created – to guide, encourage, and protect.  He spent every moment of his angelic existence preparing for his earthly assignment.  What he didn’t expect were the intense emotions – love, loss, heartache – that came along with it.  More specifically, the feelings tied to one of his “callers,” a girl named Providence.Will Providence’s love drive him to choose the unthinkable?
