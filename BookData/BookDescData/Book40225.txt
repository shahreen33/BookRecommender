
  «After all, weren't we... just friend?»
Romance is never easy. Missed signals, passive aggression, and ego all threaten to get in the way of any budding relationship, and it's no different for Ally and Lisa. Best friends. Roommates. BFSB practitioners. Still, Ally and Lisa's romance is built on more fantasies than one—including the infamous lie of "friends with benefits". When a figure from Lisa's erotic tales finds real life representations in new friend Anne, are a few missed signals more stress than this fantasy romance can stand?From critically acclaimed writer/artist Stjepan Šejić (Switch, Death Vigil), comes this fourth installment in the New York Times bestselling saga of love, bad jokes, and, of course, bondage.
