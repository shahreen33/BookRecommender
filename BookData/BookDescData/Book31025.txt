Dystopia: Revelations  A Character Novel   Rita is plunged into a river, battered and drowning. Her son in the grips of the very madwoman who did this to her and no memory of her past. How can she survive?   Stumbling into a refugee camp thinking she is saved, her supposed saviors only seek the information locked in a memory forgotten. Stronger than she thought she could be, determined to reach her son, the secrets of the Reset are revealed.  Those who thought her long dead, find revelation in her emergence and betrayal in their midst.
