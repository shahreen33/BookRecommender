Where's baby's nose? There it is! Baby Touch Your Nose is an adorable board book with crystal-clear photos, read-aloud text, and an extra-special twinkle on every page. The glittery tactile finger trails encourage pre-writing and fine motor skills, and the sturdy book pages are perfect for small baby hands.Baby Touch Your Nose engages young babies in early learning skills with singsong rhymes and bright, colorful pages.
