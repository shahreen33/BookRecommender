Confrontation With Destiny Their mission to the lost city of Ka a resounding success, Rikardon and Tarani begin a long trek across Gandalara. Their journey leads them to a savage conflict with the ape-like vineh, a bittersweet reunion n Raithskar, and, at last, to their goal: Eddarta, seat of government. There, Tarani must call upon more strength and determinatin than she has ever summoned in her life to face off against the treachery of the wicked High Lord Indomel for her rightful place on Eddarta's throne.
