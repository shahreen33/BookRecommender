It is in the moments of silence, that we can be taken to our own universe, where our real essence longs to be part of. Through the pages of this thought provoking book you are encouraged to embark upon the most wonderful of quests and that is to find your reason for being on this earth, to remember who you are. By encouraging ones heart to speak, it gives you an eternal door through which you can walk through, so you are able to find the true purpose of your existence.By healing our past & our present ultimately gives us hope for the future of our dreams and aspirations. Once we learn the beautiful language of love, we may understand the language of everyone's heart and thus find the key to Eternity.
