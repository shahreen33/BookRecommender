From coast to coast, Canada is a spooky place. Chains rattle and footsteps shuffle in empty alleys in Victoria, British Columbia. Poltergeists terrorize hunters in a remote cabin in Red Deer Point, Manitoba. The mournful wail of a woman haunts Signal Hill, Newfoundland and Labrador. With over 100 true ghost stories from across the country prepare yourself to be haunted!This collection includes the first three books in the award-winning Haunted Canada series: Haunted Canada True Ghost Stories, Haunted Canada 2 True Tales of Terror and Haunted Canada 3 More True Ghost Stories
