Set in the futuristic world of Rapture, where fairy tales have always existed and are at war with humans. When Fyra Grimm is attacked in her home by a dragon, she discovers a frightening ability within her that can create, but also destroy.Exiled from the Human compound, Fyra is sent into the unforgiving landscape that is the Dark Seas. Fyra is then kidnapped by brothers Echo the Wolf and Fuse the Huntsman, and brought to the Snow Queen, who believes she is the key to waging war on Humans and Fairy Tale creatures alike.As Fyra is thrown deeper into a world she does not understand, anger and fear will unleash a power so dark and destructive that Rapture will fall to its knees.
