The sequel to the New York Times best-selling original graphic novel is here with Teen Titans: Earth One, Vol. 2!Picking right up after the cliffhanger ending to the first volume, Cyborg, Beast Boy, Terra and Aqualad are in desperate straits: they're homeless, destitute, and on the run from Cadmus, the project that created them. Unfortunately, Cadmus has more surprises up its sleeve... and a whole new set of teenage weapons, with none of the bothersome morals of the originals!From writer Jeff Lemire (Sweet Tooth, Animal Man) and illustrator Andy McDonald (The New 52: Futures' End) comes the highly anticipated sequel to the critically acclaimed original graphic novel in Teen Titans: Earth One Vol. 2!
