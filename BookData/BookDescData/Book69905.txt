Leer en espanol is a series of Spanish-language graded literature that includes action thrillers, mysteries, fantasy tales, and adaptations of literary masterpieces from Latin America and Spain. These carefully controlled texts provide high-quality, comprehensible reading for intermediate-level students. Each book provides solid support to help the reader understand the text, including a glossary of key vocabulary and comprehension questions.Several characters show a unique way of dealing with reality in Uruguay. Then a surprise brings them an unexpected truth.
