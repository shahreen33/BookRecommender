When millionaire Justin Wayne finds himself in need of help, he figured his girlfriend would step up and be there for him; instead he must turn to his maid for it. Dava Flores is a single mother, studying for her degree, just trying to make a life for her and her son, but when she sees Justin in trouble she doesn’t even hesitate to lend him a hand.Justin isn’t used to someone helping him out without expecting something in return, even his gold digging girlfriend. Will Dava be able to help Justin break the chains his girlfriend has on him, or will Justin give into her demands to fire Dava, and give her more money?
