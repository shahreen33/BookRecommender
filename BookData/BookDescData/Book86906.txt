 Talk about one hell of a ride ...   Robin is an arms smuggler with a bit of magic under her belt. When her secretary asks for a tank, she sets out to steal one and get paid. But how on earth can she get away clean when a jungle army patrol, band of rebels, and literal demons from hell all stand in her way?  Find out in Hell on Wheels - a thrilling prelude to the thrilling Brimstone Hustle urban fantasy series!
