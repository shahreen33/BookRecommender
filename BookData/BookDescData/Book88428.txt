A Post Apocalyptic Skirmish Wargame: Three generations on from The Last War, pockets of humanity roam the ghost towns and ruins of civilisation, looting and fighting for profit, glory and their very lives Across the Dead Earth.Assemble your Gang of deadly warriors and embark on campaigns to upgrade your characters and their equipment from game to game, building a persistent and truly personalised Gang, honed to your style of play, destroying your enemies as you go and carving out your survival Across the Dead Earth.
