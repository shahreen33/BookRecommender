With the unemployment rate at its highest level in six years and many companies looking to tighten their belts, it has become even more critical that job seekers find an edge in an increasingly competitive market. Praised by the Financial Times and the Wall Street Journal as one of the best career books on the market, Knock 'em Dead gets even better in 2003. The newest edition of this New York Times bestseller features the most up-to-date advice available on recent developments in the job market, featuring invaluable advice on getting organized, landing interviews, and staying motivated during the often grueling job search process. Knock 'em Dead, 2003 also features the latest information on selecting a job-hunting coach, online research and networking, and -- as always -- savvy answers to hundreds of tough interview questions.
