Shi-Hyun's new "friend," Hee-Soo, is secretly plotting revenge against her in the name of family. And Shi-Hyun is walking right into her trap...But that's only the beginning! Still ahead are confessions of love, acts of jealousy, and an unforgettable fit of rage. Will Shi-Hyun come to terms with her feelings for her stepbrother? Will Ma-Hyun convince her to date him instead? If hatred doesn't break Shi-Hyun's spirits, perhaps love will!
