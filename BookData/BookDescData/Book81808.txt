When you go sticking your nose in places it doesn’t belong, all Hell can break loose. Nadia finds out the hard way when she decides to snoop through her best friend’s books on Wicca. Now, the priest, who claims to be Satan, is after her. He wants to kill her in order to raise his wife from the dead. Nadia makes a deal with the real dark lord in exchange for all her wishes to be granted. What she never expected was to find her future husband in a demon. Her very own guardian demon. But that demon doesn’t always come to her rescue. Nadia is in a fight for her life. She is up against evil forces she didn’t even know existed - one worse than the next - and she is scared. Will Nadia survive the night? Will she find her way in a world of pure evil?
