From the motion picture About a Boy comes this matching folio to accompany Damon Gough's album of music that so perfectly set the tone for the movie. Titles include: Exit Stage Right * A Peak You Reach * Something to Talk About * Dead Duck * Above You, Below Me * I Love N.Y.E. * Silent Sigh * Wet, Wet, Wet * River-Sea-Ocean * S.P.A.T. * Rachel's Flat * Walking Out of Stride and more.
