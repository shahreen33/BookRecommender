She's a struggling single mom of two...He's a sexy but jaded single dad of one. —Clara—All I've ever wanted was my Prince Charming. Life gave me a monster instead. Thankfully, my sons and I escaped his clutches.Now we're safe. Happy. Secure.My friends beg me to give love a second chance. I only have one thing to say to that... Not in this lifetime. As far as I'm concerned, men don't exist. But when Brantley and his daughter burst into our lives, everything changes.I try to fight my feelings, but it's pointless. I'm already falling in love with both of them. Problem is, my past still has an iron-clad hold on me. It doesn't matter though. My demons don't stand a chance when Brantley decides to fight for me.Warning: Contains triggers.
