Nominated for BEST COMIC/GRAPHIC NOVEL at the 2014 Geekie Awards and the recipient of an HONORABLE MENTION for best Comics/Graphic Novel at the 2013 Geekie Awards, Boston Metaphysical Society was also nominated for Best Webcomic at the 2013 and 2014 Steampunk Chronicles Reader's Choice Awards and a 2012 Airship Award (SteamCon).The first chapter in a six-issue graphic novel mini-series, BOSTON METAPHYSICAL SOCIETY is about an ex-Pinkerton detective and his medium/spirit photographer partner who battle supernatural forces in late 1800's Boston.This special edition is soft-bound with a hard spine and a quality laminated cover.
