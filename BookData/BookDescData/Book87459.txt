Russian ex-wrestler Roofy has survived, only to find so has the demon, Apocalypse. Hunted by the blood-dealer, Ambrose, and lured to Richmond by news that Constance may be alive as well, he is reunited with Detective Laura Stenks, fighting her own inner demons and also tracking the seemingly reborn teen.  In the midst of a city gripped by a deadly new serial killer, the four will be brought back together and, each driven by their own desires and frailties, will find out who amongst them is the true evil.
