She’s smart, she’s sassy and she’s sexy as hell - meet Tawny, aka “The Happy Spinster,” an auburn haired siren whose seductive style has set many a male heart racing in old Montreal. While at her local one Friday night, she meets Troy, a Texas cowboy whose smoldering good looks immediately ignite Tawny’s sexual craving. With a mutual attraction between them and a burning carnal desire within them, Tawny and Troy embark on an ardor filled night of sensational sex and passionate lovemaking but – will Tawny break the rules and fall in love with the handsome bronco buster? And is there a future for a woman from the city and a ranch hand from the Texas panhandle?
