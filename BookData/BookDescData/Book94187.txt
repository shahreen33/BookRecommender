A collection of more than 650 relevant quotations from well-known authors, leaders of industry, and teachers of management, including Bennis, Burns, Drucker, Galbreath, Iococca, Kanter, Maslow, and many others. Arranged within a powerful framework, the book's structure illustrates the view that effective leaders are, first, fully functioning people. Contents: The Fully Functioning Person, Reasoning, Coping, Knowing, Believing, Being
