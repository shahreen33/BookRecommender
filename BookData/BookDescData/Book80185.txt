What’s more dangerous than dark magic?  Deadly secrets.  And The Academy of Six is hiding both within its ancient walls.  A History of Magic sends Izara on a literal trip down memory lane. She uncovers that there’s more than what the students think they know about the supernatural school in the middle of New York City. It’s mysterious past, as well as her own, are twisting together in a destructive way.  She always knew the Academy was created to stop a reckless power.  And she’s just now realizing that if her and her friends can’t contain the monster within her, history is bound to repeat itself.  This is Reverse Harem series containing M/M themes.
