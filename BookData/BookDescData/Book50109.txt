When Jess meets the Devil, and he offers to give her the sexual experience of a lifetime, she can't think of a single reason to resist temptation. Good girls might be good, but bad girls have all the fun. The Devil promises to make her burn in Hell...and love every minute of it. Jess goes on a journey to explore the limits of her sensuality. No matter how far it takes her she has a ready made excuse the Devil made her do it!"
