I am Dead, to begin with. Isn't that how all the best ghost stories start?Lucas has made his decision but certain reservations hold him back. The Cailleach, one of the faces of Lord Death, offers him the opportunity to lay his misgivings to rest. Accepting the offer, Lucas little realises the war he will have to fight in his own heart.Death is only the beginning in this short story set during and after book four of the Unveiled Series.
