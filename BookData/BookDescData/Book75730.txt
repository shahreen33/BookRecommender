A LONG, LONG TIME AGO...The planet Jakku is littered with countless rebel and Imperial shipwrecks. But one vessel has long eluded discovery. When the Imperial Star Destroyer Spectral reveals itself from its sandy grave, untouched after decades buried, renowned scavenger Rey leads the race to claim whatever supplies might remain within. Perils abound as Rey descends inside the "Ghost Ship" and learns just what (and WHO) caused the Spectral to crash headlong onto Jakku!
