When a mongoose and a viper are natural enemies, can true love ever come to be? Shima's hidden relationship with Hiromasa may be revealed when suddenly there's a baby on the way... And our Missing Link Norio has his own hands full when he's forced to undergo intense Zooman social etiquette training--at Kunimasa's house! The strict training and even stranger family members should be the least of his worries, however, when he finds a side to Kunimasa that may destroy their relationship...
