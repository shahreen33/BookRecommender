General Andrew Biman doesn’t know failure, he doesn’t understand subtlety, and he’s wholly unacquainted with humility. When the Queen invites him to join the interplanetary Congress, Andrew unveils his aggressive plans for reinstating a republic. All the political corruption, betrayal, and deception of a rotten government is unleashed, and he is framed for murder, branded a traitor and forced to run.While enemies amass all around, and civil war engulfs the galaxy, Andrew’s pride alienates his few remaining allies. The deaths of those around him mount, forcing humility on Andrew at great cost. Pride crushed and questioning his own existence, he fights his demons in order to protect those he’s come to care for. Striking deep into the heart of enemy territory, he must accomplish the impossible…survive.
