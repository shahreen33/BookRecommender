A small Wyoming town is about to learn a few lessons -- from a new schoolteacher with the courage to win the heart of a man who swore he had nothing to give...Mary Elizabeth Potter is a self-appointed spinster with no illusions about love. But she is a good teacher -- and she wants Wolf MacKenzie's son back in school. And after one heated confrontation with the boy's father, she knows father and son have changed her life forever.Still paying for a crime he didn't commit, Wolf MacKenzie has a chip on his shoulder the size of Wyoming. But prim-and-proper Mary Elizabeth Potter doesn't see Wolf as the dangerous half-breed the town has branded him. Somehow she sees him as a good, decent, honest man. A man who could love...Wolf's not sure he -- or the town of Ruth, Wyoming -- is ready for the taming of Wolf MacKenzie.
