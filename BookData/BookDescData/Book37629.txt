When Jess’s daughter, Anna, is reported lost in an avalanche, everything changes.Left to explain her mother’s absence to Anna’s five-year-old daughter, Rose, Jess isn’t yet ready to admit to herself that her daughter might not be coming back. But Anna’s disappearance dredges up some life changing questions: Jess must uncover her daughter’s secret life – and unearth a secret that changes her world irrevocably.The day I lost you was the day I discovered your secret life.The day I lost you was the day you tore our family apart.
