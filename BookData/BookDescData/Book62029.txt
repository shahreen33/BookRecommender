A limited publication of a small book of poetic adorations dedicated to Our Lady of the Cup, BABALON. The book is the result of a magickal working in which the Scarlet Goddess was invoked. It was Her wish to be adored in songs of love.This publication will be strictly limited to a single run of only 156 copies and will never be reprinted. Each copy will be hand numbered and signed with the sigil of the author.
