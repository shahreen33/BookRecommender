A bright light shone and the next moment, Lauren Black is gone without a trace. Lance Black, an ex-soldier and her brother, and Tony Gwyn, an archaeology student and her boyfriend, are the only witnesses of what happened, and they come together to search for Lauren. Following the trail of the flashing light, they encounter a being that they never knew even existed. And it might be their only chance to find Lauren.  In this supernatural mystery thriller, a search for their loved one leads to an ancient race that have been here all along, invisible and waiting for the right time to take back what was theirs. The exhilarating introduction of this new science-fiction series will take you to down a path filled with paranormal happenings and strange beings as Lance and Tony uncover the mystery of the Prime Insurgency.  Book One of Eleven.
