Picking up right where Drako’s Return left off, Victoria and Claude are still at odds. Claude still wants Vic to be his, and Vic still claims her love to Robert. With the promise of destroying everyone and everything Vic loves dearly, she must decide her fate, as well as the rest of the kingdom’s fate. A battle leaves many dead or dying. In order to save lives and bring the dead back to life, they will have to be turned or given the antidote. With a plan in play to combine the Hecatian blood with the cure, will it save them? Or is it too late? The plan for a New Race!
