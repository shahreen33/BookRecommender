Police detective Penny Britain is transferred to Dorset while her earlier exploits fade from memory.The peaceful County hides a crime of staggering proportions. Triggered by the death of a professor, the appearance of a stalker, a missing child, a paedophile, and mysterious happenings involving an ancient church and derelict stone house, the breathless pace of the story heats up in ways that just gets worse for poor Penny.John Day is known for his gripping stories and this is the best yet.
