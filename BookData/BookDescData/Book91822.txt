Eric Bittle—vlogger, baker, and starting forward for Samwell men's hockey—is entering his junior year with a secret. He's dating Jack Zimmermann. And Jack? Just happens to be starting his professional hockey career with the Providence Falconers. Off the ice, Jack and Bitty must figure out how to come out to friends, teammates and family. But on the ice, the stage gets even bigger. Follow Bitty as he dodges every check life throws at him during his junior year!
