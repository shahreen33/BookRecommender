A compendium of trivia, news, rumors and information from the Outer Rim Territories. Inside this Galaxy Guide, there are countless new characters, ships, aliens and locations to populate your campaign. Meet some of the most famous Rebel heroes, Imperial despots and dangerous pirates and smugglers in this section of the galaxy.This Galaxy Guide provides gamemasters with a wealth of information about life in the Star Wars universe, and includes chapters on the Rebel Alliance and the Empire, swoop gangs, mercenary units, pirates, corporations, media organizations, the Force, and even music, social life and culture! The perfect supplement to add atmosphere and flavor to your Star Wars game
