Sally Dubats, herself a witch, offers an enchanting and comprehensive guide to Wiccan practices and principles. Perfect for practicing witches -- as well as for everyone interested in folklore, alternative healing, and the natural wisdom of the ages -- "Natural Magick" provides in-depth, fascinating information about the types of tools and elements used by modern witches in their daily lives, as well as the traditional meanings behind a variety of magical rites. Topics include: instructions for using candles, elixirs, jewelry, stones, crystals, oils, and incense; the benefits of using herbs, from relieving headaches to creating prosperity; and much more.
