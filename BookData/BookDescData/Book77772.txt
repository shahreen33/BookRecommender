When a little princess advertises for a big, cuddly bear friend, the replies come from all over the world. But the bear with the friendliest eyes, Kvitebjørn, is the bear she chooses and thus begins a most unconventional friendship.The sweetest friendship story we've seen in a long time, this is a snowy frolic with style. Starring a lovable little girl and a big cuddly bear, children and adults will surely want a Kvitebjørn of their own!
