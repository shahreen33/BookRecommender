Spellcaster: The Frostgrave Magazine exists to bring new rules, scenarios, and gaming ideas for players of Frostgrave and Frostgrave: Ghost Archipelago.  This first issue contains:  * Rules for using black powder firearms * Rules for horses in the Frozen City * Rules for Knightly Orders which add variety to your knights and templars * Alone in the Crypt – a solo Frostgrave scenario in which you play a captain with a quiver-full of magic arrows trying to escape a crypt full of undead * The Catacombs of the Evrenbright – a three scenario mini-campaign originally created for Adepticon 2017
