Want. Need. Lust. Just one more night!For book editor Veronica Johnson it's sheer hell seeing her ex Rafael Velez again. He's the man she thought she'd be with for a lifetime, and here he is at her best friend's wedding. How she hates him still! But Rafael has an outrageous proposition: just one more night together! It's madness -- but achingly tempting. Especially if she walks away without a backwards glance, just as he did to her...
