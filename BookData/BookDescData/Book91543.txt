One Hit Man Hired.The sleepy village of Waterfell Tweed is rocked to its core by the murder of a local teacher.The plot thickens when the police reveal that the murder was committed by a hired hit man, and Sandy Shaw is thrown into the case when an old acquaintance phones for her help from HMP Leyton Scrubs.Sandy has solved her share of murders, but she is about to enter a strange underworld: where a man's life has a price. And nothing is as it seems.Can Sandy solve the case, or has she met her match?
