Havana, Arkansas isn't exactly the picture of mystics and magic, and Lynnie Russell is anything but special. But on the night of her twentieth birthday, Lynnie learns quickly you can't judge a book by its cover.  When an unexpected encounter turns deadly, Lynnie discovers myths and legends are alive and well in her tiny little town and she is at the center of it. Her world is quickly turned upside down and the quiet existence she once loathed is nothing compared to what she has become. In the end, her becoming is only the beginning.
