A board book featuring Peppa--a lovable, slighty bossy little piggy--and her first pet!Oh no! Peppa Pig's pet fish, Goldie, isn't feeling well. Join Peppa Pig, George, and Mommy Pig in this new storybook, as they take an exciting bus ride to the vet to find out why Goldie isn't eating her food. Featuring a beautiful cover with matte and spot gloss.
