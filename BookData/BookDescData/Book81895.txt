Imprinted By The Alpha - The Marked One Book 1  NOTE: BOOK DOES CONTAIN A CLIFFHANGER!  Ava is drawn back to her hometown just weeks before her 21st birthday and she has no idea why. After meeting Alpha Wolf Caleb, she’s content with the small town living she’d known before, but her best friend Kelly will reveal Willow Falls is a town with many faces.  When Ava’s mother reveals a secret prophecy, she is faced with a future far removed from the one she expected.  She must navigate her new love while facing a responsibility she doesn’t feel prepared for. Whether she is ready or not, everything she knows about Willow Falls is changing - including her.  This is the first book of an ongoing serial and does contain a cliffhanger. This story is for ages 18+ and is not intended for readers uncomfortable with sexual scenarios.
