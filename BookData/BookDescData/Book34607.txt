Anthology 2017 is a collection of writing from the students of the University of Greenwich.It contains a diverse selection of short fiction, poetry, novel extracts, and scripts showcasing the talents and fantastic potential of students studying a variety of courses on the university's thriving undergraduate and graduate programmes.Edited by: Greta Alp, Stacey Alsworth, Helene Anglade, Megan Avery, Daniel Barber, Joseph Eccleshall, Amy Ensinger, Curtis Garner, Jemma Ginn, Thomas Hadfield, Alice Hinton, Bethany Jones, Hanne Nord, Emily Page, Molly Purdue-Guy, Janayne Riani, Matthew Rooke, Natalia Rose, Jade Sydenham, Molly WoodwardContributions by: Greta Alp, Parissa Bagheri, Daniel Barber, Shakela Begum, MJ Bellini, Aidan Brunt, Cecilia Campanale, Isobel Clarke, Bobbie May Corleys, Cristina Cristache-Schuller, Acacia Daud, Hannah Davies, Samia Djilli, Aylia Fox, Sophie Frankis, RÅ«ta Gaile, Melissa Garrett, Shin George, Jo Gerrard, Jemma Ginn, Nick Granata, Nicholas Paul Griffin, Eren Gudney, Sarah Gul-Mohamed, Thom Hadfield, Luisa Hausleithner, AL Hinton, Meggie Johnson-Clarke, Victoria Macfarlane, Holly Matthews, Rebecca Moloney, Juwairiah Mussa, Hanne Nord, Chloe Norton, Mabel Perry, Shikira Porter, Inga Raudina, Joe Richardson-Smith, Kern Robinson, Lily Roubians, Marcel Ryde, Annalise Seixas Sa, Katha Shah, Paige Smith, Niall Somerville, Jade Sydenham, Emily Wood, Molly Woodward
