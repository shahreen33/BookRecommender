Calling on all of his elemental powers, Hephaestus, God of Fire, forged a mighty sword of fire for his king, Zeus. But in a bold and treacherous move, marauders have stolen the magical blade. Now it is up to Hercules to recover it--though he may be in for more than just a fight with ambitious thieves. For someone far more powerful has plans for Zeus's sword of fire. And for Hercules!
