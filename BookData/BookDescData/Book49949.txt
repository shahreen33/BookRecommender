A short story featuring Deegie Tibbs from the series The Complicated Life of Deegie Tibbs Deegie just wants to go home, but her beat-up old van has other plans. It breaks down in front of a dusty old motel that looks like it came from a Wild West movie! After renting a room to stay in while her van is being repaired, she discovers that she has a most unusual roommate, someone who's been staying in this room for a long time. Deegie soon finds herself in the middle of a mystery dating back to the 1800's--a mystery only she can solve! It's a creepy little adventure featuring lost treasure, a long-ago murder, and a cheeky flame-haired ghost!
