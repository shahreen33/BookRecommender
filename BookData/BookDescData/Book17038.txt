Can Scarlett choose between her fey love or her vampire love?Sightseer Scarlett Seelie is lured into the immortal realm in her quest with the charming Lord Darthmoor and gorgeous Tavian in search of the Immortal Devices that wield immense powers, like the power to control all beings of Love. In this exciting sequel in the Steampunk Scarlett series, Scarlett encounters danger and enough twists and turns to keep her guessing as to who to trust and who to love.
