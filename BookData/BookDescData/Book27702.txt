Some cats just want to watch the world burn... Jill and Bill Cook didn’t care much for their slice of hamlet, but The Noke is home. And sometimes home can be the most difficult place to get away from. Jill also didn’t care much for cats, but cats don’t seem to be in short supply in The Noke. Swarming in from the abandoned sections of used up coal refineries, their home soon becomes overrun by multitudes of feral felines with strange glowing green eyes. What do these strays want? And why won’t they go away?
