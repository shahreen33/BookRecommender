Christopher Caldwell is a man with many demons. Chris’s love for beautiful women and his insatiable sexual appetite took him on a journey of lust, passion and sizzling hot sexual encounters. Due to his experience as a boat captain and his natural ability of driving high speed boats. He quickly became popular with the local smugglers. He avoided his destiny until a notorious crime family requested that he pay an old family debt. It wasn’t long before the drug lords found a way to put his talents to work and changed his life forever.
