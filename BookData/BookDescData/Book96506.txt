A criminal mastermind named Malestar terrorizes the city of Salvador, Brazil, and after the crime spree, the police department and mayor become desperate and unofficially seek help from a band of mercenaries that are known to find criminals, but unlike bounty hunters, they don't return them alive. One such mercenary, Mauricio Campos, draws the interest of Malestar, and the line between predator and prey becomes blurred.
