 What happens in the city of sin during a total blackout? EVERYTHING...Cane O'Reilly has one job, keep Charlie Adams alive. When witness protection fails, more than once, he's the only one who can protect her.As the only surviving victim of an international serial killer, she comes with a hell of a lot of baggage and...other needs Cane is happy to fulfill.Hiding out in one of the most secure cities, they keep each other company while Cane has eyes everywhere searching for the killer with unfinished business...Charlie.The day started with a quick screw, a couple of quirky two-bit cons and ended with a diabolical serial killer. What do you do when the lights go out? Run.
