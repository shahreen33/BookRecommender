"He's not a friend, he's my husband. And he's gone."​En route to Salt Lake City, intending to start over, their car breaks down near the struggling mining town of Helper, Utah—and then Tony leaves Kora behind.​Alone, broken and angry, especially that her family was right in their judgment against Tony, Kora decides to stay in Helper and aid in it’s art-centered transformation.​But in working to save her new home, Kora learns first love only happens once.For a second chance at love, a much greater risk is required.
