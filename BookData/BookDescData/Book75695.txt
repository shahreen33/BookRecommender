Special Agent Patrick Powers returns in an electrifying prequel to The Bowers Files from critically acclaimed, national bestselling novelist Steven James.   While investigating a murder scene alone, Bowers is attacked by a man who keeps asking him inane questions for which Bowers has no answers—and who then takes his own life rather than be arrested. Later, on the attacker’s body, Bowers finds an letter containing a vague apology.   This enigmatic murder and suicide is the first step on a dangerous journey that will take Bowers into the very depths of human depravity. For there appears to be an online cabal of sexual predators snatching children off the streets.   With time running out, Bowers must think fast and move faster if he is going to stop them and save whatever victims are still alive.
