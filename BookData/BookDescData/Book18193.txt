Book 1 of the Liliana seriesBoom!That deafening sound changed Liliana’s life forever.Her mother sits dead besides her, shot to death on the streets of Chicago.Within weeks, Liliana is sent to live with a father she doesn’t know in Colombia - a foreign land filled with challenges for Liliana. While working to pay off her father’s debt, she meets the love of her life, who frees her from her father’s illusory home, only to bring her into a new world of twisted surprises, dangers, and deceit.
