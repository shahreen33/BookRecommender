Rain is a waitress by day, but when not at the Black Cat she's working hard to make her art career take off. Her paintings are the center of her life until Laurent walks into her cafe. Suddenly she's dealing with a very alpha male pursuing her and the not-so-very-tiny detail that he happens to be a werewolf. Laurent Cole has been the Third in command in the Cherchez Wolf Pack for decades. His life is simple, good--but he knows he's missing something, and when he claps eyes on Rain, he knows what it is. But their coming together is complicated by the past Rain has worked so hard to keep a secret. Laurent isn't about to let anyone do his mate any harm. But the world becomes more complicated every day ...
