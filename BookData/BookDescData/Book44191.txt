Hex11 is the epic story of a future where magic has been discovered as a new technology. Elanor, a young witch-in-training, navigates the urban underworld known as the Hex; facing corporate conspiracies, demon mercenaries, and black market magic dealers.In the wake of the events of Volume One, Elanor has become both hero and villain within The Hex, finding herself caught between two worlds. The power she has and the evil she vowed to fight, may turn out to be one and the same.Contains issues #7-#12
