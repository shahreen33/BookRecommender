Brake hard, jostle for position, and watch for the blind corner. In a swirl of controversy, Declan Reede lost everything important to him. Now old temptations resurface, old battles reignite, and he has nothing left in reserve. When a glimmer of hope is offered, Declan has to stand and fight for what he wants or he might just lose it all forever. How can he decipher the clues and get revenge on those who want to see him burn?
