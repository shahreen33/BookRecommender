Herewith begins the fourth volume from your hardy heroine, Cat Royal. In which I become an unlikely recruit for the British Navy, take passage to America, and navigate my way through a fiendish plot to do away with Lord Francis, heir to a dukedom.Cat is living in the lap of luxury--and so bored she's out of her mind. But when she and her friends are captured and forced to work on a boat bound for the New World, Cat may finally be in over her head. Sail with Cat to America, to explore the wilds of a new frontier and maybe even find a place where she is finally free to be herself.
