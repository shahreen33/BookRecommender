Touching poetry, thought-provoking flash fiction, fantasy stories, contemporary stories, a children’s story, a comic and several illustrations – all found within this book. Twenty-seven Maltese or locally-based talents got together to offer you the first fiction Anthology full of queer identities as part of the Kitba Queer project.This project is supported by Arts Council Malta – Creative Communities.Poeżiji li jmissu l-qalb, kitbiet qosra li jġegħluk taħseb, stejjer ta’ fantasija, stejjer kontemporanji, storja mmirata għat-tfal, komik u bosta llustrazzjonijiet. Sebgħa u għoxrin talent lokali ingħaqdu sabiex din l-ewwel Antoloġija tieħu l-ħajja. Antoloġija mimlija b’identitajiet queer bħala parti mill-proġett Kitba Queer.Dan il-proġett hu megħjun mill-Kunsill Malti għall-Arti – Komunitajiet Kreattivi.
