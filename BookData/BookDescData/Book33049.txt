Collects Giant Size X-Men #1, Uncanny X-men #94-131 and X-Men Annual #3. When Chris Claremont took over X-Men in 1976, few fans could predict the impact he would have on the Marvel Comics series. With a flair for realistic dialogue, heartfelt storylines and hard-hitting action, Claremont's writing breathed life into the characters. This title brings together his first five years work in one volume.
