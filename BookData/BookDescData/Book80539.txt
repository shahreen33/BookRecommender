A young man goes to buy flowers for his girlfriend... and makes a mistake he'll never forget. A disgruntled man of the old world order can accept it no more. But how far is he willing to go? Following her grandmother to the shore, a little girl notices that this time, something is different.An old, haggard man prepares to leave for his final journey.Eight intriguing stories of mystery, love, horror and action from Petteri Hannila, the author of Fargoer.
