Genny the thirteen-year-old genie sets out to create the perfect boy in the latest installment of this sassy, fun girls series.Andrew thinks he has one big love problem, but really he has three -- MacKenzie, Jane, and Genny. MacKenzie is the ultra-cool girl he's crushing on. Jane is the best friend who's unfortunately crushing on him. And Genny is the genie who's supposed to make Andrew the boy of MacKenzie's dreams. But things with Genny never work out exactly as planned, and soon Andrew's life is totally out of control.
