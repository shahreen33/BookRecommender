The author of the stand-alone series of Small Town Bachelor Romances brings readers I've Got You, a story of what happens when love finds a way in through the cracks of our armor.A sudden windfall brings Edie to an odd little town in search of herself, and some much needed peace and quiet. She’s got no time for male attention, especially not from Lane, the town’s charming golden boy. Will Lane’s actions prove to her that charm and substance can coexist? Will she believe he’s always going to have her back?
