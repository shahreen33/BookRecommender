Margene Dalton has dealt with fat jokes for most of her life Known as Mushy Maggie throughout high school, Margene Dalton decides to execute a plan of revenge against her teenage nemesis, Kimmie Anderson. When Margene's initial plan backfires, she finds herself enlisting the help of an unlikely ally, Kimmie's boyfriend, an Army officer who has recently returned from deployment.Michael's last deployment has left him emotionally scarred, but he is unwilling to talk about his experiences with Kimmie. He doesn't truly understand the complex history between Kimmie and Margene, but when Mushy Maggie initiates a passionate romp, he is more than eager to find pleasure in this plump girl's arms.
