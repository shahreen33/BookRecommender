TOGETHER FOREVER?Yuma and Hotaru have been best friends and neighbors all their lives…but when they became lovers, things got a lot more complicated! Now, as Yuma tries to figure out her future, she also has to figure out how Hotaru fits into it. Just as Yuma comes to terms with her feelings, Hotaru vanishes! In this final volume, will the two girls end up together…or will they be split up forever?
