Milana is a beautiful and mischievous little girl with a naughty habit of running away. While she finds it amusing, it frightens her family.One day, on a trip to the shopping centre with her grandfather, Milana is excited by all the shops and people, lights and indoor play equipment. Milana wants an adventure, but she’s promised her grandfather she won’t run away. Sometimes, the call for an adventure is too hard to resist, and sometimes, adventures aren’t all they’re cracked up to be…
