Selina Kyle put aside her life as Catwoman to run Gotham's criminal underworld as its queen of crime, but now the catsuit's back on and Catwoman's got a new mission--find out what happened to Batman! Spinning out of the events of Scott Snyder and Greg Capullo's Batman: Endgame, Selina Kyle finds herself juggling a criminal empire constantly on the brink of war and a search for an old friend and sometimes-foe. Will Selina find the answers she's after, or will curiosity kill this cat?Don't miss the second volume of Catwoman from novelist Genevieve Valentine, with art by Dave Messina (Angel, The Bounce).Collects Catwoman #41-47.
