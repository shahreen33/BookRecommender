The year is 1890, and a small town mayor is trying to hold his community together, while seeking revenge for his wife's recent murder.The year is 1972, and a small time crook is in way over her head as a deal goes very wrong.The year is 2020, and a homicide detective is on the trail of a serial-killing vigilante.On the eve of the 2020 presidential election, these characters, their stories, and more collide in cataclysmic fashion. The world will never be the same again, in an often grim, sometimes hopeful, modern fantasy that spans across the ages.
