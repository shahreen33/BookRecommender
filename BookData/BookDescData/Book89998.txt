This is one of Ace Books "double novel books," featuring two novellas.In "Slavers of Space" human society has spread to numberless planets, and robots and blue skinned androids (artificially created humans) do all the menial labor. Robots are nothing but tools, and androids are little better than slaves. Derry Horn, young scion of a wealthy family thinks nothing of this until a mysterious man is found dead in an adjacent hotel room, and an android convinces him to investigate."Dr. Futurity" follows the desperate journey of a doctor plucked from his own time and taken hundreds of years into the future in order to save the life of a man killed in the 17th century.
