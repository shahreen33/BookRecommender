Leanne Simmons and Callum Davidson are complete opposites. As soon as they meet they know they won’t get along. However, for the sake of their TV show they agree to put their differences aside, but it’s not as easy as they think.For one, there’s the unexplainable physical attraction they can’t seem to shake. And for two, everything out of their mouths seems to rub the other the wrong way.Leanne wonders how she can be attracted to such a careless, narcissistic playboy.Callum wonders why he can’t get a girl who dresses like a boy out of his head.In Air Kiss readers will get to experience first-hand the trials and tribulations of the pair during the early days of Running on Air.
