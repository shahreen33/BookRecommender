New to the city of Olympus, Cora runs from a stalker straight into the clutches of mob boss Marcus Ubeli. Wealthy and powerful, the handsome business man sweeps her off her feet in a dizzying romance. But as she falls deeper under his spell, she begins to discover the truth of the city's criminal underworld. Can she truly love a man who will never let her go? * Cora is the maiden Persephone and Hades is Marcus Ubeli in this modern retelling of the Greek myth.
