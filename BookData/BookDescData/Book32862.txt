** Going Down is a little bit sweet &amp; sexy rom-com and a whole lot of second chance romance.When the power goes out Reece finds himself stuck in an elevator alone with his ex and a bag of vibrating glow in the dark bachelorette party favors, but more importantly, the opportunity of a lifetime with the woman he’s never stopped loving. If he’s lucky their time alone in the dark may just help them push past all of the feelings that come with twelve years of heartbreak, hatred, and secrets. By the time they walk off this elevator, there’s no way she’ll be able to deny their chemistry still runs hot. If only she could stand the sight of him.
