A killer runs rampant in the Ozarks.The supernatural community is at risk.It’s up to James Marius, Praetor of the Ozarks region to stop the killings and save the day.But he can’t do it alone. With unknown enemies rising at every turn, Jimmy has to rely on new allies; a foul-mouthed, bass singing gnome and a killer from a bygone age to stop his enemies from raising Hell in the Holler.If you’re a fan of Bill the Vampire, Montague and Strong, or Bubba the Monster Hunter, you’ll love the Ozark Mountain Mysteries.Get it now!
