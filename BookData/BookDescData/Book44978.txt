The authorized graphic novel sequel to Saban’s Power Rangers, the 2017 film that reboots the classic TV series for the modern age. Five teenagers, brought together under extraordinary circumstances, are entrusted to defend Earth against alien threats as...the Power Rangers.   Based on the world wide phenomenon, the Rangers have returned to the big screen like never before and this authorized original graphic novel serves as a direct follow up to the blockbuster film.  Written by Ryan Parrott (Star Trek: Manifest Destiny) and illustrated by rising talent Lucas Werneck, the Power Rangers face off against an all new threat to the city of Angel Grove.
