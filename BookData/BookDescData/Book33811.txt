Eddie Dean, the troubled young man gifted with the ability to open doors to other worlds, has smuggled narcotics from Nassau to New York City, but now has to escape a packed airplane guarded by armed Custom Agents! How will Eddie avoid prison and yet also fulfil his contract with the dangerous mobster Balazar? The answer lies in Mid-World and with a dying gunslinger named Roland! Collecting: Dark Tower: The Drawing of the Three - House of Cards 1-5
