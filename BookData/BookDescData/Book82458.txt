Twenty two years ago, Alex McCray found the first of the Twelve Stones, a set of powerful artifacts left for Humanity to find and use to save Earth from certain destruction.  While billionaire industrialist Rupert Kline consolidates his empire and searches for the final stone, Alex McCray and his friends have been lost in time. Emily, Scott and Christina desperately work to stop Kline from collecting the final stone, Alex is forced to confront the biggest enemy he'll ever face - himself.  A thrilling tale that mixes romance, cutting edge science, and oh yeah, the fate of human civilization.
