Diverse, dark-humoured, and deliciously bite-sized, this compelling collection of 12 short stories includes: EscapeA suicidal baby who knows he was born into the wrong life must get creative to correct the mistake, much to his mother's horror. The ItchA story about an intense, uncontrollable, unexplainable itch that lands the protagonist in a mental institution. Bridge GateA daughter years to connect with her absent father through they letters they exchange. She's not put off by his pedantic corrections of her writing, despite the slow reveal that he is less than perfect himself.The Unsuspecting Gold-diggerA woman gradually poisons her husband so that she doesn't have to break his heart.
