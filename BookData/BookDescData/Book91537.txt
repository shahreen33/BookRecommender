In the woods, nightmares come to life...   Halloween should have been a fun evening out in Chapel End village, but it quickly descends into mayhem for postwoman, Jenny Reid.   When a dead body is found in the woods, the victim is quickly connected to a man she once loved, but does he have a motive for murder? Frustration mounts when the old flame refuses to give an alibi for where he was at the time of the death. Surely someone else is responsible, but with most partygoers in costume, how is she supposed to identify the culprit?   Jenny feels the need to prove his innocence for him and becomes embroiled in the case.
