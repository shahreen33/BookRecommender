Is Man's World ready for Wonder Woman to bring Amazonian justice to today's society? James Robinson writes a tale of Diana visiting the small-time crook known as Mayfly in jail over the course of several years. But how do these visits impact Diana and Mayfly?At the orders of Aztek, Diana and Artemis join her for a trip to Mexico, but there's no sightseeing on the agenda. Instead, they must rescue a long-lost Amazon who's being held captive by rogue deity Tezcatlipoca, who's trying to break into our world. Can these three heroes save the missing Amazon, stop a raging god and not kill one another in the process?Find out in Wonder Woman Vol. 9!
