A comic, chick lit tale wherein former beauty queen Foxy Anders, who's fallen on hard times, rents an apartment to mysterious, bumbling Myron Standlish who's arrived in the city looking for a long lost trunk containing who knows what. When Foxy's teenage daughter, Amanda hooks up with Nick, a cute guy at school, while getting cooking lessons from Foxy's new assistant Knot, they're all in for some romance with a dash of suspense and a sprinkle of supernatural.
