Spring is in the air, and with spring comes break! When Shigure takes Tohru, Yuki, and Kyo to the Sohma's vacation home to make the most of Golden Week, the inevitable game of cat and rat is already underway, with sparks flying between Yuki and Kyo. But leave it to Tohru to smooth things over! The peace doesn't last, however, when another Sohma (or two?!) drops in! But even a vacation can't prepare the group for the surprise that awaits them upon their return. When a face from Kyo's past makes an appearance, he lets a shocking secret about the cat out of the bag...
