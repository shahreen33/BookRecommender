Beyond Downton Abbey is a guide to 25 great houses in Britain, with 25 compelling stories and 25 reasons to visit.The popular television series Downton Abbey is just the start. Britain is filled with historic houses, castles and palaces. Join us as we take you to dozens of the grandest, most romantic, quirkiest great houses in England and Wales.Beyond Downton Abbey is filled with fascinating tidbits from the history of the houses and the families that lived and worked in them.Full color, with beautiful photographs, this is a guidebook for serious travelers and armchair tourists alike.
