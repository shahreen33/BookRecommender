Anna Docherty loves the woods. Until the night a game of hide and seek sends her running a little too far, a little too fast.  Lost in the dark, it doesn’t take long for Anna’s love of the forest to turn to terror.  When Ryan finds her, huddled in his childhood hideout, and leads her back to safety, Anna is forever grateful to him.  But is she grateful enough to fight her fear when Ryan’s the one who needs help?
