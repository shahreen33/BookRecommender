Sorrow’s Queen Sorrow slips in quietly when no one is looking — just like I do. It robs them of life and joy — just like. Sorrow is a killer in the night, murdering hearts and leaving destruction in it’s wake — and so am I. I am Tristan Sorrow, I stole happiness, and now she is mine. He came in in the dead of night to steal me, to take me home. He was sent to rob me of my happiness and murder my hopes.Creeping in in slowly, he was an unexpected twist in my tale. But he stole the wrong thing, he made me his Queen. Tristan Sorrow stole my heart — and my life. Every love story is written tragically in the blood of those who fell.
