When twelve-year-old Trey and his best friend Eddy play a prank on Officer Mack, the resulting chain of events rocks the small town of Bay's End.Today, Trey Franklin is a man haunted by his past. Tormented by that one tragic, fateful summer, Trey searches for catharsis the only way he knows how - by writing.A tale of love and loss, bittersweet memories, and the depths of human evil.Welcome to Bay's End.*Warning: Contains graphic language and adult situations.
