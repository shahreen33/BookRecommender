Collection of Additional Point of View (POV) Scenes from Smoke and Wishes (Best Wishes book 1)  This includes over ten additional POV scenes from the characters of the Best Wishes series including a deleted chapter from book 1. There are also three additional scenes that are pre-Smoke and Wishes including a never before seen POV from Camden and Nikolai, as well as an exclusive look at the unedited first chapter of book 2- Smoke and Survival (releasing July 1st, 2019).  These POVs are in chronological order, corresponding chapters to Smoke and Wishes are noted for easy reference.   The Best Wishes Series is a WhyChoose/Reverse Harem saga featuring MMFMMMM meaning there is M/M content, and the female main character doesn't have to choose between her love interests.  These POVs possibly contain references involving PTSD, sexual assault recollections, domestic violence, and other themes that some readers may find triggering.
