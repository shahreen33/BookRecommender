A Murderer named Gary Soneji patiently waits in the basement of Alex Cross's home in Washington. He has come to kill Cross and his family. But this cunnign hunter is not alone. Now two mind-numbing killers-one operating up and down the East Coast of America, one in Europe-believe Cross is their only worthy opponent. They dare to ask the ultimate question: Is Alex Cross about to die? You won't put CAT AND MOUSE down until the question is answered.
