Psychic, Oliver, and DI Langham have the huge task of finding more than twenty men who have committed a murder.  Adam is exposed to a frightening episode in the mini-mart, forcing him to move away to the one place he shouldn’t have gone… What should have been a shelter from the evil of city life becomes another nightmare.  Why are the men killing?  What does the ritual beforehand mean?  Oliver and Langham don’t know, but together, they are drawn into a web of deceit.
