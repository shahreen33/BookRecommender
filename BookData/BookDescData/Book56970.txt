Goodfellas meets Freaky Friday in this mind-bending new ongoing series from Fan-favorite creators GAIL SIMONE (Wonder Woman, Batgirl, Deadpool, Secret Six, Birds of Prey) and CAT STAGGS (Adventures of Supergirl, Smallville Season 11, Wonder Woman '77).A slick and ruthless Chicago hitman.A smart but downtrodden Seattle housewife.When an inexplicable event strikes these two random strangers, their bodies, souls, and lives are switched--to potentially deadly effect.Collects issues 1-6.
