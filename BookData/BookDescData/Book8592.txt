There is a fire star coming, signaling a time of new beginnings. A time for dragons to rise again...A research trip to the Arctic and a contract for a new book - life can't get much better for David Rain. But as soon as David finds himself in the icy climes, he begins to write his legend of bears, dragons, and the mmysterious fire star. Soon he realizes that his tale is starting to mirror real life, and that an old enemy is on her way to meet him.Can David thwart her terrible master plan? Or will his world be destroyed forever?
