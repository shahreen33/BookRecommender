How one night at the Mansion could’ve changed Jocelyn’s life. Now living there, becoming a hostess and sinking into the darkest fathoms of lust. Jocelyn’s life changed but her past still haunted her. The grip of insecurities, never being good enough, gnawed away at her. Could she finally put Devin to rest or does his evil shadow haunt her? It wasn’t till she confronted the demons, did she truly find peace within her dark abyss. Or has she? This is the second installment in the Dark Desire series. Jocelyn’s adventures don’t stop here, book three will be released in June.
