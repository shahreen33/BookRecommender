Sink your teeth into A MONSTROUS LOVE: Two Halloween Romances from Magen Cubed (FLESHTRAP, THE CRASHERS). This mini-collection features two short stories about monstrous women in love, just in time for Halloween. THE HAUNTING OF DIVINIA is an unconventional ghost story, and TASTE tells the story of a young couple with an unusual hobby. If you enjoy your romance on the eerie side, A MONSTROUS LOVE is the perfect short read.
