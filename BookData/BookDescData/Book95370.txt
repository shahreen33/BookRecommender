Written by Will Pfeifer Art and cover by Cliff Chiang It's the rebirth of a hero in this eerie volume collecting the 3-issue miniseries that links the events of INFINITE CRISIS to the present and the future! The supernatural spirit of vengeance has joined with a new host: Detective Crispus Allen from the Gotham City Police Department. But Allen wants nothing to do with the Spectre or his holy mission - even if it means jeopardizing the mission!Collects CRISIS AFTER MATH: THE SPECTRE #1-3 and TALES OF THE UNEXPECTED #1-3.
