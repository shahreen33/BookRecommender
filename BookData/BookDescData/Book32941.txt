Elissa Holt is beautiful, intelligent, and a kickass stage manager. She's also the sister of Ethan Holt, one of the hottest young actors around. When Ethan's world is turned upside down by an ex-girlfriend bitch-monster from hell, Elissa is there to help him pick up the pieces. Helping Ethan get over his heartbreak isn't easy, but when a fiery young actress comes into their lives at drama school, Elissa hopes she may have an ally in making her brother whole once more.(BACKSTAGE PASS is a collection of scenes designed to give insight into the characters from BAD ROMEO and BROKEN JULIET. Warning: may contain minor spoilers for the main novels.)
