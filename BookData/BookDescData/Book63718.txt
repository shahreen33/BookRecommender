PAST SIN, INSECURITIES, DOUBTS, FEARS: THEY PLAGUE US. THEY KEEP US CAGED.Many Christian men struggle with feeling unworthy or unqualified to do God's work. You are not alone. Giants of the faith have felt helpless. Most saints would be surprised at the title. God is not limited by broken vessels. He is given greater glory through them. Through stories of biblical heroes - Jonathan, David, Moses, and more - we can learn to see the power of God in unremarkable human beings.Men with failings, flaws, doubts, and fears.Men who were chosen, though they were broken.Men who were helpless without their Savior.
