The momentous final volume of the magical manga classic!Anna Heartfilia was convinced that the space between time would imprison the evil dragon Acnologia forever. But when the space allows Acnologia's physical body and spirit to split in two, his body wrests free, setting out on a rampage of utter destruction... And before they can fight back, Natsu and the other dragon slayers get dragged into the space between time! Will Fairy Tail be able to share one last laugh together? Or is it all too late? With a family like Fairy Tail, the impossible might just be possible! FINAL VOLUME!
