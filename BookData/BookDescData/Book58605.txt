Find the compass, save the world or save herself?  For Austin robbery detective Maggie Parker, dating is harder than running down a felon, even if the guy is worth it – just ask Jake. Now add in magic.  When she finds someone breaking into her garage, stealing a favorite wooden puzzle box everything changes. Did she just see a compass fly?  Can she learn how to use the magic of bubbles to chart a new course in time? The mechanics may hold the answer.  Her new quest: Rescue the passengers on an ancient ship – a big blue marble called Earth – and save herself.  Recover the compass, find the other Elementals and help decide Earth’s fate.  Simple? It’s a lot harder than it sounds. And there’s that fourth date…
