Cub and his little sister are fighting, and Cub gets in trouble with Dad. Cub walks off angrily and sees Zebra, who tells him that zebras stamp and stomp when theyre angry. Hippo explains that hippos crash and splash to get the anger out. Still upset, Cub runs into Elephant and causes even more trouble. Maybe Baboon can help. The perfect story for any child experiencing anger and learning how to handle those feelings.
