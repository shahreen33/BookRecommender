Just when you thought it couldn't get any sexier... they're back. But this time Max needs Brooke's help.   When Max receives a phone call from his step-brother, Thomas, inviting him to their parent’s tenth wedding anniversary party, their rivalry for one another comes straight into play. A barbed comment from Thomas forces Max to tell one hell of a lie and now he needs help. Cue Brooke Knight and a very awkward conversation for Max.  Will Brooke accept his proposal and help him carry out his charade...? Of course she will. But she has conditions.  It's a weekend full of sex, booze and lies. But at the end of it will Brooke be glad she went along for the ride?
