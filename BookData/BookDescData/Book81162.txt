Lizzie Hart is back - snooping, lying, and chick-fighting to uncover the truth, hoping not to break any hearts along the way... Especially her own. Lizzie Hart hoped her first day back at work after nearly being killed would be uneventful. No suck luck. Before she can finish her morning coffee, Lizzie and her co-workers find a dead body on the rooftop of their office. Media vultures that they are, the Liberty Chronicle employees are psyched to have first hand news to report. Lizzie, however, is devastated when she realizes that the victim is her ex-boyfriend's brother.
