The Charmed Killer is on the loose in Atlanta and Carlotta Wren is caught up in the terror�especially when her body-moving side business brings her dangerously close to the action.
  And then…
She's forced to take refuge in her former fiancé's house�much to the chagrin of other interested parties…Her brother Wesley begins to behave as if he has his own death wish…And someone close to her is implicated in the mass murders.Meanwhile, Carlotta can't shake the feeling that danger is dogging her seemingly cursed family�and that the serial killer's exploits are starting to get personal….
