Meet Caroline Quiner......the little girl who would grow up to be Laura Ingalls Wilder's mother. Caroline and her family are leaving the little town of Brookfield and moving to a new house in a clearing among the big trees of Concord, Wisconsin. As the Quiners travel through the dense forest, Caroline is excited, but she is also a little bit afraid. Will shelike her new home in Concord as much as her little house in Brookfield? "Little Clearing in the Woods" is the third book in an ongoing series about the adventures of another girl from America's favorite pioneer family
