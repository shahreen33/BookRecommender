The best of the old and the new come together to create a beautiful volume featuring 13 sensational quilts. Using traditional designs and contemporary fabrics, this collection of popular favorites includes pieced quilts, applique designs, and a final section combining the two methods. Each quilt, showcased in brilliant photography, is classic in its simplicity and design. All are presented with four optional color schemes featuring contemporary fabric choices, and step-by-step instructions for creating bed quilts, wall hangings, and throws. From piecing to applique to a combination of both, these quilts display a stunning range of skills for the intermediate quilter.
