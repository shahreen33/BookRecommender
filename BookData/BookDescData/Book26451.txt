Not looking for love. No details. Follow rules and restrictions. Only fun. No feelings. Forever--not an option. Simple Flings. They were all just a number. Dash Oliver and Willow Dane both shared the same philosophy. That is, until a merger of sorts. Thrown together by fate or serendipity, was their connection... their chemistry... their compatibility enough to convince them that their rules were made to be broken and that they belonged together?
