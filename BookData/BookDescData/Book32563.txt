You can't choose your family. Or can you? Meet the Bensons. A pleasant enough couple. They keep themselves to themselves. They wash their car, mow their lawn and pass the time of day with their neighbours. And they have a beautiful little girl called Daisy.There's just one problem. Daisy doesn't belong to the Bensons. They stole her.And now they've decided that Daisy needs a little brother or sister.D. S. Nathan Cody is about to face his darkest and most terrifying case yet . .
