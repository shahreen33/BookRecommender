Jeff Lemire and Andrea Sorrentino begin their ground-breaking saga in the fourth volume of Green Arrow.A mysterious villian called Komodo knows Oliver's secrets and uses them to rob Oliver of all his wealth and his company. Now on the run from this seemingly unstoppable force, Oliver finds himself in a mystery involving the island where he first became Green Arrow and his father! Everything will change for the Emerald Archer in this new beginning for the character.Collecting: Green Arrow 17-24
