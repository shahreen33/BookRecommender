Is a Smurf really three apples tall??Pub Facts are improbable, bizarre and yet somehow convincing claims that are often wheeled out by armchair scientists, amateur lawyers and pub historians. They'll tell you, without a shadow of a doubt, that you can get tonsillitis even if you have had your tonsils removed; that it's illegal to drive in bare feet, and that Attila the Hun died from a nosebleed. But is it fact or fabrication??The World According to the Man in the Pub 2 will help you stride up confidently through the treacherous trivia minefield, while providing definitive answers to life's most pressing concerns.
