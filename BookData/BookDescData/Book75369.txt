A life-affirming debut, perfect for fans of Rowan Coleman and Lucy Diamond. 
  
  You can lose your memory, but you never forget how to love…
  
Lana Green has a talent for pushing people away. As a writer, she’s perfectly happy to be left alone with her books. But when she meets Jack Buchanan and Nancy Ellis Hall, Lana’s solitary life will change for ever.Nancy has dementia, and social services believe this makes her vulnerable. But Lana can see the funny, brilliant woman underneath the illness.As Lana and Jack struggle to keep Nancy out of a care home, Lana starts to question everything she ever thought mattered.Because what’s the point in stories, if there is no one to share them with?
