The One Year Bible helps people read the entire Bible in one year in as little as 15 minutes a day. Each day features a reading from the Old Testament, New Testament, Psalms, and Proverbs. This electronic edition comes with navigation features that make it easy to find any reading on any day, as well as find the first chapter of any book of the Bible. This edition also features the New Living Translation second edition text. This updated (October 2008) electronic version includes reconfigured navigation for better compatibility among various devices.
