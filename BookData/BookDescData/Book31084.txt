Survival has a price that’s paid in blood… Running out of options and time, Lee joins Dominic on his dangerous mission. Little does he know she’s on a mission of her own—to seduce the cure from him at all costs. Hunted by both the living and the dead, they will face the ultimate choice between love and survival.  This 21K+ word novella is the third episode in the Heaven in Hell series and ends on a cliffhanger. Please note that you can read the first four episodes individually or all together in the boxed set.
