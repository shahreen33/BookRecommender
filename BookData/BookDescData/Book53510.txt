Meet Mai, a woman born with a flower bud growing from her belly. The rare condition, Botanical Development has followed her from childhood into adulthood. In order to rid the bud it has to bloom and be deflowered. But the bud will only bloom once Mai has found a mate. Unfortunately Mai is awkward and inexperienced when it comes to the dating world. Join this eccentric twenty-six year old on her journey to becoming deflowered in Late Bloomer.
