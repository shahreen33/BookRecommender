Come along with Little Pumpkin and his friends on a fun romp through a not-so-spooky old house to find the Halloween treats!“Presents cheerful evidence that Halloween tricks and treats—and sights and sounds—aren’t just for big kids.” —Publishers Weekly   Where are the treats in the spooky house? Could they be behind the swishy, swooshy curtains? Or on the creaky stairs? Little Pumpkin is looking everywhere, and one by one, his friends join the search—including Little Franken, Little Bat, Little Ghost, and a little black cat too. With its irresistibly cute illustrations and sweet story, this is the perfect Halloween book for younger children.
