Owly is a kind-hearted little owl who knows what it means to be human.This fifth graphic novel in the amazing all-ages Owly series collects many unpublished and out-of-print Owly tales. Tiny Tales kicks off with a brand-new Owly adventure. Included are the Owly Free Comic Book Day stories Splashin' Around, Breakin' the Ice, and Helping Hands; the very first Owly mini-comics; and rare, never before seen drawings of little Owly before he met his best friend, Wormy, and grew into the character that is loved by fans and critics alike all around the world. Other bonus material includes a Sketchbook and "How To Draw Owly" sections.Andy Runton's animated and heartwarming style makes Owly a perfect read for everyone.
