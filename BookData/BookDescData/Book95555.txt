Brings the action placed a year and a half after the events seen in the "Last Book" but also the famous gallery of characters. The main hero, Inspector Lukić, will take over the case of the mysterious disappearance of the famous writer of detective novels Jelena Jakovljević. The intrigue of the task lies not only in the fact that the door of the writer's apartment was internally locked when her disappearance was discovered, but also missing the manuscript of her new detective novel called "Find Me". During the case, Inspector Lukić will meet an irritating literary agent, a blind painter, a virtuoso locksmith, and angry publishers, and they all will give their share in expanding the investigation, and sometimes sending it on the wrong path. And to make matters more complicated, the detective must also face the dark aspirations of the National Security Agency and secret cult who seek for immortality.
