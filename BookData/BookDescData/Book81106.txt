Suzie and Mary love running Dune House, the majestic bed and breakfast by the beach. They are enjoying hosting their guests with their boisterous yellow Labrador, Pilot by their side. But when a guest who is writing a manuscript is acting suspiciously and there are some angry and mysterious trespassers on the property, they find that they have more than their fair share of work to do.  Then things get even more complicated when one of the guests at the B &amp; B is found murdered. Suzie and Mary find themselves tangled in a mysterious tale. With the suspect list getting longer, they must unravel a web of secrets and lies in order to disclose the truth.  Suzie and Mary are in a race to read between the lines and solve the mystery so the murderer can be caught, and Dune House’s doors aren’t closed forever.
