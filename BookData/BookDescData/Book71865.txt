Ulic Qel-Droma has spent a lifetime learning to control the Force. He wanted to be on the side of the good, a member of the Jedi elite. But it is not the Force one must control to become a Jedi; rather, one must learn to control oneself. Ulic Qel-Droma has tasted power, and now he cannot fight the dark hunger inside him . . . Set centuries before Luke Skywalker's birth, when the original Jedi Knightsilluminated a dark galaxy, these are the stories of some of those earliest heroes; these are the Tales of the Jedi. Written by Kevin J. Anderson, author of the Star Wars Jedi Academy novels. Don't miss this third volume of the Tales of the Jedi!
