Unlike the first eight stories in the short story collection Ghosts of the Shadow Market, this and The Lost World were not released as separate ebooks, but included in the print edition.The Jace Herondale who crossed into our world from Thule is torn between his feeling of responsibility to Ash, who is ever-increasing in power, and his desire to take back the life he lost from the Jace of our world, by force if necessary. Meanwhile, in the peaceful countryside of England, Kit Herondale struggles to adjust to a new life and a new family, and to forget Ty.
