What’s more powerful than magic?  Revenge.  King Tristan wasn’t prepared for a prisoner quite like Zakara Storm. The three beautiful fae men led her to her imprisonment and it’s hard to see who is friend and who is foe in the dangerous and destructive kingdom.  A savior is all the humans want. Freedom is what the fae seek. Survival is all Zakara knows.  She’ll survive, even if she has to watch the whole world burn to ash.  This is a reverse harem series, recommended for readers 18 and over.
