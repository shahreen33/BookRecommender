A beautiful and easy to understand guide to the Messianic Passover seder, this Haggadah (order of "service") is a must for those who desire to conduct their own Messianic Passover Seder or to gain a deeper understanding of this important event. This Messianic Passover Haggadah shows God's redemptive grace in the Passover through the death, burial, and resurrection of Yeshua, Jesus, and symbolizes God's spiritual redemption of His people.
