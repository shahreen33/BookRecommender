It sounded like all the ingredients for a bad joke: A fireman hotter than Ricky Martin, a personal trainer fitter than Michael Jordan, two recent college grads, and the heir to a Manhattan real estate fortune.But when Captain Eddie’s scuba charter runs smack dab into a tropical storm when they're over a hundred miles from Hawaii, it’s no laughing matter.When the eclectic group of men find themselves shipwrecked on desert island, the tensions will rise. And so will other things.There’s no telling where they are, or whether they’ll be found. But one thing’s for sure...No one gets off alone.
