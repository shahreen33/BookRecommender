Gumshoe is a blackly comic novella from Paul D. Brazill, author of Guns Of Brixton and Roman - Dalton - Werewolf PI.Gumshoe is the short, sharp story of Peter Ord, a divorced teacher who decides to become a private detective in fading 'one-whore-town' somewhere in the north-east of England. Dark farce and tragicomedy ensue.
