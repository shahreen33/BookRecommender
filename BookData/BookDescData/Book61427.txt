On a street that borders East Mount Projects and Garrison Heights lives two kids, David and Sam, who are born into a life of poverty and abuse, but are searching for a way out. Society tells them that they can become anything they desire but to them life is about surviving another day. Then there’s Daniel, who seems to have a loving family and perfect life but wants more. He is willing to risk it all to see what’s on the other side of his quintessential world.In this powerful and captivating story of three children trying to find their way through adulthood, witness their lives as some are manipulated and changed. Experience the affects of how one person can influence one’s destiny. It’s a thought-provoking story of love, tragedy and triumph, and one you will never forget.
