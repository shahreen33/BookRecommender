Spellcaster: The Frostgrave Magazine exists to bring new rules, scenarios, and gaming ideas for players of Frostgrave and Frostgrave: Ghost Archipelago.  This issue contains:  * Rules for Dragons in Frostgrave, including a co-operative scenario. * Traps in Ghost Archipelago, including a solo scenario. * Frostgrave Mech War, rules and a scenario for fighting with Mechs using Frostgrave rules. * The Bridges of the Mal Dreath, a scenario reprinted from Tabletop Gaming Magazine * The Mail Bag and Horizon
