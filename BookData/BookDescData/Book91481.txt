Moonlight, a handsome man, and… a dead body? Definitely not what Noelle expected when she cast off with Hunter to do a little romantic night fishing.  Hunter discovers the girl didn’t exist before moving to Keyhole Lake. A day later, he’s ordered to close the case before he even opens it.  Meanwhile, the living-impaired community pulls together to help one of their own, and Max, the talking donkey, knows more than he's telling.  Something’s fishy in Keyhole, and Noelle and crew are determined to find out what.
