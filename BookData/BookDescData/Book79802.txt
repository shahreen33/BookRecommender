Explore Paris with two lovable pups in this story of friendship and compassionHudson, an American terrier who lives in Paris, is happily going about his day when he notices he’s being followed by a tiny dachshund puppy. Quickly grasping that the puppy is lost, Hudson helps the little dog find his way home by way of a whirlwind tour of Paris with stops at Hudson’s favorite haunts. When our compassionate hero realizes the puppy is homeless, Hudson is forced to dig deeper—how is he going to solve this problem?"Absolutely delightful, especially for dog lovers and Francophiles." Kirkus Reviews
