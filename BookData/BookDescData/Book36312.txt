Magic was supposed to make stealing easier.Ever since her parents' deaths, Yeta has been stuck stealing in order to pay back their debt to the city's most dangerous crime lord. Though she clings to her dream of one day attending the prestigious Academy, the closest she's managed to get is impersonating a student and using her aptitude for ink magic to aid in her thefts.But when her ink dragon inexplicably develops a mind of its own, debt is the least of her worries. With an arrogant Academy student as her only ally, she'll have to get back what her inkling stole--or more than her future could be at stake.
