"Fowl On The Prowl: A Day With Sgt. Ducky" is about the adventures that Sgt. R. Ducky embarks on when he takes readers out on patrol. Sgt. Ducky wants to show you what it's like to work as a police officer! But Sgt. Ducky does more than patrol the streets - this special police officer is on a mission to educate children about law enforcement and the many jobs police officers do on a daily basis. Sgt. Ducky will warm the hearts and minds of children and adults alike!
