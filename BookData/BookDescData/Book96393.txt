Have you ever fantasized about being a super hero? Well Dru Dragowski does...that's all this nineteen-year-old, comic book obsessed, high school senior thinks about. She NEEDS to escape the normalcy of suburban life! Her plan? To become a real-life superhero by recreating the origins of her favorite comic characters! Hopefully then, she can become a hero not only for herself--but to protect everyone around her! If only that plan went just the slightest bit right...
