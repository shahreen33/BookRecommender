The rainforest of British Columbia holds many secrets, but none stranger thanthose of Nearby Island. After hair-raising events during a Pacific storm, Tomand Liz Austen seek answers among the island's looking trees. Alarmed by theghostly shape of the hermit Mosquito Joe, they look for shelter in a desertedschool in the rainforest. Then, in the night, Tom and Liz hear a girl's voicecrying, "Beware! Beware!"
