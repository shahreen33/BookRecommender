The last thing Jo expects when she agrees to help a friend is to be accused of murder.  When Jo runs into an old acquaintance and she offers to look into something for him she lands up right in the middle of a murder scene and she is the prime suspect.  Walt, Eddy and Samantha join forces and use their skills to try and find the real murderer and clear Jo’s name before she lands up behind bars.  The ‘Sage Gardens Sleuths’ find themselves embroiled in a hot bed of criminals. Will they find out who the murderer is before it’s too late and Jo is arrested?    This is book 9 in the Sage Gardens Cozy Mystery Series. The main mystery is solved in each book, but the series is best enjoyed when read in order.
