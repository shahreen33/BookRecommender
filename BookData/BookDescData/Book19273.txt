Mathias is the first of his kind; born with the power to reanimate a human's shell. He can bring the pure back with the gift of immortality to help fight in the war that has been raging for over 1900 years.Lara, brought over 19 years ago, has had two things on her mind. Revenge and sorrow. Time is running out as she tries to finish her quest. The reason for this is so that Mathias can take back what he has given her but the vampires will not make it easy. Rollan, the first that Mathias created, races against Lara's clock to try to stop her. She is his destined soul mate and he does not want her to leave. Only problem is, she doesn't know. Will Lara change her plans to be with Rollan? Can she let the past go and look for a future?
