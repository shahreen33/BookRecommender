Souichi's Diary of Curses is volume six in the Horror World of Junji Ito series.It was originally published in Japan in 1997.None of these stories have been officially published in English. But some have been translated by fans.The stories in this collection focus on Tsujii Souichi, a young boy who is obsessed with curses and the occult, much to the annoyance of his typical Japanese family.
