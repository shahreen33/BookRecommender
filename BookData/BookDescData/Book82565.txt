Nicole’s life is just fine. She likes being an only child, and even if her parents are divorced, well, things are fine the way they are.But Nicole’s life is about to be turned upside down. Her mother is getting remarried, to the man Nicole calls The Mouse. That’s bad enough, but when Nicole meets her future step-brother, Jonathan, that is absolutely too much. Jonathan is just Nicole’s age, and a troublemaker...It’s all too much for anyone, even calm, collected Nicole, to bear. She has no interest in being part of this new family, but no one wants to hear her feelings on the matter.Nicole comes up with a plan: run away to Key West to find her father. But things don’t turn out the way she planned, and soon it’s up to her best friends, Alicia, Quinn, and Esme, to bring Nicole home before it’s too late!
