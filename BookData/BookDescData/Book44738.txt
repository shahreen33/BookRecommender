(Winner of the British Fantasy Award)  Daniel is ten years old when his mother dies. She dies young, and with so much left to give. He does not understand. He cannot let her go.  After the funeral, his father begins talking to a large wooden box that he keeps beneath his bed. And when Daniel whispers to the box one day when his father goes out … it answers back.  It’s a voice he does not know. But this voice knows so much.
