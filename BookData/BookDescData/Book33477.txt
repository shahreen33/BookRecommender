Before you watch the upcoming Netflix series (The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance), read these original novels from J.M. Lee that tie into the events of the series.Jim Henson's The Dark Crystal: Tides of the Dark Crystal is the third book in a series of original young adult novels set in the world of Jim Henson's The Dark Crystal. As the Skeksis turn on the Gelfling, Amri must learn what it means to be a leader before the Gelfling clans--and his friends--become fragmented beyond repair.
