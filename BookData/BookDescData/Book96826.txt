It's simple. I have two goals for my freshman year of college. Goal #1 - Lose my virginity before my 20th birthday. Goal #2 - Get over my hopeless crush on my roommate.Should be a piece of cake, right? But when it comes to Tucker Caine, the possible suddenly seems impossible. 
  
  Tuck &amp; Josh is a 56,000 word MM contemporary romance. Set in the same "universe" as the Sheltered Hearts series, it has a guaranteed HEA, with no cheating or cliffhangers.
