Long ago the ruthless High Queen of the Elves conquered the nations of 21st century Earth. Centuries later, Nadia Moran is a thief and a wizard bound in service to the cruel Elven noble Morvilind, forced to use her skills and her spells to steal treasures for him.  If she does not do Morvilind's bidding, her brother will die.  But there are more dangers in the world than Morvilind.  When rebel Elves launch an assault upon the High Queen, Nadia Moran finds herself caught between the rebels and Morvilind's implacable demands.  One false step, and she will die...
