Dakota del Toro is a painter turned bounty hunter in lycanthrope infested Santa Fe, New Mexico. Don Diego de Gama, gallery owner and vampire, is staging a little undead revolution in her town. He wants her help—and her heart.When the search for a missing mafia princess leads Dakota into the mysterious world of Changelings, teenage outsiders who like to pretend they are were-creatures and play for blood, she stumbles upon a wicked witch of the Amazon and a villain from her past thought long dead. In a deadly race to save the very soul of her city Dakota has no choice but to add revolutionary to her résumé.
