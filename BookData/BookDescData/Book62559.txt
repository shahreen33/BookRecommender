A Packing Guide for the Traveler Spending a Year Abroad or the Digital Nomad Embarking on Indefinite TravelPack what you need and need what you pack. If you’re looking for advice on how to whittle down to three pairs of high heels, this is not the ebook for you. But if you want to make sure you have everything you need — and nothing more — to travel and work remotely from anywhere in the world, you’re in the right place. You’ve probably heard the advice to “pack light”. Well this book goes into the details of exactly how to do that, from someone who has been traveling nomadically for over two years without ever checking a bag.
