“LIFE IS RUNNING OUT OF TIME, AND TIME CAN DISAPPEAR IN THE BLINK OF AN EYE.”Christopher returns home and revels in the land’s new-found freedombut things are not as peaceful as they seem. For deep in the south, the sorceress Athena is conjuring plans to resurrect her dark Lord. As her newly created creatures terrorize the night, the wizards of Canistar are alerted to her devious scheme.In the battle to oust the dark Lord from their lands, the wizards and their army find themselves out numbered.Will Christopher make a mistake that will bring about the evil Lords destruction of the realm?Resurrection of an Empire is a story of the never-ending battle between good and evil. A tale of wizardly love, war and intrigue, steeped in a mystical age of magic.
