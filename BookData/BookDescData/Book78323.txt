Ami escaped the devils' torture, but her body and mind are far from healed. She fears what she was forced to reveal when the mind-mage invaded her thoughts.  A dark protector, exotic and powerful, arrives from the west. Seònaid knows from the first time she meets Ami that the two are destined for each other. But Seònaid harbors a secret that threatens to destroy the budding relationship between the two women.  When the secret is revealed, Ami is shattered. Will she overcome Seònaid's betrayal? Can she ever trust her again? _______________ Note: Destined is a novella of approximately 45,000 words. It takes place immediately after the events in By the Fates, Freed. It contains mature themes including short descriptions of torture and abuse as well as a tender love story between a human woman and a witch.
