MEETING THE ICE PRINCESSHe'd danced with her on a dare. But Samuel Baldwin had seduced Princess Anne to quench his own desire. Chipping away at Anne's icy demeanor had been pure pleasure ... until he discovered their passion-filled night had left him responsible for one pregnant princess.He was set to be the country's next prime minister, but marrying a royal would mean an end to his career. Duty to the mother of his child was etched deep in Sam's bones --- their wedding would take place. But after making such a sacrifice, would this groom's heart ever thaw?
