High-school student Nate Turney only cared about playing video games, lording over the skate park, and making his girlfriend, Janis, laugh.  Now, he and his two best friends find themselves in the crosshairs of her killer. Blamed for the murder of the very person he wanted to make happy, Nate is forced to run for his life while wrestling with his own feelings of guilt.  Hunted on all sides, he and his friends race to survive and uncover the truth behind Janis' death, but the answer to all their questions may be bigger than any one of them can handle alone.
