A gift for every single reader who said they needed to know what happened next for Jack and Riley…When Hayley arrived on the steps of the D, Riley and Jack knew life would never be the same.Told through Riley and Jack’s eyes, this is ten years in the life of their family and watching Hayley grow up, fall in love, and start her own life. Hurricanes, illness, babies, happiness, sadness, work, play, the barn, the office, horses, friends, enemies, and above all, love.
