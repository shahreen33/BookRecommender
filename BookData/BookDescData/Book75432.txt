DEAD HOPE is the new thriller by Nicky Wells, bringing you a delicious cocktail of crime, suspense, and romance:Cat Hope doesn’t want to go to prison. She needs a job, and she needs it fast: judge’s orders.Kay Mahon, office worker by day and hacker by night, is on the run from a past life that he’d rather not remember.When their paths cross, they begin to investigate the truth behind the deaths of Cat’s parents, the successful rock star couple Jackie and Adam Hope. Little do they know that their quest is putting Cat in grave danger.
