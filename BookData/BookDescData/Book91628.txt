Keith DeLong and his polio-stricken best friend, Flea, overcame individual hurdles in the first novel, ‘If Bullfrogs Had Wings’. This sequel, set two years later, contains another heart-warming story of striving against the odds to get: ‘Out of the Rough’. Family, friendship, and determination can bring individuals out of the darkness to grasp the rainbow of their dreams.Keith watches his dreams smashed by a series of events, out of his control.
