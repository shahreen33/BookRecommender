Delilah Howell grew up in a small country town but she’d always dreamed of going to the city that never sleeps. Even though she knew her parents wouldn’t approve of it, she’d made herself a promise that one day she’d go. There was something she had to find.Drake Weston is used to getting what he wants. The problem is he hasn’t wanted for anything in a long time until a sweet country girl comes falling right into his lap. Except, she’s the daughter of the man he holds in the highest regard. Drake promised to keep her safe.
