Pleasant Hill looks like the perfect small-town community - but underneath the surface, a deadly secret lurks. One so explosive it will draw estranged allies Steve Rogers and Sam Wilson back together to save a friend in danger and put things right. But when the combined might of the Captain Americas isn't enough, it's time for the Avengers to Assemble like never before!
