The Elements of Style (1918), also known as Strunk &amp; White, by William Strunk, Jr. This book is a prescriptive American English writing style guide comprising eight "elementary rules of usage", ten "elementary principles of composition", "a few matters of form", a list of forty-nine "words and expressions commonly misused", and a list of fifty-seven "words often misspelled".+Active table of contents+Biography of William Strunk
