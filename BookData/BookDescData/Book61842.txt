Outgoing and ambitious, Paige Jordan has firm ideas about what success looks like. That’s the reason she stays in medical school despite not having the slightest bit of passion for her chosen career. She can’t admit, even to herself, that becoming a doctor might be all wrong for her.  And speaking of all wrong for her…  Rock star Jared Sloane is a temptation she can’t quite resist. But he’s her dirty little secret. Even her closest friends don’t know she’s hooking up with him every time he hits town. The passion she finds with him almost makes up for what’s missing in the rest of her life. But when Jared suggests she spend her spring break on tour with his band, Paige might find it’s more than just passion she can’t live without…
