Is the grass really greener on the other side?Holly Horse thinks it is and she is setting out on a journey to find it.In this exciting kids book, Holly Horse meets many new animal friends on her quest for the greenest, most delectable grass. The story includes a beautiful message for both young children and adults alike: Be grateful for what we have right now! The end is so endearing that if you are an adult, it may even bring a happy tear to your eye.Holly Horse and the Great Quest is a beautiful story that can be enjoyed by younger children as a bedtime story, and as a self read story for your older child as well.
