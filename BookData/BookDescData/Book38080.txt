Nathaniel Brill spent most of his life surrounded by the safety of his parents farm. When the offer of a job aboard a luxury ocean liner came his way, he jumped at the chance. But as his year long contract with the orchestra draws to a close, he's finding that his job isn't the adventure he thought it would be.The Majestic is approaching London and Nathaniel is looking forward to a few days on solid ground to figure out his next steps. He just has to make it through his last big performance and everything should work out fine. Right? Wrong.A mysterious stranger, drunken band mates, and a run-in with airship pirates bring him way more excitement than he could have ever imagined.
