On a relaxed holiday picnic, 9 yr. old Krithi, her pet dog Sushi and their neighbour's dog Trixie, stumble upon something unreal and get pulled into a world of super heroes and adventure. How they along with their friends adjust into this new world and create an ecosystem of super heroes to tackle problems around the world, forms the crux of the story. The brave heroes help share a message that, one is never too small to make a difference and if we all stay united we can fight anything.Is this all for real or just a dream? Join Krithi, Sushi and Trixie on this joy ride, where every page will surely take you by surprise and excite you.
