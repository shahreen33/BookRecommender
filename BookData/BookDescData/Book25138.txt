FROM THE CREATOR OF BUNNY DROP COMES AN ALL NEW MODERN DAY FABLE!High school student Yaneko loves sleep so much she can barely wake up of her own accord–until she’s roused by a sleep spirit known as Nerimu. Yaneko must accompany him as he heads off to work, granting the gift of sleep to people (and animals!) of all ages. But what is his true purpose? And why has he chosen Yaneko to be his assistant? This could very well be the beginning of a beautiful friendship!
