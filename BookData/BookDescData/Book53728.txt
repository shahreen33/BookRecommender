A modern way of finding love. One deserted island of luxury. Two people. Three out cards. And a whole lot of possibilities.Melinda takes the advice of her best friend, Chris. She signs up to a new dating service that pairs you statistically to the one you’re supposed to love. Finding it — love that is — using the more conventional methods, has not been a successful venture for Mindy, and with her biological clock ticking, she uses a more desperate method and takes the plunge into a possible happily ever after.The thing is, when she boards the boat to a secret location, she doesn’t realise the man waiting for her is someone she’s previously met.Chance encounters occur more frequently than we realise. Being blind to them is part of a bustling world. But, being faced with them can lead to a hilarious three weeks between two opposites who might just attract.
