Alternate cover edition for B077J14PWKBook 1.5​​​​ The members of the Seaside Wolf Pack are investigating a murder of a human on their territory when they start finding evidence of a female wolf that may have been involved. Females are rare and it is unheard of to find a female who has managed to survive as a lone wolf. But that is exactly what they find when they meet Anna.  This is a collection of POVs that follows Austin, James, Cody, Mason, Jason, and Caleb as they search for the mysterious female on their territory and meet Anna.  This is a 38,000 word novella.
