An in-depth review of the Core Worlds region of the Star Wars galaxy, with emphasis on the capital world, Coruscant.This title takes a detailed look at more than 25 worlds in the Core Worlds region, otherwise known as the hub of the civilized Star Wars galaxy. Worlds both familiar and new will be described, with extensive coverage given to the crux world of Coruscant. Each entry will be rich with detail, including four-color presentation throughout and maps and digital images of each world. For ease of use, character information and gamemaster information are divided into separate sections.
