"She loves me... she loves me not."Ally and Lisa's storybook BDSM romance is at an end, the two women too stubborn to admit the depth of their feelings. But even as they go their separate ways, Lisa returns to the writing that brought her and Ally together in the first place. With a little help from their friends, and a lot of mutual self-reflection, these allegorical love stories represent one last chance to salvage Ally and Lisa's relationship.From New York Times bestselling writer/artist Stjepan Šejić (Switch, Death Vigil) comes this fifth installment in the saga of love, bad jokes, and, of course, bondage.
