Ruth Everett was an average woman living in an apartment in Houston, Texas, when news leaked that an asteroid, was hurtling towards Earth. There would be few survivors— if any.Ruth voluntarily finds herself trapped in a epic fantasy based VRMMORPG, Panguardia Online, fighting the same evils she faced on Earth, and quickly uncovers a conspiracy theory to take over the world. If she doesn’t stop the evil at work, who will?
