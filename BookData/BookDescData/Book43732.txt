CASTAWAY meets MISERY - with a dash of THE MAGUS - in this scintillating thriller.On a remote island in the Adriatic, the playboy son of a president hosts a spectacular entertainment to satisfy the jaded appetites of the super rich. And for one young family, the holiday of a lifetime is about to become a desperate battle for survival.As teenage parents Sam and Jody managed to defy the odds once before, striving to raise their children in a settled home. But the years of struggle have taken their toll, and Sam's worst demons return to haunt him at the worst possible time. Can he and Jody put their differences aside and work under intolerable pressure to save the lives of their children?
