
  To Love Her Could Destroy Them Both…
For centuries, Roland de Courtemanche had spurned Rhiannon, banishing her to eternal darkness, eternal loneliness. But now the man she loved and the boy in his care are in deadly danger. She has no choice but to risk her heart and warn him.It takes all Roland’s strength to resist Rhiannon, but he has no choice if he is to protect both himself and the boy he had promised to protect. If he succumbs to his desire and then casts her away, he might as well drive a stake through her heart…exactly what the avenging mortal on their trail has in mind for them all.Book 2 in Maggie Shayne’s Bestselling Wings in the Night Series
