Selena Foster has slain both monsters and men alike, but will she come out on top when the only opponent left standing is herself?  After months of training to hone her ability and self-control, Selena’s life is thrown into disarray when a war she never expected looms on the horizon. Blackmailed into servitude by the Council, her mind falls deeper into darkness than ever before, and this time—Lucas isn’t there to pull her out.  Old foes and new faces surround her at every corner, but Selena knows better than anyone that even family can turn on you when mind games are at play. Selena must learn to navigate the Supernatural waters, and fast, before her ignorance spells death for them all.
