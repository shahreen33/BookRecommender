Available digitally for the first time.   The first Calhoun romance from “America’s favorite writer” (The New Yorker).   Catherine “C.C.” Calhoun isn’t about to let anyone take away the Towers—her family’s crumbling mansion—especially not handsome hotel magnate Trenton St. James. As far as Trent is concerned, his dealings with C.C. should be strictly business. But soon, C.C.’s mix of fire and fury seduce his mind and his heart, leading them both in a completely unexpected direction.   Includes a teaser for
