Meet Ermine. She may be small but she’s on a BIG journey around the world.Ermine the Determined is off to explore NEW YORK.She can’t wait to visit Central Park Zoo, ride in a yellow taxi, and zoooom to the top of the Rockefeller building!But when her suitcase is switched, Ermine finds some robbers are hot on her tail…Ermine: one fabulously funny and furry heroine who can’t help but find adventure wherever she goes. First in a new series from best-selling and award-winning Jennifer Gray, with gorgeous black-and-white illustrations from Elisa Paganelli throughout.
