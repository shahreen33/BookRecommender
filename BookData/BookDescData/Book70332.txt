 A formidable force was making its way to Caesars Creek, and one person wouldn’t make it out alive.   When Stormi’s mother arrives in town to help with her daughter’s wedding, the entire community shudders. But she hasn’t come alone. Find out what happens when wedding planning runs amok and a certain person succumbs to a pointy end.  This is the 7th book in the Caesars Creek Mystery series, but it can be read as a standalone.
