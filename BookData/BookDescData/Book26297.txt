Alison Anders is a nobody.Alone, awkward and plain, she goes through life under the radar. Until the day comes when she can’t take it anymore.When she hears about the dark and enticing Underground, she gathers up the courage to go. The Underground is an illegal cage fighting league that’s full of primal violence, debauchery and passion. Where sex and pain are delivered by the alpha male fighters who dominate in the ring.When Alison steps through those doors, she can be anyone she wants. Anyone but the nobody she is when the sun comes up.What does she have to lose? Just her heart, body and soul…
