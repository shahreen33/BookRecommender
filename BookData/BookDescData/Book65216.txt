Schrödinger’s God is a tense psychological thriller and the fourth novel in the Dr Power and Superintendent Lynch series. The novel is set in Northern Spain and leafy Cheshire.  A peaceful holiday walking St James's Way to Santiago del Compostela takes a tragic turn leaving Power and Lynch trying to solve two shocking murders, while unraveling secrets and a conspiracy that spans the Continent.  Schrödinger’s God follows The Darkening Sky, The Fire of Love and The Good Shepherd.  www.hughgreene.com Illustrations by Paul Imrie. Eduted by Judith Eddles
