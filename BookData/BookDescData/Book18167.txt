The Rebel: A Short Story Prequel to The Highlander Trilogy by Julianne MacLeanThe year is 1715 and the Jacobite Rebellion continues in the Scottish Highlands.He is Alexander MacLean, a fearsome Highland warrior fighting for his true Scottish King. She is Elizabeth Curtis, a beautiful Englishwoman who wears the scarlet uniform of an English soldier and wields a sword like a seasoned fighter on the battlefield.Enemies meet, and passions collide...in THE REBELThe Rebel is a short story of 7000 words (approximately 28 pages).
