CRUELTY is a serial novel in ten parts.Episode Seven.Cruelty is changing…Sheriff Randy Miser and Trooper Natalie Holden’s seemingly everlasting night continues as they are called to yet another crime scene. What they find in Monlezun Estates will send them careening into the unknown.All the while, Nell Morgan is making a deal with a devil.Will Special Investigator Frank Markum find his lover before Regret comes calling? Two worlds are about to collide…
