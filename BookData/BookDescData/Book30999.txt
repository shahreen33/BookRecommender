Joshua is convinced there is a family curse. It has taken loved ones from him, it has robbed him of his eyesight, and it is the reason why his father was killed while investigating the homicide of a young woman. But after his father's death, Joshua is handed an opportunity he can't refuse: an operation that will allow him not only to see the world but to see the world through his father's eyes. As Joshua navigates that world of sight, he gets glimpses of what his father's eyes might have witnessed in their previous life. And whatever it was, there seem to be consequences-including the wrath of a man who is drawing closer and closer to Joshua and who seems hell bent on killing...
