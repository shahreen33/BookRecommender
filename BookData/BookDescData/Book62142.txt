New York Times bestselling author Alyssa Day introduces the League of the Black Swan--and the dangerous game one woman plays when her family's curse dooms her to kill the man she loves--in this novella from the Enthralled anthology.A thousand years ago, Brynn Caroll's ancestor was cursed: every third night the eldest daughter of each generation is transformed into a black swan and forced to sing songs to the moon. The curse is transferred when she meets the love of her life and bears him a daughter to carry on the legacy. Intent on ending the curse once and for all, Brynn refuses to fall in love. But she never anticipated meeting Sean O'Malley--a man who is also burdened by a difficult secret--and facing temptation like never before.
