VP of Finance, Jennifer Hayes, returns to Seattle from a five day conference, ready to enter her office like a triumphant empress returning to her kingdom. Instead the day turns into one of the worst in her life---ever.Jennifer has gone from a well-paid female executive one minute to a desperate woman the next. Thinking her day can't possibly get any worse, she finds out how wrong she is when she goes home.The only way to set things straight is to get even, and she uses everything in her power to do just that.
