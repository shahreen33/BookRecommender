David Goodis. Night Squad. New York: Gold Medal Books/Fawcett Publications, [1961]. First edition, first printing (February 1961). Mass market paperback original, Gold Medal Book 1083. 191 pages.When a cop goes bad, he can always become a crook.  When a crook goes bad--that's when the Night Squad wants him.David Goodis's irresistibly readable study of corruption is a masterly portrait of a man clawing his way back from betrayal--and betraying countless others along the way.
