When Emma moves to Astoria, Oregon, she is given two rules:#1 – Don’t cross to “their” side of the street.#2 – Don’t mess with the elites, especially Lexen Darken, top elite and resident bad boy.While she finds this both weird and insulting, Emma has no actual intention of breaking these rules - until her guardians go missing. Now all bets are off. Only a single line separates her side of the street from the elites', and she's crossing it.Captured and held by Lexen and his brothers, she learns the rules are in place for a very good reason. There's a secret world hidden in this small town which goes far beyond the one she knows and understands.A world not for humans.** This is a full length (105,000 words) fantasy/paranormal romance story. It's Upper YA, with cursing and some non-detailed sex scenes/references.
