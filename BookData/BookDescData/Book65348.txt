Read by Dylan BakerApprox. 2 hours2 cassettes From the best-selling author of "Because of Winn-Dixie" comes the moving story of an eleven-year-old-boy, Rob Horton, who finds a caged tiger in the woods behind the hotel where he lives with his father. With the help of his new friend, Sistine Bailey, Rob must decide what to do with his discovery and at the same time come to terms with his past.
