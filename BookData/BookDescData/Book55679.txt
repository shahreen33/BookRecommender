What do monsters do? Mostly nasty, creepy and bloody things, sometimes they laugh and sometimes, all too often, we do things just as they do.Remember, it could be a monster sitting opposite you on the bus; you can never tell, just by looking.A monster may be the next person to place their hand on your shoulder.Tomorrow, a monster might look at you from your mirror.Nicholas Vince is known for playing the Chattering Cenobite in Hellraiser and Hellbound: Hellraiser II. He also played the crescent moon faced Kinski in Nighbreed.This volume contains six new stories of psychological horror, plus the story 'The Beast in Beauty', originally published in Fear magazine.
