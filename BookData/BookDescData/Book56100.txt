IT KILLS BUT IT CANNOT DIEIt begins as it will end, in the woods.THE WOODS - where twenty years ago, Sarah's brothers and sisters were horribly slaughtered. Only she and her brother escaped with their lives.THE WOODS - where now it is beginning again. Once again Sarah, her brother and her young son Jeremy confront the power of evil and witness the aftermath of more dreadful deaths.THE WOODS - where something inhuman has been waiting all these years for this very moment, waiting for them to return. Now it emerges with lustful vengeance to stalk them, trap them, to fulfill its hideous quest for blood...EVIL STALKS THE NIGHT
