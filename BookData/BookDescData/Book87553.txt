Billy loves Monster and Monster loves Billy. They play together. They have a bath together.They even go to school together. There's just one thing that threatens to spoil their friendship.Monster loves to Fart...and everyone blames Billy for it!Will Monster make Billy the un-coolest kid at school? Will Monster cause an irreparable hole in the Ozone layer? Will Monster and Billy both learn how to behave properly in a social environment?This book deals with friendship, doing things in moderation and behaving properly in social situations. This funny book for kids of all ages will be a delightful addition to your library.
