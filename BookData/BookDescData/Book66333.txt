Rhett had a huge crush on Darren McKissick back in elementary school and on Valentine's Day, he gave him a special valentine. Despite their obvious attraction, Darren rejected it, making everyone in the class laugh at Rhett and cause a rift between the two friends. Now as adults the two of them have found one another again through an internet dating site. Rhett only wants a one-nighter but Darren is looking for something long lasting. Will this new meeting mean love for two men who haven't seen one another in over a decade?
