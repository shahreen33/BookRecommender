Each year the L. Ron Hubbard Presents Writers of the Future anthology series expands minds and enlightens those who love to read. From cutting-edge high-tech to evocative fantasy, these volumes provide the very best from the new generation of science fiction and fantasy authors.The winners are selected by a blue-ribbon panel of professionals, including grandmasters Frederik Pohl and Jack Williamson, and award winners such as Anne McCaffrey, Larry Niven, Jerry Pournelle and Tim Powers.
