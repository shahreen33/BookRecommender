A shattered woman.A changed man.Church Logan wanted to kill someone, and Emma Hartley wanted to die - they were a match made in hell.But one little cut, a pint or two of blood, and suddenly everything between them is different.Are they still destined for greatness? Or has the darkness finally eclipsed them and torn them apart?Looks like it's time for Church to practice what he preaches.Ignition, meet explosion.
  warning: if you have triggers - this story will touch on a lot of them. This book is a dark romance, 85,000+ words.
