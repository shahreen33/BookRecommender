
  They say time flies when you’re having fun, but what about when you’re falling in love?
Bianca Evers is recovering from the end of her marriage to her college sweetheart when she’s seated next to the sexiest man she’s ever seen. Lucas Hawke is flying home to Australia, still dreaming of something more with the right person when he encounters a beautiful woman with sad eyes. 
  And now they only have fifteen hours to figure out if their connection is worth upgrading—or just a layover in life . . .
