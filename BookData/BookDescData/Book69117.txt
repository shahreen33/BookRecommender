Hiding out in another country, Hope believes she's finally escaped The Organization. Maybe this time she can finally start over, leave her violent past behind. But unbeknownst to her, someone close to her has a connection to her past and he won't stop until she pays for what she's done. Now there's nowhere to hide and no one to help her. Or is there? Furious an amateur has escaped them not once but twice, the Organization sends one of their best to finish her off once and for all. There’s nowhere to hide, she’s out of options, and in a shocking twist, Hope finds out information that changes everything. Can she escape one last time or is this the end? Don't miss the thrilling conclusion to the trilogy!
