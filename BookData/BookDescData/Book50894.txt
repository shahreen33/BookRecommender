Zach Samuels is a devoted father, musician, teacher, and counselor who simply cannot put the pipe down. One day worth half a million dollars, the next day he's picking up butts off the ground, homeless. Zach tells of his trials and tribulations in his addiction and his fight for life and recovery with the help of his sponsor Bob and his family's support. Through it all Zach maintains his love and dedication to his daughter Paige and desperately prays for her to one day understand. This is a riveting tale of strength and hope you do not want to miss."Confessions of a Crack Head" is written anonymously to abide by the principles of the program Samuels practices and to avoid causing further shame or embarrassment to his friends and family.
