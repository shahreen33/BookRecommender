A special book turns up on the doorstep of bookstore clerk Sue Talbot. With the book come dreams that Sue can't explain, but reach deep into her heart and soul. Dylan Hunter is a werewolf on the trail of a certain book. When he realizes who has possession of it, Dylan knows he can't use force to get it back. Invading Sue's dreams is the surest way of revealing her legacy.Paranormal erotic romance short story, approx. 4,500 words or 15 pages.
