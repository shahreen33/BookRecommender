In a world of glamour, lies, and betrayal threatens to tear them apart...Mable’s life as a model may be over after her agent drops her, but a chance encounter with a fashion photographer, Simon Rowe, revives her dream—and awakens desires she never imagined. Mable soon discovers there’s more to this sexy motorcyclist from down under. As her relationship with Simon flourishes so does her modeling career. Suddenly, Mable struggles to find a balance in her life in a world that keeps on trying to tear her down, forcing her to choose between the life of fame she always wanted and the man that, until now, she never knew she needed. In a world that’s a stage, can their love thrive under the lights?Lyrical Lights is a charming, modern love story full of free-spirited characters and a story that’ll hook you from the very first page.
