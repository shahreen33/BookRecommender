The world’s first international moon base has been established by MoonX Ltd., a joint venture company with American, Chinese, and Russian government shareholders to promote the peaceful use and development of the moon for all. However, political tensions arise and raise the specter of total annihilation.  A young nuclear physicist, Rex, works on the base to create nuclear fusion using helium-3, but personal tragedy draws his mind away from his work. His fiancée, Avery, the captain of the first mission to Mars launched from the moon, has gone missing.  With the help of his AI computer companion, Rex searches for answers to the cause of the loss of the Mars bound ship, but he discovers military secrets instead.   In outer space, there is nowhere to run.
