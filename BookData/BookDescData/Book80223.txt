I know the truth now. I know who I am—what I am. Now, it’s not just me that needs protection. It’s all my people. But I can’t stay in this bubble of safety. I have to fight. It’s my job—my duty as their leader. I never thought they would be my biggest opposers. How do I convince them that we’re not dangerous?  Hybrid is the third and FINAL book in the Shifter Academy Series, an RH series. Book 1: Different Book 2: Monster Book 3: Hybrid
