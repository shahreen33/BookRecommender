THE SOMEDAY GIRL is the 95,000 word follow up to THE MONDAY GIRL. It concludes the angsty, addictive storyline of THE GIRL duet.    I fell in love with a man who could not love me back. A man I will never deserve fell in love with me.   Katherine Firestone has finally made it  in Hollywood. New house. New car. New closet full of designer clothes. She's got it all. And yet... without him.... she has absolutely nothing.   THE SOMEDAY GIRL arrives February 1, 2017. It is recommended for readers ages 17+ due to mature content.
