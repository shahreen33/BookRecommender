Assigned a new area from Hell, Arkady Dhark is on a mission to sign up as many souls as possible to serve Hell.Until he sees her....Always given the short end of every stick on the planet, Brynn doesn't understand when kindness is shown to her. However, one thing she does know is that she will never forget the massive and kind man that walked into her life.Can a demon and a human find their happily ever after? Or will Arkady's service to Hell ruin it all?
