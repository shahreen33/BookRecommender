Most of the world’s population has died in a mysterious plague. Those who survived did what they needed to in order to see tomorrow’s dawn.  Bill Arthur has grand vision: to regain everything that was lost and make the world a better place. Settling in the little town of Rocheport Missouri, he sets out to make his dream reality.  But dreams don’t always come true. Bill finds himself in constant conflict not only with those in his own group who have a different vision of the future, but also with sinister outside forces which threaten the tiny community’s very survival.  Love Is Little continues the story of Bill Arthur and his band of survivors, following their attempts to regain technology and build a better world in the face of insurmountable obstacles and innumerable foes.
