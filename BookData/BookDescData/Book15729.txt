5 hr. Energy Shots, Bravo! TV marathons, and Starbucks keep Penny sane. Until they don’t. Even paddleboards, bonfires & bikinis won’t help Penny escape her reoccurring nightmare.When Penny meets Delsin everything changes. Each morning she comes to meet him, handing over pieces of her heart. But when Penny is confronted with her worst nightmare, Delsin asks for a sacrifice she didn’t see coming.
