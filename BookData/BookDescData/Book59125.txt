About This Book From April 2008 to June 2009 and from April 2014 to November 2015, rapper Tablo hosted his own radio show, "Dreaming with Tablo", on MBS FM4U. Every day, Tablo shared short messages with his listeners. The topics of the messages covered everything from family, friends, hectic daily routines, relationships, and much more. The book is filled with personal messages handwritten by famous musicians, movie directors, comedians, models, designers, and DJs among many others. This edition includes the original Korean version, the English version (personally translated by Tablo), and a CD of ‘Blo Radio.
