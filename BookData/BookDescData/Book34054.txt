This novella contains sexual descriptions. It is intended for adult readers. Tracie is a country girl, and the youngest wife in her community. She escaped the farm to marry an older man, but her man is in a hospital bed and she's suddenly fair game for every male in the neighborhood. Tracie wants to resist but her short marriage has awakened deep, hungry fires in her body. She is desperate to quench them, but she knows that once she gives into the first man, she won't be able to resist the others.
