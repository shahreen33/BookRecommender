The perfect family. A moment that will change everything.Emma thought she had the perfect life: a beautiful home, a loving husband and a gorgeous son.She was wrong.She wakes up in hospital, with no idea how she got there or why her husband and son won’t come to see her. What happened to Emma’s family?As Emma tries to piece her memories back together, she remembers that her husband was hiding something from her, and that someone was watching their house.She remembers that she was afraid.Emma is desperate to find out what happened – and that her loved ones are safe – but remembering the truth could be the most dangerous thing of all…An addictive and page-turning psychological thriller that will having you looking over your shoulder and checking the doors are locked. If you love B.A. Paris, Shari Lapena and K.L. Slater, The Last Thing I Saw is for you.
