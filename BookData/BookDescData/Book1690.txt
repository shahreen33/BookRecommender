Eager, naive Heidi starts a job with one of the largest fashion companies in the world, but it isn't as exciting as she'd hoped. A wanna-be designer, she finds herself nothing more than a glorified go-fer. Then, she accidentally stains CEO Warren Kaiser's pants and she gets two things she didn't expect--a spanking... and a new position.Kaiser hires her on as his assistant, and her "training" proves to be quite a test of surrender.
