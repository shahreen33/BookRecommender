Just as today's world is on the brink of all-out nuclear war, wizards appear out of nowhere, seizing control of every continent. They install themselves as benevolent tyrants, vowing utopia for all.The four wizards—Indie, Demus, Regi, and Justica—seat themselves as divine rulers of North America. Amanda Fullteron, who is chosen as Supreme Liaison from a shell-shocked human race, must seek the wizards’ secret and soon, bring about their downfall. But as she races against time, the wizards are ready to unleash the Prophecy, a 1,000-year oracle that promises them eternal glory.
