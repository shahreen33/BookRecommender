TEEC It To The Top profiles a teacher's journey in developing a friendly approach to reading comprehension and writing processes. The book takes the reader through the process of introducing the TEEC system into a classroom, making it a key component of the students' day, and then getting the support of other teachers to create a school-wide continuum. The author has used the steps and templates included in the book to help several schools improve students' written communication and standardized test scores. The French Immersion Edition contains information in English to guide teachers through the process of implementing the TEEC system. It includes all templates in French for use with students in a French immersion classroom.
