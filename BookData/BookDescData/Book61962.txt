This is a collection of crucial practices that you can use to help others and yourself. The most important function that the main four offering practices in theis volume serve, in general, is as potent methods to accumulate both merit and wisdom. Three of these were mind treasure revelations of the enlightened meditation master Trakthung Dudjom Lingpa from the sky-like expanse of the fundamental nature of reality (Cho Nyid Namkha Long). The final practice, the concise body offering, dawned for him in pure vision. We have also included two targeted purification opractices for places and for people, the nol sang and the drib sang.
