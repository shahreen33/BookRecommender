Six years after her epic trip through central and eastern Europe Deborah is off again on her travels by train. From Venice to Rome, from adoration of saints to sinful thoughts, Deborah dips into Italy's history and looks to her own future.A captivating, often hilarious account of her travels, Deborah belnds an entertaining mix of facts with the everyday tribulations of life. The result - a superb cocktail that everysone should taste.
