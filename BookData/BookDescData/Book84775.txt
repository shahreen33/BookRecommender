The world today seems more complex than ever. Change is happening faster than ever and the pace is accelerating.The lifespan of companies decreasing and the list of case studies of those that failed to adapt grows longer by the week. Life seems more unpredictable than ever, we face a growing abundance of data to process, more decisions to make and new technologies that promise to be the next big thing. In this book Tom Goodwin aims to provide CEO's and Business Executives across all industries with leadership through change. A guiding hand through through the turbulence of the moment, a way to reimagine business and products leveraging the profound new opportunities that new technology, new behaviors and new thinking allow. This is a practical guide, drawing on the past, with clear actionable steps on how leaders for the future can thrive today and tomorrow.
