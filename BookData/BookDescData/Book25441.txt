Professor Luci Ames doesn't know what to do about her husband, Dalton, who's become obsessed with the need for a child. As pent-up pressures and desires enter the marriage, everything solid around her dramatically shifts and Luci finds herself turning more and more into someone she's not. The charmed neighborhood oasis of CafE Antoinette offers Luci relief, but also ignites a passion inside her. In this story of husbands and wives, neighbors and lovers, fantasies come at a greater cost than Luci can ever imagine. And any choice she makes is bound to be a game-changer.
