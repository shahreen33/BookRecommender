Nothing Lasting by Glen Krisch Twenty-five years ago, Noah Berkley's childhood was stolen from him. Twenty-five years ago, he lost the first and only love of his life. Twenty-five years ago, someone died at his hand. Only now, after all these years and spurred by the death of his father, does Noah Berkley believe he can face the memories he buried in the winter of 1984. But sometimes memories aren't the old things we recover when we reopen the wounds of the past...
