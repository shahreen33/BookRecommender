Broken Faces is the story of Freddie Chevalier, a Jerseyman who suffers a life-changing disfigurement in the Great War, his betrayal of his closest friend, and his unrequited love for the woman who nurses him. 'One to Watch, in Deborah Carr's Downton-esque tale, Broken Faces, a soldier suffers a life-changing injury in the Great War'. GOOD HOUSEKEEPING MAGAZINE
