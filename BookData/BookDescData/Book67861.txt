Jack knows what he wants: Channon, conspicuously his in a way that no-one around them can possibly mistake. But he also wants Channon to choose this for himself, and the more leeway he gives him the more likely it seems that Channon is going to slip out of his grasp. Jack doesn't want that. Will giving Channon greater freedom mean Jack will lose him? And why does does he hate the idea of that so much?
