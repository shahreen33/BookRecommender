Clinging of all its forces to the least glimmer of hope, it conceives a plan with the assistance of its personal doctor, Doctor Murakami. Little time afterwards, it gives birth to a girl. Then it disappears abruptly, and nobody hears any more of it. The seasons pass... Some share in a city, a mother and her daughter live together. It is about Izumi, the face devoured by a pushing back scab, and of its daughter, resplendent Sakura. To find lost youth and beauty, Izumi is ready with all, including fomenting most Machiavellian of the projects...
