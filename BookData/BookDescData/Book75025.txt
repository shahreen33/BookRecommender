USA Today Bestselling Author Geri Glenn returns with an unforgettable story of hardship, strength, determination and love in the most unlikely of places.A rich girl trying to save the world. A hood rat living by a moral code of his own creation. Georgia knew the south side was going to be rough, but she never dreamed she’d come up against anyone quite like Tripp. When Tripp’s kid brother gets caught up with a local street gang, the two of them have no choice but to set aside their differences and work together. You’ve read the novella in the Wanted Anthology, but Hood Rat is back and better than ever. Now a full length novel! Prepare yourself to fall in love with Tripp and Georgia all over again.
