We had one night together. One unforgettable night. But that was over two years ago and we haven't spoken or seen each other since.Cole Taylor is a part of my past. A part that I desperately want to move on from. To forget how he made me feel. To forget the way he touched me. To forget his voice. It's easy to do that when he's not around.Only he hasn't stayed gone like I hoped he would. He's back and I can't seem to keep my heart from pounding when I see him. I can't seem to convince myself that he means nothing to me.That he's not part of my future.*Part Of My Future is Raegan's story from Demons From My Past.
