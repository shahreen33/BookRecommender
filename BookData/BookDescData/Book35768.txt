 A new novel from the bestselling author of Life After Life During WWII, Juliet Armstrong was conscripted into service as a young woman, transcribing conversations between an MI5 agent and a ring of suspected German sympathizers. Years later, in 1950 post-war London, Julie can't escape the repercussions of her work for the government, and is pulled back into the life of espionage she thought she'd left behind.Kate Atkinson's latest novel brings mid-century London to life in a gripping tale of deception and consequences.
