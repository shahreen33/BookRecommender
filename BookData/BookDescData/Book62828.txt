Straighten up, pull it around, and get back on the track. Declan Reede's life is in a tail-spin. Although he knows what he wants, he can't see the road that will take him there. Wherever he turns, new secrets, lies, and half-truths threaten to send him crashing off-course. To deserve his family, he must fight his past mistakes and prove himself worthy. But can someone out to deceive cost him the life he now craves? **Due to strong language and sexual content not intended for anyone under age of 18**
