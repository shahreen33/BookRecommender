CRUELTY is a serial novel in ten parts. Episode One.On a lonely stretch of deserted Texas highway, Will Longmire breaks down. But he’s not alone.In the dead of night, Innis Blake hits someone, or something, with her car. The figure shouldn’t be getting up. But it is.An unstoppable force now stalks Will and Innis. And before the night is over, both strangers will know the face of Cruelty.Forgiveness is only a few miles down the road, but safety is nowhere in sight.
