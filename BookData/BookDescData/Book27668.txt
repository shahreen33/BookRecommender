A new trilogy of horror tales from the award-winning writer's second collection – HASTY FOR THE DARK – featuring more of the nightmarish visions and ghastly creatures that have been disturbing readers for years. In this book you'll find three stories of occult and demoniac horror.No blackmail is as ghastly as extortion from angels.A swift reckoning often travels in handheld luggage.No sign of life aboard an abandoned freighter, but what is left behind tells a strange story.
