Genius. Wealthy. Lonely. Odd. Sophie Berger spends her free time forming intricately laid plans for world domination. Her guinea pig just died. Her car is falling apart. Her parents ignore her. Her best friend is paranoid that her older brother is stealing Sophie away… and rightly so, because Sophie has been getting goosebumps whenever Andrew is within a hundred yards of her…Handsome. Sweet. Genuine. Intelligent. Off Limits. These are just a few words that describe Andrew Stevenson, the older brother of Sophie Berger’s best friend. He would be perfect except for that last one…
