Some secrets never die. Hiding who you are can kill you.Declan and Sebastian St. Clair set out on a fishing trip with their father, when an unforeseeable storm bears down on them, tossing their tiny boat far into the sea, sinking it. Declan and Sebastian are forced, helpless, as they watch their father swept under the waves.Both brothers wash up on a deserted island, where for five months they work to survive and escape. After a tragic accident, they perish, only to be reborn as immortal creatures of the night, hungry for human blood.They are then forced to leave the island by their creator, where they enjoy their new immortal lives. Until a mutual catastrophic event forces the brothers to succumb to all too human destructive methods to bury and numb out the pain of their dark secret.
