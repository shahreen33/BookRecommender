Weekend Away is a modern romance, filled with ups, downs and everything in betweenHolly is eighteen, and has spent the last two years studying to be an electrician. Holly has also been the only girl in the class, so now it's time to leave and face the real world of full time employment.To celebrate her and her five male friends from college have decided to go to Blackpool for a 'Wild Weekend Away.' There is only one problem; she is in love with one of her best friends. Will she ever puck up the courage to tell him how she feels, or will she leave Blackpool never knowing what could have been?With twists and turns that make you cry out along with Holly. It will keep your finger turning to the next page.***Contains adult themes throughout.***
