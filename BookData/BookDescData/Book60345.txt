In this informative and lively book, 101 top physicians, medical researchers, and health experts share with the reader what they do to keep themselves and their families healthy.Each chapter focuses on a specific disorder and features a primary doctor and one or more "second opinions." The disorder is defined by the primary doctor, the common symptoms are detailed, and up-to-the-minute research confirming the doctor's findings are presented. The doctor offers his or her personal nutritional, supplemental, and/or lifestyle measures. Q&amp;A's, lists, charts, round-ups, and profiles complement the entry; and chapter wrap-ups highlight the most important points readers should remember.
