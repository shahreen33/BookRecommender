Soon after notorious child abuser Kate Montalvo is released from prison, she is murdered. Her death marks the beginning of a spree of crimes against known or suspected child abusers where the crimes mimic those committed by the abusers. Jennifer Riley, a young new social worker, begins to suspect her colleagues of being involved—except one, Joe Carpenter. Jennifer and Joe become an item and decide to investigate the group of “helpers” who become known as the Karma Club.
