FOUR swoon-worthy never before published, standalone stories. HEAs guaranteed.  You’ve done what’s expected. You’ve stayed in the “good girl” lane. How’s that working for you?  If you have a chance at falling in love . . . Will you take matters into your hands? Kiss a stranger with wild abandon? Throw caution to the wind along with your clothes?  Don’t think about the consequences. Dive into the forbidden, the off-limits. Be fearless. Be wild. Be unpredictable. Be reckless.  ***Each novella in the Reckless anthology is an never before published, standalone story by Mika Jolie, Kylie Stewart, P M Carson, and Sharla Lovelace.
