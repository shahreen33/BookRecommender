A second chance to build the family of their dreams.  Dr. Grant Anderson never expected a second chance with Leo Garner. Leo’s current significant other is a movie star, and who can compete with that? But when Leo’s kidney problems resurface and his relationship falls apart over the care of his adopted daughter, he returns home to North Carolina to start over from scratch. Does he want a do-over with Grant too?  The heat between Leo and Grant is explosive and undeniable, and their affection reignites as well. Can their fresh start survive Leo’s health scares, his daughter’s anxieties, a return of the jerk ex, and both their insecurities? And can they make a new family for themselves?  This book is over 60,000 words of sweet, sexy romance with a happy ending you’ll love!
