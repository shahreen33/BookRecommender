Everyone loves the holidays. Good food. Great times. And the presents! When some friends decide to become each others' Secret Santas, it should have been a blast. There might even be the opportunity to play a joke or two -- because no one knows who their Secret Santa actually is. But when people in the group start disappearing, it's clear that the joke has gotten out of control. And that one of the Secret Santas is not so jolly after all. . . .
