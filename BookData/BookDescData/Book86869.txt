Dean has made peace with her animal half. Having regained her abilities, she's become the threat feared by the three races, but that is only the beginning. If she wants to survive and seek revenge for her family, she'll have to master her nature and uncover the secrets of her people that have been forgotten for generations. Franklin Freewood plotted to bring all of the changers in the country together. With his death, the consequences of his schemes impacts those left behind as rogues invade the city by the dozens. In the shadow of their violence, Katherine Meadows, a human detective who has stumbled onto the three races, must make a decision. Will she side with the mysterious hunter who claims to be a part of the last group fighting for humanity or will she trust her partner, a changer who exposed his secret to protect her?
