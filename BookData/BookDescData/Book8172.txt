Welcome to the world of Maddie Maze, a 14 year old girl who is about to embark on the adventure of a lifetime.Maddie finds herself accepting life in "Nowhere, Colorado" after waking up at the age of 8 and finding her parents missing. She is sent to live with her Aunt Bernice, who is desperately trying to remind Maddie that she is a girl.But things are about to change in Maddie's world with one recurring dream, a boy's kiss and a book.
