Ernst, Graf zu Reventlow (18 August 1869 – 21 November 1943) was a German naval officer, journalist and Nazi politician."The Vampire of the Continent" is First World War propaganda. The 1916 English translation is by George Chatterton-Hill, an Irish traitor, and was smuggled to New York by U-boat for publication. It is heavily abridged. The 1915 original German text is "Der Vampir des Festlandes; eine Darstellung der englischen Politik nach ihren Triebkräften, mitteln und wirkungen", and is about twice as long.
