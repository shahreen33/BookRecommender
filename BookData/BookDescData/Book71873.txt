Voices of the Martyrs A.D. 34 - A.D. 203 is a powerful addendum to the Kingstone Bible Triology which carries the story beyond the Bible through the lives of the early Christian church. The hardcover compilation begins with the Apostles (including Paul) focus' on undying obedience to Jesus Christ, and then expands to include early church leaders who paid the ultimate price. This biographic anthology covers the stories of: Stephen, James the Great, Phillip, Matthew, James the Less, John Mark, Paul, Peter, Matthias, Thomas, Judas/Thaddeus, Andrew, Nathanael, Simon the Zealot, Mary, Barnabas, James, Jude, Luke, John, Polycarp, Justin Martyr and Perpetua.
