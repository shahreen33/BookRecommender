Two lost brothers venture across a strange forest and discover incredible and bizarre things on their journey home in a style reminiscent of The Wind in the Willows and Sleepy Hollow.Wirt and Greg find themselves at a crossroads in the Unknown and it looks like the brothers will each have to take his own path. With the liklihood they’ll come across fantastical creatures or ghastly towns, it doesn’t matter which path is chosen when something far more sinister is on their tail...Eisner Award-winning cartoonist Jim Campbell returns To Over the Garden Wall with short stories by Danielle Burgos (Over The Garden Wall), Cara McGee (Clarence), George Mager (Uncle Grandpa), and introducing Kiernan Sjursen-Lien, in these not-to-be-missed adventures based on the Emmy award-winning show from Cartoon Network.Issue 5-8
