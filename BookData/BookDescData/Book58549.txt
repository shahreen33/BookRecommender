A Fistful of Pesos is a cinematic, darkly humorous crime thriller. Recently released from prison after serving seven years for dealing marijuana, American demolitions expert Zachary Taylor travels to Mexico City to help his old friend Pancho Sanchez and his motley gang rob a bank. However, the hapless would-be thieves arrive to find that another group of criminals has just robbed the same bank minutes earlier -- and they become trapped inside by the city police and the Mexican Army. Will they get out alive?
