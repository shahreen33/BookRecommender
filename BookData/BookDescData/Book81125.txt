When Walt gets a call that his old boss has been murdered and he is the prime suspect he goes into panic mode. He needs to clear his name before his worst nightmare comes true and he lands up behind bars.  Walt, along with Eddy, Samantha and Jo, go on a search to uncover the truth and work out why Walt was implicated in the murder. But is Walt hiding something that will prevent the truth from being revealed?  Will the ‘Sage Gardens Sleuths’ be able to protect Walt from danger and clear his name before he lands up in prison?
