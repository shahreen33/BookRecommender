What stalks the land cannot be, but is.Where magic is outlawed, a troll shaman calls from her deathbed to her heiress, Mirandra Var, daughter of the storm. Mirandra promises to find her missing kin, sort friend from foe, and claim the dangerous secrets guarded by unthinkable creatures. If she succeeds, she will become the leader of her tribe. If she fails, there will be no tribe to lead.Contains scenes of a sexual nature.
