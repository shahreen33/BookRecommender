Two years after his wife's murder, Kirby Mayhew submits his resignation letter as pastor of Harvest Church. He’s ready to pack up his life and move away from Caney Cove and away from the shadow he's been living under. But things aren’t that simple for Kirby and his plan goes awry when he learns new information about his wife’s death. As he pursues the truth, an unseen enemy fights to keep it hidden. He enlists the help of a local prosecutor but her presence in his life only raises the stakes.
