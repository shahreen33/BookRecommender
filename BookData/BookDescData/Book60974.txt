When you get your slice of life’s happiness, take it with both hands and run with it.The struggle is real. Beverly spends so much of her time juggling her four kids, a dead-beat ex, and two jobs that she never has time for herself or another man in her life.Then Connor moves in next door.Connor is healing from a hard break from his family and is working to find his own identity. He has no interest in joining another one.Then he meets his neighbor.Two people building new lives. Two people with no time or energy for love. Bev and Connor are determined to stick to their plans, but life has other ideas—now they just have to run with it."Run With It" is a Dragon Runners motorcycle romance spin-off., featuring Connor from "Stud."
