No need to sacrifice beauty for time. This stunning new collection of quilts brings new vibrancy to classic favorites, using up-to-date fabrics and short-cut methods. Fourteen designs, each shown with four additional color schemes, give optima! variety and inspiration for choosing a project. From small floral prints for a country cottage to bold, plain colors to enhance a contemporary decor--the operative word for every pattern is quick. A chapter on Basic Tools and Techniques gives technical background for beginners, and brilliant photographs throughout display the finished project. The patterns cover double-, queen- and king-size beds. All are easy to make but will provide warmth and comfort for many years. By the same editor of the popular quilt books, Quick, Colorful Quilts; Super Quick Colorful Quilt; and Quick Colorful Quilts for Babies and Toddlers.
