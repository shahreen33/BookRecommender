"Murder by Proxy" is a revisit to "Valley of Obsessions", fiction based on personal experiences.  Will an American woman discover her destiny in Egypt, a country that has captivated her since childhood? As she treks through shifting sands and enters portals of pharaohs long since entombed, she is unaware that someone in this alien territory wants to end her fantasies. How far will he go?
