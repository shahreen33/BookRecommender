Stephine Willis no longer believed in happily ever after. At least not for herself. Max Vicente was determined to change her mind. After all, some fairy tales really do come true.  This 20k word novella is part of the Alluring series but can be read as a standalone. ***Beware*** This is insta-love with an OTT nod to Cinderella. It's meant to be slightly unbelievable but 100% fun. Due to sex and language, it is recommended for persons over 18.
