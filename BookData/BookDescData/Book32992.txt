He is revered among his kind,an archangel who can do no wrong.But when temptations and choices arise,the pedestal will come crumbling down.As the Archangel of Death, Jeremy prides himself on his loyalty and high-ranking status amongst his peers. From the outside, it appears as though he deserves the utmost respect and admiration, but he knows the truth—a secret that could destroy his family. Opening up and succumbing to his inner desires would be the ultimate betrayal, a betrayal he can never risk.When Jeremy receives an order he knows will shatter everyone he holds dear, he’s forced to choose between family and duty. His decision shocks everyone, even himself, and leads to inescapable anguish for everyone he loves.Will the once revered archangel suddenly become one of the fallen?
