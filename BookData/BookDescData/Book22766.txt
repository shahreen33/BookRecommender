Meet Emma West, a beautiful, smart, and organized girl, who knows what she wants in life.Once she's met Matt, she feels like she's headed in the right direction. He's the perfect guy, handsome, funny, and caring, all she could want in a guy. So why does she become so disoriented about their relationship?Meet Matt Nichols, a gorgeous, careless player, who doesn't have a care in the world. But all of that changes once he meets Emma, the most beautiful girl he's ever laid eyes on.He feel he has to be perfect for her, and he must do everything right.In his mind he HAS, so why doesn't their relationship work?Are these two better off friends? Or are they really the perfect match?* This story follows Emma and Matt's eventful friendship through the years, full with humor, love, disappointment, hurt and betrayal. *
