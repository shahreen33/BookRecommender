Journey to a galaxy far, far away in Volume Two of an all-new series that captures the Star Wars universe for readers of all ages.A rotating cast of characters (and creators!) takes us on a tour through Star Wars history and across the galaxy! Stories range from before the events of Episode I: The Phantom Menace all the way up to Episode VIII: The Last Jedi and beyond! Characters major and minor, classic and new, expand the world of Star Wars into fresh territory, telling larger-than-life stories that encapsulate the breadth of the galactic struggle between good and evil for a new generation of fans.Collects Star Wars Adventures #3-5.
