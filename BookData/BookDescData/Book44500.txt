“Compliments of the Domino Lady!” In order to avenge the murder of her father, socialite Ellen Patrick donned a domino mask, an evening dress and packed a .45. Running for six rare stories in mid-1930s pulp magazines, these stories remain elusive. Now, these are collected in an affordable edition, complemented by an all-new Afterword by pulp historian Tom Johnson.The Complete Adventures of The Domino Lady by Lars Anderson contains the following stories: “The Domino Lady Collects” “The Domino Lady Doubles Back” “The Domino Lady’s Handicap” “Emeralds Aboard” “Black Legion” “The Domino Lady’s Double”
