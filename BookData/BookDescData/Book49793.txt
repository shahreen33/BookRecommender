Wendy the Wild Wolf is a delightfully entertaining picture book story following a wild wolf named Wendy as she sets off on a fun and enthralling adventure deep in the woods.With a skip and a hop; a shake and a sway; and a stomp and a stamp what will Wendy find and who will she meet along the way?This is a rhyming picture book story suitable for the entertainment of both early and intermediate readers and for engaging excitable audiences.
