Belle Harrison, vampire slayer, has just leveled up spectacularly. She’s thirstier. And sensier. Yes, that’s a word now. But her new dead life isn’t all bloody cocktails and hot sex with her three gorgeous vampires. The dark unknown who wants Belle dead—permanently—has been up to no good. It’s time to take him down. No problem, right? Wrong. No slayer has ever survived until their twenty-first birthday except one, and he lost his damn mind. And something’s not quite right with Belle’s…
