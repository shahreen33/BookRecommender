Life seems good to fifteen-year old Englisha. She's finally captured the attention of David, the perfect boyfriend. She's also excited about her upcoming sweet sixteen party. At Englisha's birthday party, her mother gives her permission to visit L.A for a week with David. Laurenthina, Englisha's best friend, warns Englisha about going on a vacation with a boy that she recently met. Englisha feels Laurenthina is jealous.On this adventurous vacation, Englisha escapes death several times. Should Englisha have listened to Laurenthina's warnings?
