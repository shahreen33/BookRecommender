Welcome to Spellbound, where paranormal is the new normal.Emma Hart would like to believe she's glowing more than growing, but there's no denying her stomach now has the look of a prize-winning watermelon. Her pregnancy results in more than physical changes, however, as she decides to delve deeper into her family history. When a routine misdemeanor case goes awry and Althea is the main suspect, Emma's personal life must take a back seat as she sets out to help both her assistant and her client. With a coven in flux, a vampire ghost roommate in maternal mode, and an angelic husband planning one last romantic getaway, Emma is ready to burst in more ways than one. Will our favorite sorceress manage to save the day or will she become the next victim? Charmed Offensive is the fourth book in the Spellbound Ever After paranormal cozy mystery series.
