"Love is Love is Love is Love!" shouted Lin-Manuel Miranda accepting his Tony in 2016. We have all witnessed the power of love, and there's nothing more important than sharing that love with family and friends. Exploring the different meanings of love to each of us, this inspiring book is filled with quotes from Johnny Cash to Mindy Kaling, all of whom speak about what love is and means to them. With beautiful illustrations and pictures to accompany the quotes, this book makes a beautiful gift for a loved one -- or to keep and admire for yourself!
