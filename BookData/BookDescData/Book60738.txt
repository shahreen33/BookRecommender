Oria is the Golden Queen of the Ahwahneechees and a Dweller of Ahwahnee. Together with her friends she travels back in time to find out why the village of lost souls cannot find their way home to the West. The Ala is back in this second book full of action packed magic and shapeshifters . Will Oria finally find love and happiness with Grey Wolf or will evil threaten her magical world Beyond Oria Falls?
