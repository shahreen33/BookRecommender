Four friendsOne summerNo regretsFour friends pledge to make their summer regret-free but will it be that simple?After their exams, sixteen-year-old friends Sasha, Hetal, Nell and Cam pledge to live a summer regret-free, taking risks however much it scares them.As all these choices become difficult, even dangerous, they will need each other for the strength to face the future.A diverse, feel-good story of friendship, irresistibly escapist.
