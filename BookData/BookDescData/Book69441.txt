A family moves into a house, a possibly haunted house, and the rabbits on the front porch stir and come to life.This special edition of Kelly Link’s ‘Stone Animals’ comes with a letterpressed cover and interior illustrations by Lisa Brown, Lilli Carré, Anthony Doerr, Lev Grossman, Daniel Handler, Paul Hornschemeier, Ursula K. Le Guin, Laura Miller, Audrey Niffenegger, Tao Nyeu, Arthur Phillips, and Lane Smith.
