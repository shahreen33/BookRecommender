It's hard for the aspiring writers to find a concise reference to creative fiction writing. On February 5, 2017, author Pawan Mishra delivered a video talk on "The Craft of Creative Fiction Writing" to the students of Lady Shri Ram College, New Delhi. The script of the talk has ever since become a popular reference for students of the creative writing and aspiring writers.The twenty-one fundamentals listed in the talk cover the most essential aspects of creative storytelling. Each one of these fundamentals is explained in a simple way, easy enough to understand and remember.
