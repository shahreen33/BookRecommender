Joseph the Good Giant, was as gentle as he was big. He lived in a cottage in the forest, using his powerful magic ring to do good for any and all who crossed his path.Far and wide was Joseph the Good Giant known, for his kindness and his magic. But wicked wizard Morgul wanted that powerful magic for himself. So he deceived the Good Giant and stole his magic ring.Joseph was determined to get his lost treasure back and set off on a quest to find the wicked wizard. Join the good giant Joseph and see where his adventures will lead. Using a magic staff to find the way, meet fantastic creatures and fierce dragons, fight magic battles and brave your fears. And above all, always remember... ...to only do good!!A fairytale of a Giant and his journey on a dangerous quest.
