Huckleberry Finn isn't very happy living with a stuffy old widow who tries to "civilize" him. But when his cruel scoundrel of a father steals him back, Huck's life gets even worse. He escapes an soon joins up with a runaway slave named Jim. Together they go down on an adventurous raft ride down the mighty Mississippi River. Join Huck and Jim as their journey teaches them many important life lessons.--back cover
