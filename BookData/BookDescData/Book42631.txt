Paras, a poor girl who lived with her cruel step-mother and step-brother, becomes rich overnight after her husband, Rizwan, who was too old for her and owned a chain of hotels countrywide, dies in an accident and leaves everything for his wife. But is it really an accident? Rizwan's brother, Faizan and sister, Sawera doesn't think so. They are determined to take revenge of their brother's murder whatever way possible. Even if it means murdering Paras themselves.But is revenge really the thing they want? Or is it the huge amount of wealth that their brother left behind?
