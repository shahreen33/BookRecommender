When she finally surrenders her heart, will it be too late?Susan Quinn, social worker turned surrogate mom to foster teens, fights to save the group home she's worked hard to build. But now, she faces a dwindling staff, foreclosure, and old heartaches that won't stay buried. Her only hope lies with the last person she'd ever turn to---a brawny handyman with a guitar, a questionable past, and a God he keeps calling Father.Like a Love Song is a romantic drama about a fiercely loyal woman, some cast-off kids, and finding the courage to believe in a love that never fails.Romantic Times gives Like a Love Song a "Top Pick" 4.5 star review.
