Noted British author, Rosalind Brackenbury, prize-winning short story writer, poet and well-reviewed novelist, steps back from her own writing to reveal her creative process. Her advice is rendered in a fluid, conversational tone, both accessible and memorable. THE NOVEL IN YOU is lush with classic examples, personal anecdotes, and accompanied by practical exercises for the serious novelist. It essentially hands over the keys that unlock the sought after components of inspiration, discipline, construction, and ultimately better writing.
