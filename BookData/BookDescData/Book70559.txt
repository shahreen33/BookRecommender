John Fried's THE MARTIN CHRONICLES, pitched as in the vein of Tom Perrotta's BAD HAIRCUT and the movie BOYHOOD, set in 1980s Manhattan, about a boy from age eleven to seventeen as he runs into a series of life-changing firsts: first kiss, first enemies, first love, first death, and ultimately, his first awareness that the world is not as simple or as safe a place as he once imagined, to Karen Kosztolnyik in her first deal at Grand Central, in a pre-empt, by Mitch Hoffman at Aaron Priest Literary Agency (NA).
