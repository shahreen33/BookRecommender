 An Exciting, Thrilling, Complete Western Romance Novel   ** No Cliffhanger   Joanna was the epitome of a southern girl- beautiful, curvy and as sweet as cherry pie. She always remained positive and happy, even without having much time outside of her work life.  With little luck dating, Joanna decided to sign up for an email order bride service, thinking little of it.  Awaiting her arrival was John, a handsome, muscular hunk with a stellar ranch.  Will love find course through old methods? Or will they be forced to walk separate paths alone?!  Warning: Readers should be 18 or over due to mature content and language.
