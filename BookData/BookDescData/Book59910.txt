From the wicked imagination of Clive Barker comes a horrific tale of a theatre stalked by the most famous movie stars of our time!When an escaped convict commits murder and finds his way to a decaying movie palace, he cannot know that his own life is about to end, while the dreams of a half decade's moviegoers are just beginning. for this convict's cancerous tumor refuses to die, and the angels of the cinema can greant mirages--and nightmares--of their own.In a world that has given up God for Garbo, life may be nothing more than an endless series of flickering images--and death the only refuge from the...Son of Celluloid.
