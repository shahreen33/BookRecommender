The year is 1927. The crew of the airship the Sweet Revenge live on the fringes of society, taking odd jobs and supplementing their incomes with activities of questionable legality. Captain Phineas Wilbert has a bounty on his head, which the British government has every intention of collecting. Staying away from the United Kingdom would be easy enough, but word has spread that someone from Phineas' past may not be as dead as he previously suspected.
