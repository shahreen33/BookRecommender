Who or what is causing the horrible deaths of people working near the Egyptian pyramids? Has something awakened the ancient curse of the pharaohs? Is it terrorist activity? Or is something even more sinister and frightening?Professor Stirling, the famous paranormal investigator, is called in to investigate and it's up to him and his son Matt to explain the inexplicable before it's too late.A gripping read for fear-addicts everywhere, by Doctor Who writer Terrance Dicks.
