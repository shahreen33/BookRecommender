Jessica Wakefield is having a blast visiting Sweet Valley University. And after just one day, she's found the perfect college guy. Zack Marsden is 20 years old, tall, dark, and handsome. They make an awesome campus couple. But Zack believes that Jessica is a college junior! And she's going to make sure he never finds out the truth.Elizabeth Wakefield is in love...with college! She spends every spare moment working on her crash-course journalism class. Jessica thinks she's crazy. But then the professor gives Elizabeth the opportunity to be a real live reporter. With an offer like that, will Elizabeth ever go back to high school?
