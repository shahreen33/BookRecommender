Devon Michaels has a problem. His high blood pressure is out of control. He’s a stroke victim waiting to happen.  Devon Michaels also has a solution. Become a vampire and gain eternal life, along with perfect health.  The problem is how do you find a vampire? But an even bigger problem arises when you do.  “Metamorphosis” is a 5000 word short story exploring an unusual solution to a common problem and when being dead just might mean you stay alive.
