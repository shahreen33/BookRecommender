British YA fantasy is hard to come by, which makes the "Celestial Creatures" even more special.With the power to end the world, would you protect humanity when it broke you or would you take revenge?Meet Ariel: a quiet, unnoticeable girl with an incredible gift…Ariel never had an easy or pleasant life, but the arrival of three gorgeous strangers meant her measured life is turned upside down, as she discovers that angels exist.Now against her will, she’s drawn into an ancient celestial conflict where her powers will decide the fate of humanity.Deceived, threatened, hunted and now on the run, who can she trust?
