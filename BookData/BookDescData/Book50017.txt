THE NIGHT IS LONG AND COLD AND DEEP is the ultimate Terry M. West horror fiction sampler. This is a full length collection featuring some of Terry M. West's best work. These tales run the horror spectrum from extreme, gory, disturbing, haunting, emotional, humorous and unnervingly psychological. Featured are five complete and highly-praised novelettes/novellas: SERVANT OF THE RED QUILL, HEROIN IN THE MAGIC NOW, CECIL &amp; BUBBA MEET THE THANG, MIDNIGHT SNACK and HAIR AND BLOOD MACHINE.
