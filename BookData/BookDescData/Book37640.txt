Welcome to bullied for being me, a real insight of what bullies can do physically and mentally as I tell you my very personal and emotional story. How being victimised and picked on really affected my life on so many levels. So as the saying goes sticks and stones my break my bones but names will never hurt me IF ONLY THIS WAS TRUE As you follow my journey you will see what happened to me and how I was affected. It will be eye opening for some of you, as I go into detail of what I had to endure on a daily basis and the struggle I have had to go through to get where I am today. All proceeds from this book will be donated to charity: Mitrofanoff Support. They help all those with a urinary diversion stoma like myself.
