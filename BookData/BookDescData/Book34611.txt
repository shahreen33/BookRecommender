Megan Walker’s touch has turned to ice. She can’t stop the frost, and the consequences of her first kiss are horrifying.When her new powers attract attention, Megan finds herself caught up in an ancient war between Norse giants. One side fuelled by a mad queen’s obsession and an ancient prophecy about Ragnarök, the other by an age-old grudge. Both sides believe Megan to be something she’s not. Both sides will stop at nothing to have her.Fire or frost. It’s an impossible decision, but she’ll have to act soon, because the storm is coming.
