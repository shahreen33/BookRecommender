DI Tracy Collier is back with a sinister case that tests her—but she won’t break. Will she? A five-year-old girl is missing, and her mother is found dead in the woods. With Damon off on leave, Tracy is working with Nada, a policewoman on her team, and together they’re finding it hard to stomach what has happened.  But Damon hears about the case and comes back to work, determined to find the killer. With the child’s stepfather, Mike, as the prime suspect, they keep close tabs on him, but Mike is being manipulated by the very person who killed his wife and kidnapped her child…  When other kidnaps come to light, the chase is on to apprehend the abductor. The problem is, with Mike going off and putting a spanner in the works, time might not be on their side.
