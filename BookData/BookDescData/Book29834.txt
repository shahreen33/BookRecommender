After excelling in her career as a photographer at a fashion magazine, Sophie is granted a new assignment: a photojournalism piece highlighting Small-Town Christmases. Being a city girl, she isn’t sure what to expect when she arrives in the mountain town of Stowe, Vermont. Everything about Stowe is perfect. From the tree in the town center, to the quaint Bed and Breakfast where she is staying. Even the inn’s charming handyman, Nick, is handsome and more than helpful.Sophie couldn’t be happier with her project. But then, after a small misfortune, Sophie and Nick uncover a precious item from the past that leads them on a mission to discover the not so perfect truth about Stowe.
