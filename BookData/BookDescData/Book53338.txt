Cyrus Walsh met Sammy when she was too young for him. He left the island knowing that he’d wait for her... and he did. But now that he’s back, she isn’t giving an inch. Now he’s going to fight for what he wants, and that’s always been the same thing. Her. Sammy watched Cyrus leave and it broke her heart. When he came back to the island years later she wasn’t ready to forgive him. But she feels an irresistible pull to him, no matter how hard she tries to fight it. Would giving in be so bad? Warning: HER PARADISE picks up right where HIS PARADISE left off. Enjoy even more island adventures with this strong-willed sassy heroine and her alpha who just won’t let her go.
