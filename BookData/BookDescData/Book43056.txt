Capitol Hell may look quaint, with its denizens wearing togas that go down to their belly buttons, but all is not well in the political nexus of Hell. Billiam must grapple with a man who has lost the benefactor who winds him up. Gershwyn must avoid roving bands of violent Vegans willing to eat the laurels directly off of hapless Forum members heads and everyone must get to the bottom of the sewer board, the lowest and most prestigious office of all. You see, there's a big secret in Hell. There is no sewer. But if that's the case, then where is all of the human effluent going?
