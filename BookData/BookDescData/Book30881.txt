With billions in the bank and my own venture capital firm, I’m living the American dream. My only problem? A car accident that leaves my mother with memory problems.Brainocytes, a new technology that can transform our brains, could be the answer to all of my problems—but I’m not the only one who sees its potential.Plunged into a criminal underworld darker than anything I could’ve imagined, my life-saving technology might be the death of me.My name is Mike Cohen, and this is how I became more than human.Please note: This book was formerly titled Human++.
