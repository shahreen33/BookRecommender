Vilgilante assassin Deathblow returns in THE WILD STORM: MICHAEL CRAY VOL. 2. His new target? A man named Arthur Curry.The hunt for his new quarry continues, but Cray's decisions and plans are beginning to push his teammates to the brink of abandoning him. And when Cray's tumor begins to act in ways that it hasn't before, his underwater brawl may end with him floating up to the surface as a dead man.The Wild Storm: Michael Cray Vol. 2 continues the adventures of Warren Ellis' breakout star! From screenwriter Bryan Hill (Ash Vs. Evil Dead) and art from N. Steven Harris, this graphic novel collects issues #6-12.
