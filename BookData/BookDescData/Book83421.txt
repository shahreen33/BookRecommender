Cordelia Lionheart is a different take on the story of King Lear's youngest daughter. As Cordelia's sisters and the oppressive lords maneuver to depose her father, Cordelia and her childhood friend Garred ally themselves with the farmers, servants and other working people. In this coming-of-age, alternate-history, medieval tale, Cordelia must find a way to bring about the new Britain her comrades are prepared to fight and die for.Cordelia Lionheart won an honorable mention in the historical fiction category of the Reader Views 2018-2019 literary awards and a finalist medal in the fiction category of the 2019 Book Excellence awards.
