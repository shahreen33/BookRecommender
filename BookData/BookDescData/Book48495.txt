The Princess and the Pea gets a fresh twist in this charming bilingual retelling.El principe knows this girl is the one for him, but, as usual, his mother doesn't agree.  The queen has a secret test in mind to see if this girl is really a princesa.  But the prince might just have a sneaky plan, too . . .  Readers will be enchanted by this Latino twist on the classic story, and captivated by the vibrant art inspired by the culture of Peru.
