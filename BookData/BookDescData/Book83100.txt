IT ONLY TAKES TEN MINUTES FOR THE WORLD TO END...  In under 10 minutes, 99.9% of the world's population will be dead… or changed. Conversion is instant. Headshots just make them angry. And they’re getting smarter.  Ken Strickland has made it through the first moments. But his family is still out there.  Can he survive? Can he find them? And even if he does, what comes next? Will they survive? Will they, with the few other survivors they have found, find a way to stop this menace? Or will they simply become a fewmore of the creatures that now rule our world?
