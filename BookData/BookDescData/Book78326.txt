I was consumed by vengeance. Living in a dark place that only fire and death can cleanse. Then I found her.She’s running from her past. Closed off and afraid to accept help from anybody else. She’s the light to my shadow.She’s everything I want. Everything I need. There’s no way I’m letting her walk out on what we have. I’ll do anything -- kill anyone – to save her from the trouble bearing down on her. Six is mine. And I protect what’s mine.BULLET is book 9 in the Lords of Carnage MC series, and can be read as a standalone. All your favorite characters return in this hot, steamy book filled with action, intensity, romance, and the loyalty of the club.
