Your boss is a bully. How far would you go to get revenge?Thomas Smeaton is a powerful man who makes life a living hell for his employees.When his bullying drives a woman to suicide, three of her colleagues decide it is time to take action before Smeaton destroys any more good people.Six months later, a car is found submerged in a reservoir, with a single body inside. Suicide? Or murder?Two people were there the night that car rolled into the depths of Loch Etrin. And one is still missing.
