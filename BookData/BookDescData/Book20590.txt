Read the fun times and adventures Harry and his Dad share together during the day while working in their garden, then enjoy the fictional bed time stories Dad creates for Harry about all the new insect friends they have met that day.The book also contains a lovely foreword from Nature expert and TV personality 'Bill Oddie' who shares his high regard for both book and author with quotes like "if you think you can write better, I bet you can't!".A book you and your young one's will love and share together.
