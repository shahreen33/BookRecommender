Getting Bi: Voices of Bisexuals Around the World, edited by Robyn Ochs and Sarah E Rowley, is an international anthology of personal writings by people from 32 different countries, ranging in age from 15-79. As quoted below, "This book literally puts a face on bisexual identity" and includes voices from Australia, Belgium, Brazil, Bulgaria, Canada, China, Columbia, Cuba, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Ghana, India, Ireland, Italy, Japan, Mexico, the Netherlands, New Zealand, Paraguay, Peru, Poland, the Philippines, South Africa, Spain, Sri Lanka, Switzerland, Turkey, Uganda, the United Kingdom, and the United States.
