Novel Re-edited March 12, 2013Six years ago, when Aubrey McCory was twenty-eight years old, her fairytale dream of living happily ever after ended the day her husband died—or did it end? Aubrey suddenly acquires the strange phenomenon of connecting with the dearly departed, namely her deceased husband. She finally finds the answer to what had taken place right before the accident that killed him. But she gets more than she bargained for when a new man comes into her life who seems to know her like a well-read book, and to her, it seems she’s known him forever. But why?
