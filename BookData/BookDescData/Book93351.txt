A Bewitched Place is the last story in the second volume of Nikolai Gogol's first collection of short stories, Evenings on a Farm Near Dikanka (1832).Like the concluding tale of the first volume, The Lost Letter: A Tale Told by the Sexton of the N...Church, it is told by an exuberant Cossack narrator, the old sexton Foma, who says that the tale is about his grandfather.
