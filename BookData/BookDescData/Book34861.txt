A 15th Anniversary, high quality celebration of THE ENCHANTED WORLD OF RANKIN/BASS: A PORTFOLIO with hundreds of new images, rare sheet music and information about televisions' dynamic duo Arthur Rankin, Jr. and Jules Bass. The Producers are best known for their stop-motion ANIMAGIC Holiday TV specials such as RUDOLPH THE RED-NOSED REINDEER and SANTA CLAUS IS COMIN' TO TOWN. This book also covers THE HOBBIT, THUNDERCATS, THE BERMUDA DEPTHS, THE JACKSON FIVE SHOW, THE OSMONDS and so much more! Rankin/Bass have covered so much ground in the entertainment industry and this book is your guide to The Enchanted World of Rankin/Bass!
