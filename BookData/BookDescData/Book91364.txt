Marybeth has decided it's time to clean up the bad side of Keyhole Lake, but when she finds a dead body in her new, rundown mansion, she has to wonder if she's bitten off more than she can chew.Between managing her upcycling business, putting up with an aunt who's taken over the local ghost community, and seeing her wayward sister off to college, Noelle's to-do list is already overflowing. Thankfully for Marybeth, she always has time to help any friend accused of murder!This is book 10 in the Witches of Keyhole Lake Paranormal Cozy Mysteries series. Warning: This fun, fast-paced mystery contains witches, talking donkeys, and a whole lot of sass!
