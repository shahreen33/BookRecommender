House of the Marionettes is volume ten in the Horror World of Junji Ito collection. It was originally published in Japan in 1997. Only one story, Den of the Sleep Demon, has been officially released in English. Others have been translated by fans.[CHAPTERS]01. Ice Cream Bus 02. Clubhouse (Gang House) 03. The Smoking Club 04.
