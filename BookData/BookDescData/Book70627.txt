The world-famous, fan-favorite animated epic returns to comics with an all-new twist on the classic transforming-jetfighters-versus-giant-aliens adventure!Return to the fan-favorite Macross Saga that began the classic Robotech franchise, as hotshot Veritech pilot Roy Fokker and skilled rookie Rick Hunter are pulled into an intergalactic war when the Earth is invaded by the insidious Zentraedi! But what happened before is happening again! Fractures in the timeline are rewriting history... which means that no-one in the cast is safe... and nothing will play out as you expect!
