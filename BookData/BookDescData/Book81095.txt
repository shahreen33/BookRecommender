When Bekki’s dad, Robert, invites Bekki and Nick to a round of golf, Bekki is excited that Nick and Robert will have some quality time together. Nick on the other hand is not so keen to go to the country club.  But when the group stumbles across a dead body on the green things start getting progressively worse. The prime suspect is Robert’s friend. Robert is adamant that his friend would not have had anything to do with the murder and enlists Bekki’s help to discover the truth which places her in a difficult position because of her love for Nick and her dad.  Will Bekki be able to uncover the clues and discover the truth? Is Robert’s friend really the murderer? Does Bekki’s support of her dad’s friend damage her relationship with Nick? Will Robert ever forgive Nick for accusing his friend of murder?
