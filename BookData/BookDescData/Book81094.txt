When Bekki’s handsome hubby, Detective Nick Malonie, surprises her with a camping trip for the weekend she is dreading the bugs, tents and lack of a private bathroom, but she puts her reservations aside and decides that she’ll make the most of her time with Nick, her best friend, Sammy, and Sammy’s boyfriend, Morris.  Despite Bekki’s best intentions, bugs prove to be the least of her worries when she stumbles across a dead body in the woods. With bad weather keeping them trapped on the mountain, a murderer on the loose, and some campers missing, the four friends join forces and follow the trail to solve the murder.  Will they be able to solve the murder before there are more victims? Or will they even be victims themselves?
