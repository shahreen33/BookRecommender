Wrongfully accused of his wife's murder, Barrister Charles Holborne must convince the police of his innocence Barrister Charles Holborne spends his life dealing with law breakers, people of violent criminality. But when his philandering wife Henrietta is found with her throat slashed, Charles finds himself up to his neck in serious trouble of the murderous kind. Arrested for her murder, can Charles discover the truth of her brutal death, and prove his innocence to a skeptical investigators? This is compelling whodunnit set in 1960s London and an addictive read for any crime fan.
