When life give you lemons, make lemonade.Despite being named after her mum's favourite saying, there isn't much for Lemonade Witt to look forward to since her mother died - especially now she has moved to the wilds of Willow Creek.Here, she meets an annoying boy called Tobin who, as head of Bigfoot Detectives Inc., is obsessed with finding the legendary beast. Reluctantly, she joins him in search of the elusive creature - but what they find instead is even better...
