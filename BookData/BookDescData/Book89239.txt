The Kobayashi house hold is peaceful once again, and Tohru has happily returned to an "ordinary" life. Whether patrolling the neighborhood for suspicious characters or taking on a part-time job, it's always "go hard or go home'' for the diligent Dragon Maid! But not all is well. Ilulu is having trouble embracing the life she's chosen, Shouta is worried about his relationship with Lucoa -- and could something be brewing in Miss Kobayashi and Tohru's relationship, too...? Change is afoot in Volume 5 of this popular comedy!!
