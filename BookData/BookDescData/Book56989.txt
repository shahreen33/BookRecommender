A new collection featuring the legendary 1990s Batman epics "The Many Deaths of Batman" and "A Lonely Place of Dying."This new collection of 1980s Batman tales includes the legendary story "A Lonely Place of Dying," in which a new Robin emerges in the form of computer hacker Tim Drake, who rescues Batman from The Joker and Two-Face. This volume also includes the mystery story "The Many Deaths of Batman," and a run of stories in which the Dark Knight must stop crime boss Zucco.Collects Batman #432-439, #443-444 and Batman Annual #13.
