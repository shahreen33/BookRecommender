With Channon now at his complete disposal, Jack finds himself at liberty to indulge his desire for control, tightening the bonds that tie Channon to him in the most literal way he knows how. Channon seems willing to do anything he orders, submissive to his wants and eager to serve, but he's still so new. Can Channon take everything Jack has to give him, or will Jack push him too hard too fast, and break him completely?
