Quantum (Eric Henderson) and Woody (Woodrow Van Chelton) grew up together, completely inseparable despite their vast differences. But, they were not a couple. They were best friends. One day, Woody disappeared from their sheltered uppercrust life, abandoning his best friend. But, they were not a couple.Now these two former best friends are stuck with each other for the rest of their lives, trying to make sense of everything as superheroes. But they are not a couple. Can Quantum &amp; Woody--and their pet goat, Vincent--handle the new responsibility thrust upon them, o will they drive each other crazy?
