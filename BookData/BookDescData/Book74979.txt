When you’re finally free and living life the way you should, you don’t take it for granted and Lauren and Natasha certainly don’t. After the dreadful kidnapping, they now live happy amongst the Kings of Rebellion MC, their new found family. However, certain people don’t like that they are now free and not spending the nights in their beds. A plan comes together and it’s the innocent that always gets hurt. When the Devil’s come to claim what they think is theirs, can the girls escape them with the help of their men, or will they live at the hands of the Devil’s for the rest of their existence because a life at the hands of a Devil is no life at all. Find out in Envy
