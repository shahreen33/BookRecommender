AN ULTIMATE MIRACLE -REVIVAL OF LIFE!The 50th Century men crowded round the gleaming case in which a young man lay sleeping. Suddenly, a terrifying mechanism began to revolve, tremble...Vials of liquid frothed, crimsoned, gathered in a hypodermic that jabbed itself into the sleeper's breast. The case shattered, the body stirred...An ultimate miracle had occurred - eyes of the 50th Century looked into those of the 20th! But Homer Ellory, alienated 20th Century scientist, didn't realize a devastating battle of wits and science awaited him.
