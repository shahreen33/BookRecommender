This book contains Four Tales to warm your heart: “The Psychiatrist and the Thief,” is a comedy- mystery. “A Marriage on the Rocks,” is all about a young couple who had serious problems in their marriage. “The Undertaker’s wife” is about a suspicious wife who doubted her husband, but you have to read the book to discover the truth about him. “Letters of Love” is exactly what the words mean. A loving brother seeks to guide his sister on the right path with his spiritual wisdom.
