Most blogs don't chronicle a horrifying, high-stakes struggle against otherworldly evil. Mark Huntley's does. In the fall of 2004, as the brutal campaign of Bush vs. Kerry draws to a close in Washington, D.C., even more sinister forces are gathering power in secret. For young Mark, who's seeing things he shouldn't see-- as a forgotten, destructive nature awakens inside him-- politics are the least of his worries. Every choice he makes could cost the lives of those he loves.Part 1 of 4.Winner of a 2014 Wattys Award. (Wattpad version: https://www.wattpad.com/story/2219334...)
