"Oracles" is a book written with the busy modern reader in mind. It is an interconnected collection of concise essays, poems, vignettes, and images that straddle the line between spirituality and philosophy. This unique book provokes the heart and mind of the reader to contemplate matters such as the condition of the heart, talent, fate, relationships, injustice, war, and faith to name a few. For a book dealing with such weighty matters, "Oracles" is written with a refined fusion of wisdom, insight, and poetic artistry. The arc of the book leads the reader on a journey to contemplate subjects that ultimately brings one closer to themselves, on their individual path of becoming that which they were created to be.
