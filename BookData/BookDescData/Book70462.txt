Noah Grimes is back, in a new, original short story!Noah is v excited: his class have been shipped off to a hotel in the Lake District to study a nearby Nuclear Power Station! On arrival, all Noah wants to do is settle in to his crisp hotel sheets and get proper rest, but his conniving secret half-brother Eric Smith has other plans. Soon everyone is sneaking in and out of their rooms, sending Noah into a Tailspin...There’s only so much Noah can’t take!
