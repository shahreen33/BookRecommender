The Patient From Hell Christopher Lowell is a patient infamous for his poor attitude toward the majority of the staff at the hospital where he has been undergoing treatment. But the attitude is just a front, because Christopher is dying, and nothing short of a miracle can save him…The Prankster Samantha Snyder enjoys playing practical jokes, but when she goes too far she is sentenced to community service at the local hospital. There are many places she would rather be spending her weekends than roaming halls that smell of disinfectant and urine. But suddenly things begin to change…The Estranged Brother Michael Lowell hasn’t seen his brother in over a year. The rift between them is more a chasm. So when he returns to town suddenly, Samantha can’t help but wonder why he has chosen to return now…
