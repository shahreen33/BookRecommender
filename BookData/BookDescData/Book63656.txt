Plant good seed, get good fruit. It’s true of farming and true of life. That’s why Jesus frequently compared the Word of God to seed that ripens to produce heavenly results in our earthly lives. Nothing is more important—and that’s why the Bible is the most important book you will ever read. Through the Scriptures, God speaks to us, challenges us, and encourages us. It’s how he plants the seed of his Word in our hearts.This guide not only stresses the importance of Scripture, but also teaches us how to study the Bible on our own. Studies include: Why Study Scripture? Getting an Overview, Looking for Details, Discovering Meaning, Learning to Apply, and Praying the Scriptures.Knowing Scripture helps you make the Bible a daily part of your life.
