Opposites attract.That’s how movies tell the tale. My lifestyle—not for the faint of heart.My oath—to protect the brothers I’ve come to call family.This life isn’t for everyone. Especially those in the spotlight. Meeting a Hollywood A-lister isn’t on my bucket list, and falling for one does not suit my plans. But a biker who hides in the shadows and an actress bathed in neon lights, calls for disaster. This story isn’t only about finding stability—it’s friendship, conflict, deception and slaughter. Death—it’s coming.Will we find the right balance?
