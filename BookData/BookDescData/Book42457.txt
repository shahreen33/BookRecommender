This is the third in a series of short stories by debut author Elliott James. The first of his novels, Charming, will be out in September 2013. Trying to make money off the grid, John Charming discovers an underground poker tournament where the hors d'oeuvres are made of human flesh and the players are gambling with much more than their money.
