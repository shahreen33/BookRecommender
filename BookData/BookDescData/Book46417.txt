Mr. Stead's graphic account of the great Welsh revival, herewith given, was first published in the magazine which he edits, and afterwards reprinted in pamphlet form. The few copies of the pamphlet which reached this country were eagerly sought and made a profound impression on all who read them.The account by Rev. G. Campbell Morgan was first printed in The Christian Commonwealth, London, and afterwards in The Congregationalist, Boston.Stead lived from 1849-1912, and was one of the victims of the Titanic shipwreck.
