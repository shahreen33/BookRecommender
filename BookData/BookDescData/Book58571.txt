Did Brad's dad make him sad? Brad thought he had...until his dog, Plaid, proved he could choose his outlook &amp; feel glad.Kids learn best through stories. Empower your child to own their feelings with this beautifully illustrated picture book by award-winning author, M.C. Goldrick.Brad's dog Plaid shows him how to feel and deal with emotions. Though Brad is having a bad day, Plaid shows him that it's in his power to choose his perspective and his feelings.
