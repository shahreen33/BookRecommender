Mandy (Angel) is a good girl, sick of living the perfect life. She is tired of fancy parties, forced dates and over controlling parents. Her world changes when she meets Caden.Caden (Chief) is part of a Motorcycle club, he is a bad boy and delves in dangerous things, but he captures her heart and soul. How will she ever go back to living a normal life, once she has experienced the happiness Chief can give her?
