Rule One: Anything Can HappenDaisy Cooper’s life has been pretty uneventful—until the moment it suddenly ends. Unfortunately, her death is—literally—an accident: Daisy wasn’t meant to die for another fifty years. One terrible, embarrassing clerical error is behind it, and Death himself is to blame.As Daisy battles against her new reality, she starts to realize that letting go isn’t just a challenge faced by those left behind. And while she learns how to survive this impossible time, friendship, hope and even love begin to come alive in the most unexpected ways.Dripping with heart and humor, Daisy Cooper’s Rules for Living is a fresh and modern take on loss, love and friendship. It is a joyful story about our own humanity and a poignant reminder of what it truly means to live.
