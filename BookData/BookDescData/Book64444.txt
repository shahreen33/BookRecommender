Simple and easy for busy or beginning quilters. Attractive and useful for babies and young children. Everyone wins with this collection of crib quilts, first-bed quilts, playmates, and wall quilts for kids' rooms. Included are 14 bright, colorful designs, each with four alternative color schemes for optimal variety. A section on Materials and Techniques helps quilters get started, and simple, clear diagrams throughout the book help keep them going. Brilliant photography and fabric suggestions eliminate the guesswork, so that finished projects are sure to please. By the same editor of the popular quilt books, Quick, Colorful Quilts and Super Quick Colorful Quilts.
