A violent disaster has caused the dead to rise, but that was only the beginning. Now, society has broken down, the dead control the cities, and what is left for humanity is entangled in a fierce struggle for survival.  Like the zombies she must fight, Scout Johnson has already lost her life. She woke up one morning and everything that ever mattered to her was suddenly gone. Even as the world around her fills with cruel survivors, Scout pushes on in search of a reason to keep fighting to stay alive.  When Scout comes across another group of survivors, she finds herself thrust into the crossfire of a bloody battle between opposing factions. One group seeks to find a cure and restore order, while their enemy is intent on creating their own twisted vision of a new world. Scout must align herself with the lesser of two evils in order to survive and joins their fight to try and save what still remains of humanity.
