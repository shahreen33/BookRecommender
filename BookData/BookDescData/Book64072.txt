First published in 1934, this collection of Agatha Christie short stories features 12 ingenious tales involving mystery and adventure, from a stolen rajah's emerald to a necklace discovered in a basket of cherries to a mystery writer's bizarre near-arrest for murder.The stories are:-1. The Listerdale Mystery2. Philomel Cottage3. The Girl in the Train4. Sing a Song of Sixpence5. The Manhood of Edward Robinson6. Accident7. Jane in Search of a Job8. A Fruitful Sunday9. Mr. Eastwood's Adventure10. The Golden Ball11. The Rajah's Emerald12. Swan Song
