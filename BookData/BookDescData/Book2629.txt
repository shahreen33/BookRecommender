Bianka, a vampire with an insatiable thirst and desire for the body unlocked Pandora's box when she disposed of Iona,and instead of getting the freedom she desired, she condemned herself to a curse. With her new found fate as the Rose,comes the responsibility of choosing her own courtesan and fulfilling her pledge to Artemis and the Oracle, but Bianka is hell bent on revenge, and if she can't have her freedom then at least she can play the King and his court for fools.Of course, nothing is never simple, and as Bianka puts all her energies into revenge, she doesn't see the ensuing chaos that she unleashes. A dance for fools is only the beginning of the end...
