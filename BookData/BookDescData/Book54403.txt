The only time Crystal “Cee Cee” Castle is confident is when she peers through her camera lens. In Africa, she finds an elusive king cheetah only to discover there is more to this cat than she could ever begin to realize. Kent “Ziggs” Ziggler hadn’t been expecting to find his mate much less meet her in his feline form while she faced down some poachers looking to kill him. However, now that she’s in his terrain, he’s out to convince her to stay. If he can keep her alive.
