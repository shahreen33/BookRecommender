A HUNGRY HOUSEAfter a series of disappearances in an old mansion, the intrepid ghost hunters of Shibuya Psychic Research try to unearth the gruesome secret of this scary maze of rooms and passageways. The discovery that the house has a history of murders leaves everyone fearing for his and her own safety. What mystery is this dark manor hiding? And will SPR crack the case in time to save one of its own from a terrible fate?
