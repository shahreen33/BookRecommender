When a lightning strike on ancient Sioux holy ground sends mining engineer Kelsey Winter to 1892 Wyoming, she turns to handsome, reckless cowboy Pete Jackson to help her find a way home.Pete’s happy to help the strange woman in tight denim trousers, despite her odd, cautious ways, when he learns she can assist him with his newly discovered silver mine. But a deadly outlaw wants Pete’s silver, and a Sioux prophecy threatens Kelsey’s return home, and Pete and Kelsey must find a way past their differences, and the century between them, if they, and their growing love, will survive.
