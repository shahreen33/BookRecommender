It’s not easy celebrating your anniversary at work when your boyfriend is your boss. It’s even more difficult when your boyfriend is also your master. Tommy is still determined to do his best. Present bought and wrapped, all he has to do now is wait for it to be delivered.Harmon Peterson makes it a personal rule not to mix business with pleasure. But, when a black gift box tied with silver ribbon is delivered to his desk he soon gives into temptation to open it. Maybe a little bit of pleasure mixed into the work day isn’t all that bad an idea after all.
