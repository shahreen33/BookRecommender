When An Innocent BBW Meets An Alpha Lion On The Prowl.... Leon is a strong and handsome Werelion who is the Alpha of his pride. It is mating season and with no desirable females in his pride he is out to seek a new mate. He is heading toward the city to find a Lioness when he stumbles across the cute and curvaceous Lauren in a bit of trouble. As far as he can see, Lauren is 100% human but her curves are just calling out to him. Is Leon about to go against all of his animal instincts and mate with a human female? Either way, Lauren is about to find out that being seduced by a Lion Alpha is something that has huge consequences for the both of them..
