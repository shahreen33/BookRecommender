You're good at what you do, but that's not what's going to get you the job. Let's face it: so often the job goes to the person who did the best job of interviewing! This book will help you transform your interview skills and win the job.Concise and highly readable, Get That Job! is packed with everything you need to prepare for the best interview of your life. Through proven advice and tips, step-by-step instructions, sample questions and worksheets, you will learn how to:✔ Stand out for all the right reasons.✔ Answer tough questions with confidence.✔ Be both strategic and authentic.✔ Ace every step – from the first screening to accepting the offer.The book includes a Foreword by Orville Pierson, author of The Unwritten Rules of the Highly Effective Job Search.
