A grieving family hire a team of adventurers to hunt down the monster that killed their father, in this rip-roaring sequel to Fury From the Tomb.The family of a famous explorer and big game hunter offer a staggering reward for the head of a mythical beast, which they claim attacked their father at a remote lodge in the Colorado Rockies. A team of investigators - an Egyptologist, a headstrong, brilliant millionairess, and a bounty-hunting sniper - accepts the challenge. Blizzards, icefalls, killer grizzly bears, mad trappers, cannibal cults, and a ghostly white buffalo await them. So does a family who may have lost their minds and souls to the legendary Wendigo.File Under:  Fantasy [ The Big Hunt - Evil Spirits - Deliverance - Holiday Cottage ]
