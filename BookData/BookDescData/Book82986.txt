Jay is a player a typical college student with one thing on his mind and that doesn't change when he meets Betsy. He wants nothing more than to take her five ways from Sunday, and Betsy doesn't seem to object. Still, the more time he spends with her, the more she seems to affect him. When she gives him a homemade advent calendar for Christmas, Jay discovers the real meaning of the phrase, Hell hath no fury like a woman scorned. Note: This story originally appeared in the anthology Shivers.
