Five of our most popular authors have contributed to this second annual short-story collection inspired by the lives and efforts of this year's More Than Words Award recipients. By sharing the stories of these reallife heroines with our readers, Harlequin strives to turn awareness into action. Proceeds from the book are reinvested in Harlequin's charitable initiatives.What Amanda Wants by Debbie MacomberThe Yellow Ribbon by Sharon Sala The Way Home by Jasmine Cresswell Shelter From the Storm by Beverly BartonInto the Groove by Julie Elizabeth Leto
