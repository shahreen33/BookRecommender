The elegance of pattern laces and the artistry of stitches wrought by patient hands captivate today's needlecrafters and collectors It is in these laces that admirers discover the splendor of a past era when life was lived more graciously. Through its exquisite examples of crochet projects, Romantic Crochet allows the needle artist not only to reflect on this history and explore the joys of this craft but also to create beautiful and cherished items. This collection of more than 80 projects includes bedspreads, tablecloths, edgings, doilies, and afghans. Plus, special chapters offer children's items and trims for an old-fashioned Christmas.
