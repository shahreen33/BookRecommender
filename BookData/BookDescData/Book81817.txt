"Monsters aren't supposed to be real and if they are, that makes me the biggest monster of them all"On the first hour of her eighteenth birthday, Trinity is scratched by the beast. By the final hour, she is bitten by the monster. An ancient prophecy leads Trinity places she never thought possible. This forces her to question everything she believes in, including the loyalty of those she loves. Alliances are made and enemies faced, but in the end, Trinity must pick a side. Will she choose the beast or the monster?
