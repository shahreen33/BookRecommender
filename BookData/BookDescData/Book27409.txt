For fans of H.P. Lovecraft and Alien comes a new work of cosmic terror!Inside an abandoned mining station, in the depths of space, a team of scientists are seeking to unravel the secrets of humanity's origin. Using cutting-edge genetic cloning experiments, their discoveries take them down an unimaginable and frightening path as their latest creation proves to be far more than they had bargained for.Black Site is a short story of approximately 10,000 words.
