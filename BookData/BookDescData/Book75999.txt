If you want front page news coverage then you've got to put on a show. In this case, a fashion show. With much to organise and excitement mounting, we follow Eva, the owner of a small-town boutique, through the highs (and a fair amount of lows), as she works her way from booking the venue, right through to the final performance. It's a comedy of errors. So, will it be all right on the night? What do the papers say when the shows over? Read all about it!
