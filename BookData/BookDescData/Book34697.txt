This book was designed to prepare teachers to deliver content area instruction to ELLs with diverse abilities using a sheltered instruction approach. Updates include Ch 1 has intervention model, Ch 2 has ELL student rights and program options, and Ch 3 discusses the research-based model of sheltered instruction. Most professionals agree that teaching content material to students who are learning English is one of the greatest challenges teachers face today. (from Preface)
