Not all things are exactly as they appear.Beau Miller thought his happily ever after had returned in the form of the girl with the seafoam eyes. He never expected a second heartbreak at her hand. When her father, Declan Reede, turns up with murder in his eyes, Beau discovers that the break-up might not have been what it seemed. He must solve the mystery if he’s to find his girl. How can he and Declan rescue their princess when she’s been taken by someone with beastly intentions? And will they make it in time?
