Nora Roberts brings you two tantalizing tales of remarkable women who live... and love... on their own terms, featuring characters from CONSIDERING KATE, part of her heartwarming Stanislaski saga!REFLECTIONS: A lifetime pursuing her dreams had left Lindsay Dunne little time for romance. But seductive Seth Bannion is about to give the inexperienced beauty a crash course in the art of love.DANCE OF DREAMS: Innocent Ruth Bannion is in awe of her demanding, mercurial mentor Nickolai Davidov. But when their partnership erupts into passionate desire, it's up to Ruth to teach the guarded Davidov the tender dance of love.
