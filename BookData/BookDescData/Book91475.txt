A handsome faerie comes to the Enchanted Coast for a killer vacation, but nobody counted on it being literal.  When Marissa Clayton, a recently turned vampire struggling with her new identity, falls under suspicion, Destiny can't just let her go down, especially when she knows for a fact the girl is innocent.  However, some secrets aren't hers to tell, so she has to do it the hard way - find the real murderer in order to get her new friend off the hook.  The Surfboard Slaying is book 2 in the Enchanted Coast Magical Mystery series and is a light, funny, witchy story guaranteed to make you laugh.
