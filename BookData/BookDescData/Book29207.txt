They needed a place to get away from it all... they'll never be coming back.When Darren and Vanis set out to free themselves from life's anxieties and rekindle their relationship, a trip to the beautiful Maine coast sounds perfect.The breathtaking views and gorgeous cabin seem like another world. One to get lost in and from which they never want to return. But something has an eye on them...For Zebulun Ayers, a trip to connect with nature is far more than he ever saw on Man vs. Wild or any other reality TV show. This is the real wild life. Paradise, Maine is home to a monster rarely seen and one never mentioned, even among locals. The Watcher is waiting.
