The word Haalim is an Arabic word and it means “A Dreamer”. It’s a long novel with episodes and each episode will have a separate title, like Namal.The book follows Taliyah Murad, a 28 year old scam artist living in Kaula Lampur, Malaysia. She's an ace at what she does and has big dreams, literally. Taliyah Murad is clairvoyant.
