Once upon a time...you were a princess,or an orphan.A wicked witch,fairy godmother, prom queen,valedictorian,team captain,Big Bad Wolf, Little Bo Peep. But you are more than just a hero ora villain, cursed or charmed. You are everything in between. You are everything. In fifty poems Christine Heppermann places fairy tales side by side with the modern teenage girl. Powerful and provocative, deadly funny and deadly serious, this collection is one to read, to share, to treasure, and to come back to again and again.
