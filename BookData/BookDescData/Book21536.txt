A couple bound eternally by love like no other in the witch world. All their spellbinding desires are to lead a true love story together, which unfortunately is not what fate has in store for them. A secret is being kept, and this secret has found its way into the possession of a dark magick. A darkness hidden eons ago in the underground of the already mysterious world of witches, and this dark magick called the Brotherhood thrives among the powerless. Some secrets are not meant to be kept...
