We all know the story of the Big Bad Wolf and the three little pigs - or do we? Meet the great-great-great-great grandson of the original Big Bad Wolf, whos also on the trail of some little pigs. Is he up to no good - or is he trying to make friends at last? Find out in this imaginative picture book by Eva Montanari, guaranteed to delight children with its refreshing spin on a fairy tale classic. It makes a great addition to any learning-to-read library.
