Sometimes love is more than a feeling…it’s a choice.Justin Doyle loves his new wife, Sarah. Deeply. Madly. Passionately.... And he wants a divorce.A few weeks into their marriage an unforeseen tragedy strikes, bringing their lives to a screeching halt.For Justin, the loss hits him in deep and profound ways. He is wracked with a guilt which feeds his belief that he has never been—will never be—good enough. Sarah tries to be there for him while she deals with her own sadness, but he pushes her away…to a place neither are sure they can recover from.The one thing they need most is each other. And it’s the one thing they don’t have.Can they find their way back to the place they were before? Or is wedded bliss just a fairy tale?
