This anthology contains: Paingod by Harlan Ellison; The Malatesta Collection by Roger Zelazny; Angels in the Jets by Jerome Bixby; Sally by Isaac Asimov; I'm Looking for Jeff by Fritz Leiber; The Roller Coaster by Alfred Bester; Eve Times Four by Poul Anderson; Final Exam by Chad Oliver; April in Paris by Ursula K. Le Guin (her first published genre story;) and A Trip to the City by Keith Laumer.
