Terrified by her mobster ex before she can testify against him, Karla Jacobson, grabs her grandmother and goes on the run. Convinced Witness Protection and the sexy US Marshal assigned to them can’t keep them safe, she builds a new life on the other side of the country.  When her grandmother goes missing and Tony Sandowski stalks her, she has one chance—trust Trevor Davis. Can they fight their mutual attraction long enough to survive Tony’s attack and Karla’s testimony?
