For the first time ever, Ms Wiz reveals the unimaginable secrets of her life beyond St Barnabas School. Traveling a million miles outside the known universe, she returns with Jack, Podge, and Caroline to the Kingdom of Paranormal Magic and Utter Eternal Mystery where her own rather unusual family lives. There a surprise awaits her. After 10,000 years as ruler, Ms Wiz's father, the king, is feeling rather tired. A queen is needed. Soon Ms Wiz is faced with the most difficult decision of her extraordinary life...
