Infamous Deception... The tumultuous saga of beautiful, adventurous Tori Rawlings, an aristocrat of breeding and virtue. When she exchanges identities with a tavern wench to escape a repugnant marriage, Tori finds herself in league with a band of brigands and at the mercy of Scarblade, the most notorious highwayman in England. ...Raging Desire!An unforgettable novel of a young woman's surrender to excitement darker than she'd ever imagined...and a love more passionate than she'd ever known.
