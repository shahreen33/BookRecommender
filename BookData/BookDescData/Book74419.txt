Some sacrifices should never be made, even for love.Nesy is the best of an elite group of angels, warriors called Sentinals, charged with one job—vanquish the UnHoly. She’s never made a mistake, never gotten emotionally involved, never failed.Until she comes face-to-face with Aydan.He is evil incarnate. A fallen angel that feeds off the souls of others. Everything Nesy is supposed to hate. But she can’t, because he’s also the human love of her life; a love she was never supposed to feel.Now she must choose between doing her duty or saving Aydan, a choice that will cost more than her life...	Or his soul. **Second Edition includes the Requiem Short, Dies Irae, and features all new interiors and cover design
