Albury… once a thriving, country town, now a wasteland overrun by the dead, and occupied by the last threads of human immorality. With limited options, those who survived the aftermath push on into more populated areas, seeking help from the authorities. The group will rescue new friends, confront foes, and battle heart-wrenching loss. Unresolved issues will surface, and friendships will be tested, as love struggles to survive in a changing world. Within the infected walls of the Defence Department facility, Klaus Weinstrem battles on in his quest for a scientific saviour, but a bitter disagreement with the last remaining military personnel threatens to destroy them both. As the invasion of the dead strengthens, risk, courage, and good fortune are essential to survive in the post-apocalyptic world, but sometimes not even these are enough…
