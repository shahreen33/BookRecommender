The RMS Queen Mary is indeed a legendary ship with a legendary tale. Born to reign upon the oceans, she has been a testament to British shipbuilding. A stately masterpiece, she survived many obstacles and disasters while traveling the oceans. In war and peace, her career has been unrivaled by any other ship. Paranormal researcher and author, Nicole Strickland, gives a thorough account of the RMS Queen Mary's historical events and discusses paranormal encounters on the ship. The book discusses the author's own paranormal encounters on the ship as well as sharing some of the ghostly experiences of colleagues, friends and visitors have experienced on the Mary as well.
