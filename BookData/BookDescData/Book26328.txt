For two years now, Melinda has been in a relationship with Brandon. He is everything she’s ever wanted . . . loving, tender, and successful. Both living a hectic life forces them to drift apart no matter how much time they set aside for one another.After overhearing Brandon’s big plan for their relationship, she begins to feel confused, vulnerable, and in need of a night out. She turns to an unexpected source, a band drummer named Ryan Michaels. They both connected instantly, and without warning, Melinda makes the shocking decision to take a venture of a lifetime. One night lead into morning changes everything. Melinda is left with a plate full of challenges between her real life relationship, and her weekend romance.Will she drop everything for love at first sight or will she realize this is just a fantasy?
