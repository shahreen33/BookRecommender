Her billionaire boss Jackson Stone punished Delilah and then swore he'd never lose control again. So far he has kept his word...But a late night at the office turns to much more when a vicious storm rolls in and in a cave-man moment he hoists her over his shoulder and carries her to his car. Delilah knows she should keep away from him but she can't control her feelings and when the storm pushes them to his mansion, she doesn't protest.Jackson Stone was number #1 on her most-hated list because of what his company did to her home-town but when she sees through a chink in his armor to the man inside, she can't hold herself back any longer.This is the second part of the "Billionaire Boss" series by Emily Cantore.
