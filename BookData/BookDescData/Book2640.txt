Deception is a game well played, especially when you have the right players. 
Responsible for the death of two people and the demise of her father’s business, Brooke Stone’s life has been anything but perfect. Working to pay off her debts, she lands the interview of a lifetime at Prescott Oil and Gas International. Just when everything seems to finally be falling into place, the man that she’s been living with for the past eleven years reveals he’s not who she thought he was.Alone in a parking lot, Brooke has no other choice but to call on a stranger for help. Dylan Prescott, the billionaire playboy…her co-worker. The one man she’d sworn she would never have anything to do with. In her moment of weakness, he was strong. But did Dylan have secrets of his own that would destroy them before they even got started?Pick up Deception today and find out.
