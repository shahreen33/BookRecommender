When Areilla and her captain land on a strange planet after their shuttle's failure, things get dicey. Not only do they not know where in the universe they are, strange creatures attack them. In comes the handsome alien, Mirsux, to save the day. There is only one problem; they understand nothing he says, including a warning to leave immediately.  While trying to fix their shuttle, the creatures take the captain hostage. Mirsux promises to save him, however, there is a catch.  Kissed By The Alien Prince is a quirky, romantic, space fantasy story with creatures unknown, in a place unknown to humans. There are sacrifices to be made for survival... or is it a sacrifice when you are getting what your heart desires?  This is Book 1 in The Nabalar Prince series.
