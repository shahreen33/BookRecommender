The top-selling monthly title brings its third knock-out story arc to trade paperback format. The Kilgore brothers settle into their newfound power, but nothing is ever easy in the world of Haunt. The mysterious creature, The Apparition, relentlessly chases down the duo. However, a new ally may have the answers to The Apparition's defeat. Featuring art from master storyteller Greg Capullo, Haunt Volume 3 is the next great edition to any Haunt fan's library.Collects Haunt issues #13-18.
