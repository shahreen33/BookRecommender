Nine year old werewolf Sam has fleas. Thanks to his sister, fleas are the least of his worries.  Sam needs to find the cure to his embarrassing problem before the town's annual fair, or he will be humiliated in front of the entire werewolf community. With the help of his best friend Chris, Sam searches for cures to his hair loss problems.Will Sam find the cure in time or forever be known as the freak of Wolves' Crossing?
