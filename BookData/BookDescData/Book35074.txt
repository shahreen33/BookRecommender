Nya is the granddaughter of Captain Hook, and she has no intention of letting Peter Pan live after what he's done to her family.When she finally finds his hideout and goes to raid it with her pirate band, she's caught, something she can barely comprehend. It’s an even bigger surprise when she realizes she’s fallen for Pan, despite trying to convince herself otherwise, throwing her preconceived notions into a tailspin.But what will happen when she loses all she's come to love, and even Neverland itself?
