Another beginning brings forth another ending....or does it? Can there truly be just one beginning or one definitive ending when the tapestries of our lives are woven together so seamlessly? With their home destroyed a mere forty-eight hours after taking Kia into their care, the group finds itself taking refuge on Rolland’s farm in the remote countryside of Dartmoor, hoping for the chance to solve some of the mysteries that surround them. But you can’t run from everything and you can’t hide for long…. Secrets begin to unfold, eroding away at the trust they’ve built like the ocean wears away the rocks… but how many of these secrets are kept to preserve their safety? And whose safety is really at risk? In Pursuit of Trust, answers are offered - but at what cost?
