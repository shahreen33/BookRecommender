The ABC's of Surviving Cancer: Alive, Beautiful, &amp; Courageous features Cornelius the Cancer Fighting Crocodile taking young and old alike on a journey of self-discovery. Presented as verses for each letter of the alphabet, the story reassures, enlightens, and empowers youngsters with the message that cancer can be beaten. Cornelius helps friends and family find joy in helping each other through treatment. The ABC's of Surviving Cancer reminds us that we are never alone, and that we can always find strength within ourselves.
