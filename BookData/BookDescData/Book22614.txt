From long haul drivers to plumbers, matchmaker Elyse Tobin refurbishes and retrains men then sells them off as husbands to wealthy San Francisco socialites. Womanizing lothario Nick Salvatore is a millionaire restaurateur with looks, charm and wit. He's the man Elyse models all her fixer-uppers after, the man who seduced her three years ago, and the one man she wants nothing to do with --despite his ongoing efforts to woo her.But when Elyse's latest groom goes south, the IRS audits her, and her home is about to be foreclosed, she discovers the one man she can count on is the one she's spent years avoiding.
