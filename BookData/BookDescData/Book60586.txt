"We put on a fake smile to hide the pain, yet we wish someone would look closely enough and see how broken we really are inside."Socially awkward is a phrase that she'd use to describe herself.Meet Wren Martin. She blocks off herself to the world, hiding behind her favorite hoodie, believing that if she does this, her agonizing pain will never resurface - that tears and nightmares will be a thing of the past.When she arrives at her over-the-top high school, however, certain circumstances lead to her meeting the school's most popular and notorious boy, Asher Reed, who has his mind set to change everything she stands for, and save her along the way.
