A beautiful young girl, drenched in rain, is being chased by a couple of goons along the narrowmeandering roads of Landour, Mussoorie, when a swashbuckling stranger comes to her rescue.She faints and on waking up, realizes she is in the company of the 'bad boy' of Bollywood-Veer Singh Tomar.Natasha falls for Veer, who, incidentally, is married and dealing with a messy divorce. By some cruel twist of fate, Veer's estranged wife Amyra comes back to his life. But by then, Veer is already drawn to Natasha.Will Veer ever find his way back to Natasha?
