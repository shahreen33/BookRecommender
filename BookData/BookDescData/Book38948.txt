A cozy murder mystery with ghosts.  Grace Abrahams helps ghosts – particularly those who were murdered. She meets the ghosts at storage locker auctions.  Grace meets Jenny Lorrimer. Jenny was strangled by the lead of her headset. Grace finds out that Jenny was an events planner and she was murdered at a wedding that she’d organised. Grace delves further and discovers two suspects – Jenny’s sister and Jenny’s best friend. A shock discovery leads her down a different route.
