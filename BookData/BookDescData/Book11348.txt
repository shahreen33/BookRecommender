A family crisis brings Travis Casey back to the land he used to call home after living in England for the past two decades.His parents need him – not as a carer as he expected, but as someone to drive them from Florida back to their home in Minnesota.Just as well; he’s not a caring person, but he is a damn good driver.But the country is not how he left it…Casey uses his own brand of humor to explore what has changed in America since he lived there – that’s when he’s not recounting the chaos that occurs as he attempts to reestablish himself as an American. He discovers that entering the United States with his British wife is more difficult than he had anticipated.***A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step.--Chinese ProverbA journey of seven thousand miles begins with a trip to the US Embassy-- Travis Casey Experience
