Gail Simone continues her Batgirl run in a tale that ties into the best-selling Bat-Family event Death of the Family. When the Joker returns, Barbara Gordon must confront her past as she deals with the crazed criminal responsible for crippling her. Plus, once the dust settles, Barbara must deal with her family demons as her psychotic brother James Jr. comes after her.Collecting: Batgirl 14-19, Annual 1; Batman 17; &amp; material from Young Romance 1
