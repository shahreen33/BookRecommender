Torn between two brothers, Reese Brady must choose whether to live out her fantasies or opt for stability. She bears the load of her guilt to the point of exhaustion, excusing unsavory behavior as a form of self-punishment. Despite the temptation of one man, she commits to the other.  When a family crisis strikes, Reese is forced into the arms of the wrong man. How will she comfort him when she longs to be with his brother?  Only Ever You is the second book in the three-book A Little Like Destiny series by bestselling contemporary romance author Lisa Suzanne.  This forbidden romance series featuring a rock star and his business mogul brother must be read in order: A Little Like Destiny Only Ever You Book 3 - Coming October 26, 2017
