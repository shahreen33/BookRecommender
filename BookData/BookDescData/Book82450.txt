Poisoned by an enemy, a young warrior struggles to find an antidote before she succumbs to enslavement. At the same time, Kira is desperate to save Dorsit, a Leopard Clan wizard whose youth and magic has been stolen by a murderous wizard on a rampage. As her homeland is invaded by bloodthirsty legions, she flees across the country in a bid to reach the Mirrum Mountain nymphs and a legendary cure.This edition features a new cover.
