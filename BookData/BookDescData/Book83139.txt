Alicia Collinson and her partner, Sir Giles Milton QC have a dinner party to host in the Chelsea home they share, but they are unaware of an extra guest; the ghost of Alicia's dead twin brother. Alicia poses a surprising question to Sir Giles and their guests:---“Do you think lying is endemic in society today?”They all have different answers based on their experiences, but what was the purpose of her question and how did her brother die?
