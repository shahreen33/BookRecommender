Abandoned as an infant on New Year's Eve, 15-year-old Jason Griffin is ready to learn the truth about his past. However, before he can question his parents, they disappear... Jason finds himself swept into a multi-dimensional adventure in a hyper reality where griffins exist and boys and girls from this world can learn magic! In order to understand the truth of his past, Jason (with the help of a mysterious deity) must... go on an extraordinary quest, face an ancient evil, and ultimately re-connect with himself.
