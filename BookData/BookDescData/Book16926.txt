From the famous drunk comedian from Indonesia comes this humorous coming-of-age not so true story about unusual high school study group adventure. James just wants to sit tight and be a normal freshman in his new high school. But after a few days he realizes it won't be. He doesn't have any idea how to study and he met many strange characters. In order to survive his first semester, he establishes a study group filled with unusual and rich personality who surprisingly colors his everyday life.
