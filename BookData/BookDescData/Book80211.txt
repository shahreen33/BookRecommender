Trouble always finds Shaun Jacobs…So, really, the sizzling hot alpha male shouldn’t have been surprised when a move to a new town and a fresh start doesn’t go according to plan - especially when Shaun’s wolf lays claim to the beauty he unexpectedly finds on his property and passion begins to soar.But Cassie comes with her own brand of trouble in the form of an ex who can't take no for an answer.To make matters worse, there’s a hunter on Shaun’s tail. One who will stop at nothing to take down the Midnight pack once and for all.The stakes are high but Shaun won't be going down without a fight.
