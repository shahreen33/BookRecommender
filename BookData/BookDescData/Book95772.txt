Ashling Morgan's work is sure to stretch your sexual imagination. Set in a world of nubile girls and cruel men, Innocent tells the story of the young and faithful Cianna and her ungrateful mistress Sulitea. Cianna wrestles other women for the entertainment of the masses in the kingdom of Alteron. But as the fur flies in her world of gladiatorial combat, the distinction between her sport and her life becomes confusingly - and excitingly - blurred. A high gothc fantasy tale from the author of "Deep Blue."
