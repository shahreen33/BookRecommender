All she wants is a taste...but can she resist wanting more?Alessandra Baroni has had her fill of arrogant American men. Like handsome-as-the-devil Dante Donato, who's determined to buy back the winery his family once owned - even if it means playing deliciously dirty! But Dante's met his match in Alessandra. Now the stakes are getting wicked...and with this much heat, even the coldest of hearts is in danger of falling hard.
