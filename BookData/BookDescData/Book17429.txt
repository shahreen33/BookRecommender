Dylan James is used to boarding schools. He's been thrown out of so many in the past two years, he's lost count. So when an elite academy in Oregon offers him a place, he doesn't think he'll be there more than a week.But Eight Mile Island isn't like anywhere Dylan has been before. In the dense forests around the school, there are things that look human but aren't.Things that are hungry, and waiting...But that's the start of the mysteries, mysteries that mean Dylan may never escape.Even if he wants to...Welcome to Eight Mile Island.
