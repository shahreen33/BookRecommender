This is it.  This is the moment Jax has been waiting for since the death of his sister: the truth. The ugly, shocking truth. Nobody saw this coming, but someone has to deal with it.  As he confronts his deepest fears, Jax finally finds the courage to tell Kendra how he feels about her. About the two of them. However, his sincerity is met with nothing but silence.  Kendra hasn’t forgotten. She hasn’t forgiven. And she probably never will.  Note: this is the last book in the series and concludes Jax and Kendra’s story. Contains explicit language for a mature audience only.
