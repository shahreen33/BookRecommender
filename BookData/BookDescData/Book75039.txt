This boxed set contains the prequel to and the full length novel Accidental SEAL. In the prequel, the reader is introduced to the two main characters, Christy and Kyle and part of the supporting cast, prior to the hero and heroine physically meeting.Navy SEAL Kyle Lansdowne is searching for his missing Team buddy. By accident, he has a hot encounter with newbie Realtor Christy Nelson, who is holding her first open house, at the wrong house.
