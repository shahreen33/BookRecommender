
   Navesink Bank's biggest b!tch finally gets what is coming to her.
They knew she was going to strike back for taking down her empire, for imprisoning her for years.All those years stuck in a cell gave her a lot of time to think, to plot, to plan her revenge.But they couldn't have anticipated this.* This can not be read as a standalone.** This is also not a romance. This is a dramatic suspense story told from many POVs including old favorites and some new faces, all dealing with the events before, during, and after V's eventual demise.
