This wasn't the type of Christmas present he'd been expecting. Trevor Jarrod--a daddy? Yet the enticing woman seated in his office claimed the Aspen businessman was the father of her baby nephew. Quick as he could say paternity test, Trevor discovered she was telling the truth. But just what did this revelation mean? The confirmed bachelor knew nothing about being a parent. And Haylie Smith wasn't about to hand off the precious infant to a complete stranger.If Trevor really wanted to claim his unexpected heir, he had an important choice--head to court...or head to the altar!
