Do you know how to handle a bully? At Billy’s 10th birthday party, his grandfather surprises him with an incredible gift. Bursting with excitement, Billy and his best friend, Ant, can’t wait to have some fun – until neighborhood bully Eddy turns up with his own ideas. Will Billy get his gift back? Will Ant keep his promise not to tell anyone what happened? And what does magic have to do with overcoming a bully, anyway? With good-natured mischief and strong relationships between characters, Billy and the Bully addresses the importance of communication between children and adults. The story’s antics will keep young readers in suspense and entertained while helping them learn important lessons about dealing with people who aren’t so nice.
