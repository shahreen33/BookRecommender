"It is in our darkest moments that we must focus to see the light." Aristotle  The Sculptor starts the countdown. He's not leaving without the one reason he's in New Orleans in the first place.  Harper's determined to fight for Evan regardless of the consequences. The ghost of her past threatens to strip away any hopes of a future. She can't move forward without looking back and taking a stroll down memory lane.  Evan Hawthorne is the most influential man in the South but he's powerless fighting against forces that have just as much to lose as he does.  This is the third book in the Carnal Exhibitions. ***Due to the dark and explicit nature of this work of fiction it is intended for a mature, 18+ audience only. You've been warned!***
