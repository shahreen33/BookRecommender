Featuring some established and new literary voices comes the first issue of Thriller Magazine!  From superhero adventures to western tales, from bloody murders and detective stories to the strange and bizarre, this issue possesses everything thriller readers love about the genre! The authors featured in this issue include Andrew Bourelle, Amy Grech, Kolby Diaz, Ammar Habib, Paul Michaels, Gage Garza, &amp; Kaitlyn Johnson.
