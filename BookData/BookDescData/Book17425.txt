Lies. Manipulation. Deceit.At the inception of the zombie apocalypse, a mother will do whatever it takes to find her family, even if it means leading the people who saved her life to their death. They have no military training, no powerful weapons, and no clear path to follow. No one is there to step in and help, and this group regular people--strangers until now--will have to put everything on the line to find what might only be a myth. Death is inevitable, and the undead will stop at nothing to devour the living. This is only the beginning of the end: "Silence of Souls" is the first novel in the series.
