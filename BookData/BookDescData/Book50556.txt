A clear, concise step-by-step guide to taking that idea for a novel you've had for years and turning it into an actual book--with your name on the cover as author! Expert writer, editor and author of over 25 books, Prudy Taylor Board, joined by author Ruth Hartman Berge, will show you the way in this compilation of 101+ tips. Learn how to create interesting characters, settings and plots; Learn what pitfalls to avoid; Learn how to market your book to agents and editors; Learn how to create a marketing plan and sell your book to interested readers. It's time to turn your great idea into reality!
