Doing a favor for your best friend never works out.Finding a best friend and roommate in Erin, Bethany agrees to use her magic to help a group of Erin’s new friends. But something told her that things were off, and now she’s got to use her powers to get her friend back.Sharing a small apartment and struggling to survive was far better than being locked away with no word of Erin, and a niggling feeling that the stories she was told aren’t the truth.Discovering another realm, learning about her powers and making things right, Bethany must make amends for all she did wrong, no matter the consequences.
