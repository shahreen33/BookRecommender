Braxton’s life hits a roadblock when in the middle of his junior year of college, his parents decide to divorce. Braxton ends up moving away from his home.When Braxton’s grades start to drop his father forces him to see a local tutor named Cody. When Braxton goes over to Cody’s house for his first tutoring session, he meets Cody’s gorgeous, youthful mom, Anna. Anna and Braxton feel an instant connection, but there is a huge age gap since Anna is old enough to be his mother. This does not stop them from teasing and experimenting with each other whenever Cody is out of the house. While it’s fun to mess around, Braxton wants a commitment from Anna. Will Anna say yes and continue the hot romance with a younger man or will Braxton leave her and end up broken-hearted so that Anna can keep up appearances?
