Vegas Bites, a werewolf anthology comprised of four romance novellas, takes place in the French Quarter casino--the only black owned, werewolf casino in Las Vegas. With a backdrop of a week long Texas Hold'em tournament, the full moon approaching and the retirement of the alpha couple, battles of love, loyalty, and lust abound.Join L.A. Banks, J.M. Jeffries, Seressia Glass and Natalie Dunbar in exploring this high roller fantasy world of feuding werewolf packs, high-stakes thieves, a sexy djinn and a mystical medallion.
