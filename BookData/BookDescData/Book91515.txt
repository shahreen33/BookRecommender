“A gripping, must read for anyone who loves detective novels.”Mysterious, ritualistic murders…Ties to an ancient Native American demon…Detective Kacy Lang and her partners find themselves chasing ghosts, as every clue leads back to Kacy’s twin brother’s murder. But that isn’t possible. Everyone connected to that case was either dead or behind bars. She convinces herself it’s coincidence… until a bloody wall reveals the killer’s endgame.Happy Birthday, Kacy, it’s time to dance with the devil.
