The story of two men—Harold Abrahams and Eric Liddell—who run not to run but to prove something to the world. They will sacrifice anything to achieve their goals, except their honor.Two men, Abrahams and Liddell, one a Jew who runs to triumph over bigotry, the other a Scots missionary who runs to glorify God.Two men eager to face their finest hour in the 1924 Olympics; two men who race toward greatness.
