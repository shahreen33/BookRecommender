Secrets. Family. Poison.This prequel novella is set before the events in Cloaked in Shadow (The Dragori Series Book 1), following Prince Hadrian and the moments leading up to his birthday feast. Readers discover relationships between Hadrian and a handful of characters we meet in Cloaked in Shadow. Read for insight into our prince's mind, and see his reaction to that fateful evening that changes the course of so many lives. *** The prequel novella to The Dragori series by Amazon bestselling author Ben Alderson. ***
