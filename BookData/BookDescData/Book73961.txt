It's war! Bookburner and his armies are laying siege to the Golden Boughs Retirement Home, and the only hope rests squarely on the round shoulders of...you know who. Jack loves his new uniform, and he loves giving orders. He's not so keen on being shot at, though, and the total annihilation of the Fables is a big no-no.
