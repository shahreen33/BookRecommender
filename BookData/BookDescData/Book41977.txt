From Brian Barr comes Cherubim of the Ultima: A Serialized Novel!  In this first chapter, Enter Genesis, Ran seeks revenge against the Genesis Circle and their evil serpentine master, Naga. But will he achieve his vengeance before they have a chance to eliminate him?  Cherubim of the Ultima is the first novel Brian Barr completed as a teenager. He only shared it with a few close friends and family members. Now, he has taken the task to rewrite this work and release it to the world.
