Yes! Old Scrooge is DEAD, to begin with...In the sequel to Charles Dickens beloved classic, A Christmas Carol, Ebenezer Scrooge dies suddenly just seven days before Christmas. Tiny Tim, now a young man who lost his sweetheart love, Becky, must battle the demons of anger and lost faith with the recent death of his best friend and benefactor. Read as Scrooge's ghost returns to haunt Tim in a wonderful way to reveal what true faith is...and the real meaning of Christmas!
