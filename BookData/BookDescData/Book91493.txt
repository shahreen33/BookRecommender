A student, who hasn't been seen in a while is found dead in her room. Her throat has been cut but it soon becomes clear that is not the cause of death. The post mortem reveals she has been drained of blood. The killer has left behind no evidence and there are no clues. Is there a vampire on the loose in York? When another body is found detective Smith and his team struggle to find a link or motive. Will this be the case that finally defeats the brilliant DS Smith? How much more heartache can Jason Smith bear? Severed will take him to hell and back.
