Several months after he established himself as a magician and discovered the truth about his kidnap when he was a baby, Jason Griffin begins to test his powers in a race... His new baby brother is also born, yet there is another dark secret that is revealed: the Griffin family have a curse and the new arrival is its next unfortunate victim, and thus Jason and his twin sister Serena must act fast or else their loved one will suffer a cruel fate that is sure to affect them all. Alongside this tragic turn of events, a new adversary enters the secret haven he, Serena and their cousin Elias once called their second home... what can he do to save everybody he cares about? Can he solve the mystery of this curse?
