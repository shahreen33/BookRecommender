After surviving the most intense day of his life, young navigator Abel is harboring doubts about having started a romantic relationship with his quick-tempered squadmate, Cain. Stories of the fighter's volatile- and violent - past abound on the station, and it's proving difficult to separate truth from rumor. When an alien menace threatens all he holds dear, Abel must keep a clear head - but how can he concentrate when his thoughts keep returning to his mysterious new partner? In the second original BL comic to be offered by Akadot, check out the second issue of Starfighter.
