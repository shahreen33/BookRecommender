A streetwise getaway driver…A drug raid that ends in bloodshed…A violent criminal hell-bent on revenge…A secret order of occultists…And something summoned from the darkest depths of nightmare.Who will survive this long, dark night, and how will it change them? And what kind of horror will be born from the chaos left behind?If the old adage is true and we reap what we sow, then only evil can be unleashed by Reaping the Dark.
