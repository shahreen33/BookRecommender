This paper addresses the impact of democratizing the Middle East. it will assess the current strategic and political conditions in the Middle East and will highlight the challenge, the risks and the advantages provided by a democratic form of government. Areas addressed will include: differing perspectives between the Middle Eastern and Western cultures; impacts of poverty, lack education, and religion; lack of strategic vision; psychological nature of the people and the government; and, inherent risks of new democracies. The paper twill conclude by addressing the future of democracy in the Middle East.
