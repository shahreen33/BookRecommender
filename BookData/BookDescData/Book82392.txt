If you want to read gripping stories that don’t rely on sex, swearing, and graphic violence—you’ve come to the right place!  DEEP MAGIC is an electronic magazine that publishes clean short fiction in the fantasy and science fiction genres (epic, paranormal, steampunk, etc). Our issues are also filled with author interviews, art features, book reviews and tips for writers.  This month, we have five amazing short stories, including Splinter by Amazon bestselling author, Rysa Walker! We also have an interview with James Dashner, of the Maze Runner series. And we're also re-printing a writing craft article from Brandon Sanderson in honor of his bestselling new novel. And we also bring you a sneak preview of founder Jeff Wheeler's new Kingfountain adventure, The Poisoner's Enemy.  Other stories you'll enjoy: – Lullaby for the Trees by Sarina Dorie – Pawprints in the Aeolian Dust by Deep Magic alum Eleanor Wood – Destiny of the Chosen by Alexandra Balasa – The Coward's Quest by Sam Muller – Splinter by Rysa Walker
