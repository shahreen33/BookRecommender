Book 3 in the Vadelah Chronicles series.Science Fiction from a Christian perspective.David and Gyra must return to face a nightmare-Earth. They tremble at the pronouncement, but Rammar the Prophet has spoken: only David can sever Earth’s secret link with the dangerous mel-naharam, and Gyra must help him.Who are the humans involved on Earth? How will David stop the contact? Will he come out alive? David doesn’t know. But Rammar has warned that David will suffer…greatly.
