Love is an emotion woven by the strands of trust, togetherness and sacrifice. ‘For the Smile of Your Eyes’ celebrates this beautiful feeling.Riley is an ambitious young man, who has only known taking from people. Circumstances force him to take shelter in a deserted railway yard, with April, a mysterious young girl hiding away from the world and who has a disgraceful secret.The girl changes the life of this selfish go-getter completely, as he realizes what true love is all about. Braving many odds, the couple goes on to make unbelievable sacrifice to fulfill each other’s dreams.A heart-touching tale of tears and smiles, come, fall in love with “For the Smile of Your Eyes.’
