“Who could do this to a child?” Peter Paul asks.  Gabriel McQueen wants to find out. Twenty years ago he was accused of and institutionalized for the infamous “Mr. Snip” murders.  Now the killer is killing again and Gabe wants to stop the murders and clear his name. The only question is IS he actually guilty?  As the killings continue Gabe must search his forgotten past for the answers he desires as he is helped by medium “Mad Meg” and a cast of reluctant heroes including the towns Sheriff and a tired FBI “G man”.  With the depth of King and the pace of Herbert, Lucas T Harmond brings you this taught serial killer thriller set in a small dusty Texas town where no one and nothing is what it seems and the heavy presence of death lurks on every fetid corner.
