Can a chance encounter that leads to a one night stand mean something more?  Tony D’Amato believed it did, but it seemed his lover thought otherwise because she pulled a ‘wham bam thank you man’ on him. Crushed, he eagerly jumped on his bosses’ offer to work in another state.  Having been the product of a one night stand Denise Mills wasn’t willing to let the feelings consuming her take over her common sense. Learning they had a connection that could end her short career, she ran. It was a decision she regretted instantly.  Can life find a way for them to meet again? Will they find their HEA?
