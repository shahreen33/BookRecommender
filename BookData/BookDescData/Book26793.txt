Bret Maverick doesn't call poker gambling. In gambling you can lose, and losing is one thing Maverick doesn't plan to do as he roams the frontier looking for action. But when he tangles with a larcenous beauty named Annabelle Bransford, a card-cutting cutthroat named Angel, a menacing Marshal named Coop, and a gambling boat owner called the Commodore, he's in a no-limit game of everything wild. 6 cassettes.
