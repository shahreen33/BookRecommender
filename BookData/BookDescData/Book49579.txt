For as long as anyone outside the sleepy town of Arklow could remember, the Moores were a terrifying, elderly couple surrounded by haunting stories. Everyone knew to stay away from their house, and with good reason. However when Alan Cullen, a young boy goes missing, his parents decide to take matters into their own hands, which unleashes an evil much more sinister than the Moore's reputation.With darkness quickly descending, the community will learn the true meaning of evil.
