From the author's website: Private detective Charlie Parker returns in the long novella, 'The Reflecting Eye'. The photograph of an unknown girl turns up in the mail box of an abandoned house once occupied by an infamous killer, forcing Parker to confront the possibility that the house of John Grady is not as empty as it appears, and that something has been waiting in the darkness for its chance to kill again...This limited edition paperback was exclusive to Waterstone's and given away free with copies of "The Unquiet".
