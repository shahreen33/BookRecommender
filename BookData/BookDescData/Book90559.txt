A beautifully written and ingeniously plotted standalone from Herron . . . this dark thriller is rife with the deadpan wit and trenchant observation that Herron's readers relish (Publishers Weekly)From CWA Gold and Steel Dagger winner Mick Herron comes a shocking, twisted novel of psychological suspense about one woman's attempt to be better than ordinaryMick Herron is an award-winning novelist and author of the Jackson Lamb series. His work has won the CWA Gold Dagger for Best Crime Novel, the CWA Steel Dagger for Best Thriller and the Ellery Queen Readers Award, and has been nominated for the Macavity, Barry, Shamus and Theakstons Novel of the Year Awards.Mick Herron was born in Newcastle upon Tyne, and now lives in Oxford.
