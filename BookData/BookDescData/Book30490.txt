Witlock and his team, the Trouble Shooters, barely have time to decompress after a rescue when Witlock's memory device is stolen. Now it's only a matter of time for everyone's favorite robot astronaut before his memory fades altogether--or will the team discover a darker purpose for Witlock's memories?Published in 2000, the 4 issue mini-series is the love child of Ryder Windham and Kilian Plunkett that is well worth the praise given. Robot and science fiction fans line up for one of the better lesser known DC characters.
