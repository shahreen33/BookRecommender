History wasn’t modest when it described the settling of the western frontier as wild. So, of course Eve lands in the middle of an unforgiving land, rife with conflict, and fueled by desperation. A treaty has been signed between the Native American’s and the settlers who keep pouring into the west, so while things seem settled, a tension lays over the land, as thick as the blood being spilt over it. But there are more than just gun fights and saloons in this place, there is an ancient source of magic that predicts a very disturbing image of Eve’s future. And she will have to fight to make sure what was predicted never comes true and that she doesn’t become the thing she hates the most…
