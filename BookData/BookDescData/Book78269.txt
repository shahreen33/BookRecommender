ライターの朝倉は偶然出会った高校生・三原の友人への恋心に、過去の苦い恋を重ねあわせるが、実は彼の担任がその想い人・水守で…。Asakura, a writer, coincidentally came across high-schooler Mihara who has feelings for his male friend. He sees himself in Mihara because of his bitter past love. By a twist of fate, it turns out that Mihara’s homeroom teacher, Mizumori, is the very person he loved…
