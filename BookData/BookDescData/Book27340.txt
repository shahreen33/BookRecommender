The incredible eleven-book Shadow Side Series comes to a close in grand style, featuring a collection of rare and never-before-published material. Included in this volume are the novellas OLD ORDER, THE CLEARING OF TRAVIS COBLE, A SOUTHERN EVENING, THROWING MONSTERS, and WITCHING HOUR THEATRE.TALES FROM THE SHADOW SIDE showcases the rarest Jonathan Janz stories yet and a fitting end to the Thunderstorm Books Shadow Side Series.
