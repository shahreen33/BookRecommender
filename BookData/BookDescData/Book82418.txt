Surviving the crew of the Star's Vengeance, Nova and her friends are safe--for now.On a new quest to reunite a family torn, they will face their fiercest enemy yet, The Band of Shadows. Armed with mysterious weapons and a deep hatred of the Djinn, they will stop at nothing until Nova faces fate on their terms.But not everything is as it seems with the Band of Shadows. Perhaps, the fate they want Nova to face is exactly where she is meant to be.
