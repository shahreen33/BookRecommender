Kendra is hurting. Like hell.  After her unfortunate encounter with Jax’s mother, Kendra knows without a doubt that she’ll never fit in. She’ll never be accepted in his world. Despite her drive and ambition, she’s a mere nanny; the help. Therefore, she decides to take control of her destiny, with or without Jax, and flies to Europe where fashion is everything.  Meanwhile, Jax struggles to express his true feelings. Will he follow her and claim her as his? Or let her slip out of his life, for good?  Note: this is the fourth book out of five in the series. Contains explicit language for a mature audience only.
