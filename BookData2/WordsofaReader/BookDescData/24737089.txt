EVERY PERFUME HAS A TALE TO TELL. The incredible stories of 100 perfumes from a whole century of scents. With brilliantly exuberant style, perfume expert Lizzie Ostrom dives into a world of fragrance, introducing us to signature scents; now-lost masterpieces; and potent perfumes from across the decades. She reveals the wild and wonderful launch campaigns; the visionaries behind these mysterious concoctions; and the loyal fans of these little bottles of delight, who sprayed them in abundance.
