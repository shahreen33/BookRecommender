"Ah, life- the thing that happens to us while we're off somewhere else blowing on dandelions &amp; wishing ourselves into the pages of our favorite fairy tales."A poetry collection divided into four different parts: the princess, the damsel, the queen, &amp; you. the princess, the damsel, &amp; the queen piece together the life of the author in three stages, while you serves as a note to the reader &amp; all of humankind. Explores life &amp; all of its love, loss, grief, healing, empowerment, &amp; inspirations.
