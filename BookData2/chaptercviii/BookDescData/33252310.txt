The adventures of the Justice League's high-tech team member continue here in CYBORG VOL. 2, as a part of DC Universe Rebirth! With a fearsome high-tech terror cell threatening to invade the country, Cyborg participates in the creation of an experimental cybernetic female counterpart--code name She-Borg--whose abilities might exceed his own! But when the process ravages her memories, Cyborg must retrieve crucial national security secrets from her mind before it's too late. What's on the line? Control of the nation's most powerful nuclear weapons that could start a new world war! From John Semper Jr.--one of the minds behind the hit '90s TV show Spider-Man: The Animated Series--comes Cyborg's next adventure in his very own solo series! Collects CYBORG #7-12.
