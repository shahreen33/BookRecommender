This innovative book from acclaimed educator Paula Bruice isorganized in a way that discourages rote memorization. The author'swriting has been praised for anticipating readers' questions, andappeals to their need to learn visually and by solving problems.Emphasizing that learners should reason their way to solutions ratherthan memorize facts, Bruice encourages them to think about whatthey have learned previously and apply that knowledge in a newsetting.
