Spring break. Aruba. Swimming, sunshine, and drinks. Lots of drinks.It’s supposed to be the best time of Anna’s life. Perfect.But then Anna’s best friend is found brutally murdered.And as the local police begin to investigate the gruesome crime, suspicion and evidence unfathomably point to one person—Anna.Now trapped in a country not her own, Anna must fight for her freedom and prove her innocence. But as she awaits the judge’s decision, it becomes clear to Anna that everyone around her thinks she is not only guilty, but dangerous.Very dangerous.And when the truth finally comes out, it’s more shocking than anyone could have ever imagined…
